Ready for some summer competition? Summer Games 2004 takes you thru 
the competition of classic Olympic events in the disciplines of track and field, swimming and shooting.


FEATURES:

- Over 10 disciplines within the ranges running, swimming and shooting
- Training, Arcade and Tournament mode
- Current 3D-Engine with support of DirectX9 feature
- Realistic stadium atmosphere
- Simultaneous mode for hot duels
- Hot Seat mode for matches with several players
- Dynamic replays and camera angles in slow motion
