Aleister Crowley Who was this man who fascinates with his superhuman achievements?

Don't get fooled by what superficially people talk about one of the greatest minds of all times! Read HIS writings (and those were prolific!) instead and make your mind.
Here the serious student will find a very abangent spectrum of A.C.'s writing corpus, as well as precious jewels and rare works. You get even facsimile manuscripts!
