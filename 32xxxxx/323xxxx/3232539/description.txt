Gameinfo:

First person adventure.

The Arrangement" wrests attorney Richard Sullivan from his idyllic life and plunges him into a maddening mystery when his wife is kidnapped and his world turned upside down. As Sullivan searches for answers he gradually learns the chilling truth. Was his marriage not what it seemed? Was his very existence not as it appeared? Was it all just a terrible twist of fate? Or was his life pre-arranged?

As Richard Sullivan, players will interact with other intriguing characters in this compelling story which will lead to dozens of areas to explore, inventory items to collect, unique and challenging puzzles to solve, and, finally, to a surprise twist of an ending.
