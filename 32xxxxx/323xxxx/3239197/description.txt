With iDVDmax you have in your hands one complete and functional tool that allows you to make backups of your films in DVD, extracting them of the disc and keeping them in an ample fan from formats:  AVI, MPG, VCD, SVCD and DivX.  The program supports to system dolby surround, use of subtitles, modification of deals with audio and video, track of audio... and includes an internal reproducer where to be able to visualize the films once you have finished to the conversion and recording.
