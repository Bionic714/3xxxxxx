     Summary 

     Army Men: Sarge's War follows the exploits of Sarge, now an army 
     of one. The Tan Army has unleashed a weapon of unimaginable

     is harder edged than previous Sarge's Heroes titles, with more 
     emphasis on combat tactics, advanced AI, thrilling destruction 
     effects, and fast-paced, action-packed firefights.
