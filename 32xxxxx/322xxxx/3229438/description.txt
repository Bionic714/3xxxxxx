Total Textures v7 is a collection of Science Fiction textures that has taken 6 experienced profession artists over 1 year to hand paint!

The textures range from star maps and exterior spaceship textures to decals and damage maps. In the case of textures such as decals, there are included alpha-mapped TIFF files. 

Specular Maps are also available for many textures and of course we have made custom bump maps for every texture.

Many of the maps have been supplied 3 times in 3 different stages of degradation - clean, dirty and rusted and many of the maps follow on in a themes to enable the texturing of many objects with different textures yes continuity of style.

There is too much bonus material to mention, but alpha maps come into play, planet surfaces and hi-res star fields can also be found not to mention the wealth of content in the tutorial sections!
