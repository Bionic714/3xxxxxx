     Beach soccer originated in Brazil and is a spectacular sport played 
     on sand - which gives it a different dimension to standard football. 
     With game modes that alternate between realism and arcade mode, Pro 
     Beach Soccer gives you the perfect opportunity of becoming this new 
     sport's main star. 

     Pro Beach Soccer combines a wide variety of acrobatic feats such as 
     chips, slides, and flying volleys with a sound and light extravaganza. 
     In Pro Beach Soccer, the athletes and spectators create a unique vibe 
     packed with action, sun and music. Bandanas, sunglasses, coloured sun 
     block, fashionable hairstyles and earrings make up a beach soccer player's 
     typical outfit. 

     This game was designed in conjunction with the Beach soccer Federation 
     (BSWW). 

     Features: 

     *Spectacular gameplay and simple controls for easy mastery and dramatic 
      aerial manoeuvres 
     *Arcade modes (Survival, Competition generator) and more realistic modes 
     (Pro Beach Soccer Tour) 
     *2 distinct light settings: dusk and night  
     *Soundscapes which are tailor-made for each location and which help immerse 
      players in the game 
     *Great attention to detail: animated faces and hairstyles, ultra high 
      resolution (7000 polygons) giving extremely realistic skin textures 
     *Players with laid-back appearances 
     *Over 1000 Motion Capture animations for maximum realism
