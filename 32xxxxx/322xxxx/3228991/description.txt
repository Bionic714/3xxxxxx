Total Textures v5 is a collection of Dirt masks and maps which come in 3 different categories. These are : Individual maps that are not suitable for tiling, such as paint splats and stains. Maps which tile horizontally such as the grime under a window, and Maps which are fully tileable. Each of these categories are then split into light, medium and heavy dirt, giving you many options to obtain just the type of surface you are aiming for.

These Masks and maps have many uses,the main ones being as a mask to mix twotextures together or being placed as a layer over an existing texture to add in detail and 'dirty it up'. Other uses can be to apply the textures as shadow maps or use them in 2d texture editing.


All image maps have been obtained from original photography. Many textures are seamless (painted out by hand) and come in large range of resolutions ( approx.500x350 - 2000x1500) Here is the CD's full contents :-

Here is the CD's full contents :-


40 Individual stains/splats/holes in High res & Low resolution options 


22 Horizontally tiling Dirt Maps in High res & Low resolution options 


86 Fully Tileable Dirt Maps in High res & Low resolution options


37 Graffiti Alpha-Mapped TIFF Files 


Graffiti 'Viewer' PSD (Adobe Photoshop). A Layered version of All graffiti maps with various Brick walls to view them against, allowing you to instantly view many combinations.
