   European Wars: Cossacks is a real-time historical strategy based on
   events of XVI-XVIII centuries in Europe. It was time when nations and
   states were created and demolished, gold was turned to numerous armies,
   and unceasing wars shed seas of blood. It was time when new regulations
   were developed in armies, and commanders' mastery was polished to
   perfection. The new era began with cannonade thunder and bullets whine!

   Features
   ~~~~~~~~
   * 3D landscape 
   * Up to 8000 units in a battle 
   * Original game interface 
   * Resolution - 800x600, 1024x768, 1280x1024, 1600x1024 
   * 16 rivaling nations 
   * Real historical battles and wars 
   * Six resources: food, wood, stone, gold, coal and iron 
   * An original economic system 
   * A huge technological hierarchy of more than 300 upgrades 
   * Military formations 
   * Real world physics 
   * Huge maps up to 36x36 game screens at 1280x1024 resolution 
   * Special effects - smoke, fog, explosions, buildings' burning,
     explosion rebounds, etc. 
   * Real-time water rendering 
   * An extensive encyclopedia 
   * Up to 8 players in network game 
   * Random map generation 

   More on http://www.gsc-game.com/english/cossacks/index.shtml
