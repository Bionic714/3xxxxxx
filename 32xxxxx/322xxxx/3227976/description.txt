        Anticipation towards the release date of DANIEL VERNUNFT's 

        fifth album has been steadily mounting. Deservedly so, then 

        "GECKO" is a further master-piece by one of Germany's most 

        acclaimed and accomplished trance producers. As the dedicated 

        audience has come to expect, each title is rich in individuality 

        and creative themes. Non-monotonous arrangements, including a 

        wealth of eccentric and sublte grooves, are the essence of 

        SHIVA CHANDRA's formula for pure morning trance. Building upon 

        this, DANIEL VERNUNFT's project repeatedly redefines itself, 

        lending each of SHIVA CHANDRA's albums a unusually unique sound 

        character. Opening track "ACHT ACHTEL" elaborates the underlying 

        theme of this album, picking up the pace with rising melodies 

        and percussive flow. Continuing on a groove oriented tip "B.S.B." 

        (bit style bytes) pushes drum and rhythm concepts subtley to 

        extremity, disecting loops high and low with surgeonic precision. 

        "VIERENKRACH" is the most recent production on this album, 

        pushing the frontiers this track draws its momentum from 

        psychedlic elements carefully laid out on a atmospheric 

        background and stomping beats. Title track "GECKO" represents an 

        emotional highlight amongst the album tracks, luscious morning 

        trance merging vox themes, vocoded pads and infatuating 

        progressive synths. Entailing track"TOM CAT" develops streaming 

        flow-motion underlined by technoid sounds and trancey FX samples. 

        Just as compulsive are the crow screams and animal noises in 

        "KARASU", continuing the high vibration with trance-house melodies 

        and powerful bass lines. This name was taken from the crows living 

        in tokyo. Original grooves are to be heard in "BALI FROGS", an 

        exstatic sample twister demonstrating the artist's innovative musical 

        talent in a fascinating way. "N.Y. PAPATACCI" is a frisky escapade 

        into the jazzy realms of a virtual ensemble, saxes encoded by 

        cryptic digital patterns resurface as singing droids. Perfecting a 

        personal technique of integrating real life samples into the music, 

        Daniel Vernunft has become a true digital master at creating dynamic 

        psy-harmonic grooves. The production quality and scope of sounds & 

        samples on this album are again of the highest standard. SHIVA CHANDRA 

        has delighted audiences around the world with a genre defying 

        sensitivity for compulsive dance music. As an album "GECKO" summarises 

        the artist's evolving musical expression in eight dance tracks, that 

        will as easily be a favourite amongst the home listening audiences, as 

        it has already begone to be on selected dance floors to date.
