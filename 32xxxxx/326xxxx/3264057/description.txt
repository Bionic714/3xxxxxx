Steganos Safe is a standalone module of Steganos Security Suite is designed for users who want or need strong data encryption for their laptops and PCs. This one features an enhanced storage capacity of up to 128 GB of encrypted data, enabling users to securely archive digital photo albums, scrapbooks, and other large, confidential data files. It also features a new Deep Cleaning Shredder to completely eliminate any remnants of data from the hard drive that were previously deleted by other means. And it incorporates a multilingual dictionary of more than half a million commonly used words that shouldn't be used as passwords. Steganos Safe also contains the Portable Safe, which ensures secure data transport by enabling users to encrypt data onto a portable media device such as a CD or DVD, and then decrypt it on any other PC by merely entering a password.
