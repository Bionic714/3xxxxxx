Label Maker is a feature rich professional quality business printing software to print address labels, letterheads, flyers, postcards, business cards, envelopes, brochures and more from your data files. It comes with many design templates and supports all standard paper stocks including Avery papers. This is perfectly suited for business printing. Product highlights: create labels, letterheads, and envelopes; import data from your data files; add tilted text, shapes, texture and images; modify color, style, size and create numerous designs; use standard templates and backgrounds; and create your own design template and reuse it later. Use this application to easily meet your printing needs. You can save your design as an image file and e-mail to your printshop for professional printing.
