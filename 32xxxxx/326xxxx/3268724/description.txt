= Castlevania Rondo of Blood =

Grabbed this off of Winny after wasting too much time with the stupid mp3 method.
This should be everything you need to play Rondo of Blood with no fuss.
Just load the iso with Virtual Daemon or something. I tested it and everything should work fine.

You may need to edit Magic Engine's PCE.INI file to get it to recognize whatever CD drive you are using.  Specifically, the "drive=" line.  Like this:

drive=d:
