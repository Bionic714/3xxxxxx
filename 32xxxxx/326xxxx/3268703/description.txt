Darius and Luc tell Dante about the Divinity Cluster. Darius asks Dante to help him on their search for information about the Divinity Cluster. In return for Dante’s help, Darius offers to help him search for his son,
Travis. After Darius leaves, Luc discloses that she does not fully trust her father. She will play along with Darius and the Orchard; however, they will work on their own. As Darius’s ship leaves, they get a big surprise.
