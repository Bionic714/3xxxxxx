Leveraging the tremendous power of the Xbox™ videogame system from Microsoft -- including Xbox Live™ support for the posting of hi-scores -- Pure Pinball™ is the only true pinball game available for current generation consoles.

Featuring photorealistic 3D graphics, Pure Pinball offers more than 30 challenges and missions across 4 wild tables -- Excessive Speed, World War, Runaway Train, and HyperSpace -- and these will set your pulse racing with their frantic special modes and classy design. Each of the tables are inspired by authentic American pinball designs - like having your own pinball machine at home!

And - you're not alone! Compete on Xbox Live to become a globally-renowned pinball wizard by pitching your hi-scores against those of other players from all corners of the earth.
