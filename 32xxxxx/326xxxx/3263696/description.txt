From the acclaimed and Emmy winning film maker Jon Alpert (LIFE OF CRIME 1 & 2) is this powerful film about the lives of three crack cocaine drug addicts. The film follows Brenda, Dicki, and Boo-Boo, three crack house friends, that have grown up in and around Lowell, Mass. Brenda was an aspiring actress who now resorts to prostitution to pay for her crack addiction. Dicki was know as the “Pride of Lowell” a boxer of some 
skill who had once fought world champion Sugar Ray Leonard. Now Dicki commits robberies to support his habit. Boo-Boo, the third member of the group, has spent the last twenty years smoking crack, shoplifting, and doing what he has to do to survive. The film follows 18 months in their lives while they journey in and out of crack houses, rehab centers, and jails.
