Accidentally deleted your files and folders? Emptied your Recycle Bin and want it back? Undelete NOW! recovers your lost files, no matter how they were deleted. Using powerful bit-by-bit scanning methods, you can be sure to get your files back quickly and easily. Undelete NOW! supports every kind of disk, including FAT 12/16/32 and NTFS (including compressed and encrypted) partitions. It supports hard disks, backup disks, SmartMedia and more. Undelete NOW! uses multiple advanced scanning techniques 
not found in other undelete software. Depending on your disk type, it employs a combination of surface scanning, bit-by-bit scanning, and advanced error detection with AFR technology.
