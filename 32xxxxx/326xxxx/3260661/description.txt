Denna ska vara komplett (vad jag kan se) 
Catherine Cookson - Bill Bailey
Uppläsare: Bibbi Nordin
Språk: Svenska


Synopsis
Set in the Tyneside town of Fellburn, a novel in which Fiona Nelson advertises for a lodger to make ends 
meet, and into her life steps Bill Bailey. It is not long before she wonders how she ever managed without 
him.
