Book Description
================
Käpp i hjulet 1978, originaltitel: The honourable schoolboy

This is the second of le Carre's Smiley novels, featuring the character of George Smiley who 
was introduced in Tinker Tailor Soldier Spy. The story involves the betrayal of a Soviet 
spy and suspicions that the network has been infiltrated from the top downwards.
