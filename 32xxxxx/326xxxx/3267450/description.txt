This program is your all-in-one drink database solution.
You have the ability to search through the inital drink database of 1072 drink recipes for specific information on each drink. Search capabilites include: Name, Type, Glass, Garnish, Occasion, Ingredients and a !(not) operator. You also have the ability to add, edit or delete existing drink entries to your satisfaction with the extended option to 
print and preview any drink recipe you wish.Overall, this is your all-in-one drink database application.

An uninstaller is included.
