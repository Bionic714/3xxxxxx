Agatha Christie - Destination Okänd

Språk: Svenska
Inläsare: Leif Liljerot
Originaltitel: Destination Unknown

With the break up of her marriage and the death of her daughter, Hilary Craven flees England 
and escapes to Casablanca, with the intention of taking her own life. Fate intervenes, 
in the shape of Mr Jessup, who enlists her help in finding out why the world's top scientists 
have disappeared.
