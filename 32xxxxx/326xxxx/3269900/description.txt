AKA A Perfect Match

Despite the fact that the number of single men is rising non-stop in Korea, charming Hyo-jin (Shin Eun-kyung) who is successfully working for a marriage referral agency cannot seem to find "A Perfect Match" herself.

To Hyo-jin each referral with a guy's picture on it is a true treasure. However, whenever she dates one of the referrals, her prayers for a marriage partner remain unanswered; that is until she receives the picture of handsome computer designer Hyun-su (Jung Joon-ho)...
