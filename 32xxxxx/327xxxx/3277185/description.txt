<h2>Jacques Brel - Ces Gens-la</h2>
<p>
<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jacques_Brel">Jacques Brel</a> (April 8, 1929 - October 9, 1978) was a Belgian French-speaker author-composer with such strong power of expression in his lyrics that many consider him a poet as well. His themes are extremely diverse, exploring love, society and spiritual concerns. His work is not limited to one style. He was as proficient in funny compositions as in heart-breaking ones
</p>
