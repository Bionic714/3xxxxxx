Rik Emmett was a former vocalist and lead guitar player from the canadian hardrock band called Triumph from 1975 to 1988.

In 1988, Rik started his solo career and Ten Invitations was his 1997 album. It is an all-instrumental outing of original classical guitar compositions that range from flamenco to folk to new age to soft jazz.

In this work Rik shows all his true music talent.

It is worth to listen to!
