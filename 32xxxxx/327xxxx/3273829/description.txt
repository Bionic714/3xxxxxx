LAME @ CBR 192

Rare 1990 CD that combines the two Blackjack albums 'Blackjack' (1979) and 'World's Apart' (1980).

Blackjack featured Michael Bolotin (later Bolton) on vocals, Bruce Kulick on guitar, Sandy Gennaro on drums, and Jimmy Haslip on bass. The sound is early-80s AOR highlighting Bolton's powerful voice.

Track 1 - 10: 'Blackjack', released 1979 on Polygram Records.
