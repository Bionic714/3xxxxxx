The original and still the best, Jet Star’s signature ‘Reggae Hits’ series returns with another essential selection of the very best in bashment, roots and lovers rock.   

Highlights include the phenomenal ‘Here My Cry Oh Lord, the gospel track from Marvia Providence that has been locking off dances all summer AND Elephant Man’s own version ‘Bun Bad Mind’.  There are also number one hits from rising star Natty King and roots kings Richie Stephens, Jah Cure and Chuck Fender as well as Shola Ama’s welcome entrance into the realms of lovers rock with ‘Step It’.  And, it just wouldn’t be a complete album without Savana and the irresistible national chart hit ‘Pretty Lady’. 
Furthermore, the package comes as a double CD set with includes a bonus mixed CD.
