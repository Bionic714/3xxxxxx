Pass it on. Mycket bra skidfilm!

TRAILER!: http://www.freeride.nu/content/434/


Sebastian Garhammer/Broken Radio Films presents:
The best out of Europe, in a not so ordinary ski film. Pass It On is a film that shows the whole spectrum of freeskiing. 

På skidor: Jon Olsson, The Falquet brothers, Laurent Favre, Lars Veen, Sebastian Garhammer, Nico Zacek, Robert Olsson, Grete Eliasen, Jon Larsson, Jacob Wester, Marie Martinod, Sven Kueenle and friends. På snöskoter: Dan Lang, Frank Huss and friends.

Platser: France, Switzerland, Austria, Germany, Italy, Sweden, Norway, Alaska and Canada BC.
