Smilie Maker is a small piece of FREE software that lets the user create  custom Smilies with ease. These then can be used on most message boards/Forums, tag & shoutboxes, guestbooks & Instant Messaging software. 
By using predefined body colours, eyes & mouths you can create your own personal smilie. 
18 Different Body Colors & Patterns
87 Different types of eyes
70 Different types of Mouths
102 Different Accessories .
