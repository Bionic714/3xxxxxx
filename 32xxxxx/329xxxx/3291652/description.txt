Award-winning director Ron Mann hooks up with actor Woody Harrelson to deal you Grass, a highly spirited and innovative look into one of America's most deeply rooted cultural myths: "the evils of marijuana."
Utilizing hilarious footage from U.S. Government propaganda films, and eye-popping animation from underground artist Paul Mavrides, Grass blows the lid off the war on marijuana. 
Find out how a nice person like yourself became a dangerous criminal. ;)
