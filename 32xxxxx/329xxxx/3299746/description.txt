This is Nico's great solo debut album from 1967, recorded shortly
after she made "The Velvet Underground and Nico" with the VU.

The first two songs on this album were featured on the Royal
Tenenbaums soundtrack, and especially These days got popular once
more then.

Musicians: Jackson Browne (guitar), Lou Reed (guitar), Sterling
Morrison (guitar/bass), and John Cale (piano/bass/viola).
