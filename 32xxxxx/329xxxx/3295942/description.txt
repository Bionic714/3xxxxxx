This TITLE was first released in 1998 and reissued Oct 23 2003, Neither have been scene released. Now Chromance brings you a classic audio masterpiece. Enjoy!  
With elements of Celtic music... A mighty collaboration between  Youth (celebrated producer of The Verve, The Orb, & Dido), 
Soul Davis (guitarist with James), and Simon Posfird (Hallucinogen and Sphongle). 

Absolutly wonderful! Sounding like early classic ORB circa
U.F.ORB/1992. Tracks One thru Seven take you on a seemingly
endless journey. Also check out the nice acoustical work on
the last track.

OBS: Namnen på de två sista spåren har bytt plats med varandra, en liten miss från Chromance. Annars en riktigt skön skiva, komplett med covers.
