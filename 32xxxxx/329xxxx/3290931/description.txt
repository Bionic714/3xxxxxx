The Seth Morrison Chronicles (2004) [skidfilm] [dokumentär]

Ten years in the making, The Seth Morrison Chronicles follows the hero, the icon, the legend on his mission to become the best skier in the world. Get to know the real Seth through the tragedies and triumphs of his rise to fame in the ski world in the 90’s. Featuring both highlights and never before seen footage of Seth pulling off huge freestyle tricks in the big mountain arena. This documentary style film proves that Seth is truly a madman.
