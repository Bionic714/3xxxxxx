Ok for my twentieth post this year i offer up a classic for you to watch, The Punk Rock Movie. This was made by Don Letts and was shot on Super 8 which isn't the greatest format but who cares considering how much unique footage is in this classic document of punk rock. For those of you who haven't seen this it's a must watch and for those of you who have seen it here's another chance to take a trip down memory lane, enjoy!

TITLE: Punk Rock Movie, The
YEAR: 1978
GENRE: Documentary
DIRECTOR: Don Letts
STARRING: Siouxsie And The Banshees, Eater, Generation X, Subway Sect, Wayne (Jayne) County
PLOT SUMMARY: A collection of Super 8 film footage from live concerts. Eater bashes a pig head with a microphone.

The Punk Rock Movie-Classic Documentary 1978 SVCD 80 Minutes

These are in PAL SVCD format and should be burned to CD and it will play in most DVD players. These will fit on 2x80min blank cd-r's using nero.
