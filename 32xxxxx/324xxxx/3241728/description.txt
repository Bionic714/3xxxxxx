The Codex Chaos Space Marines contains the complete army list to create your own Chaos force. Also included are special rules for fielding any of the First Founding Traitor Legions, plus Gifts of Chaos and wargear. There are sixteen colour pages packed with advice and tips on collecting, modelling and playing with a Chaos Space Marines Army.
