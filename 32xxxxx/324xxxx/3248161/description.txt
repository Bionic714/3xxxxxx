Release Notes:

   Every girl dreams of having their very own horse! Now you can practice
   your riding skills as you look after your horse, providing it with all of 
   the necessary grooming and care that every championship horse needs. 
   Feed, clean, groom and tack your horse. Compete in several Show Jumping 
   and Cross-Country events as you work your way up the ranks as one of the 
   world's best equestrian riders!

    * Compete on 14 unique courses 

    * Ride through 6 different landscapes 

    * Customize your horse with your favorite features 

    * Varied gameplay including Competition, Adventure, Horse Care and Learning 

    * Over 14 different horses to collect and ride
