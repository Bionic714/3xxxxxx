Watcher is an intelligent webcam software. It monitors your home or office 24 hours a day, captures motion event using webcam, saves into compressed video clips with audio and triggers various alerts including ftp upload, email or phone. It also has camera image broadcasting capability, which can publish your webcam on a remote website or Watcher's embedded web server. Watcher can run in stealth mode, it can also be scheduled, or run as an NT service. RemoteView is an additional free tool that can connect to Watcher via a telephone line (modem) or internet (TCP/IP). It enables you to do remote video surveillance from anywhere in the world by real time video/audio streaming.
