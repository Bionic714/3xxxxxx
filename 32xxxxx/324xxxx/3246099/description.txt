Film Director(s): Kwak Jae-Yong
Released: 2003 [Korea]
Genre: Romance
Sub-Genre: Drama
Cast: Son Ye-Jin, Cho Seung-Woo, Cho In-Sung
Running Time: 133 mins. (approx.)

Overview: Ji-Hae and Soo-Kyoung are attending the same university, and both have a crush on Sang-Min. Soo-Kyoung then asks Ji-Hae to do her a favor: write love letters to Sang-Min. Ji-Hae pours out her own feelings for him on paper, but sadly in her friend's name. One day, she finds a secret box full of her mom's first love story, which is, coincidentally, similar to her own story...
