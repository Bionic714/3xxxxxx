Codex Witch Hunters allows you to create an Inquisitor's own army or add entries from the book to your existing Imperial army. Inside you will find rules for the Sisters of Battle, Inquisitors of the Ordo Hereticus, Henchmen, wargear and psychic powers. the codex is packed with full colour pages providing you with advice and tips on collecting, modelling and playing with a Witch Hunters army.
