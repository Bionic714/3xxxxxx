This hardback rulebook contains the complete updated rules for Warhammer 40000. In addition to the core rules the book also includes extensive history and background for the races of the 40K universe, together with scenarios, modelling guides and painting tips. The book is 288 pages long with over 100 pages of full colour artwork and photographs.
