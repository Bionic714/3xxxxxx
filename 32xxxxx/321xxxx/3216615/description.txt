The second installment from the popular series Onegai Teacher.

The main character of the story is Kamishiro Maiku, a high school student who is recently lost his parents and is living alone. At his high school, he meets Miyafuji Miina and Onodera Karen, two girls with completely different personalities, in fact, opposite to each other.

This summer onegai/please expect the romantic drama between these three persons...
