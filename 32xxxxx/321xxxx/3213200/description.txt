The much anticipated re-release of FRONT 242's cult
debut-album "Geography" in a special 2CD deluxe edition,
limited worldwide to only 4000 numbered copies! Truly one
of the essential, groundbreaking albums in the history of
electronic music.

Disc One of FRONT 242's "Geography [limited]" is include
each track of the original album completely remastered
and remixed with current technology while staying
faithful to the spirit of the original versions. Includes
the bonus track "He runs too fast for us" which did not
appear on the original LP back in the early eighties.

Disc Two is a limited edition bonus CD featuring
previously unreleased tracks, demo's, try-outs,
versions... in short: material which constitutes the
layers on which FRONT 242 was constructed.
