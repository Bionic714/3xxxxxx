Trespasser, from Spielberg's brainchild Dreamworks Interactive, provides one of the best environments for immersion around. There's no one else around, of course, save for the scads of Land of the Lost rejects that you'll have to find your way past and/or fight in order to escape from the island. 

Genre: Action
Requirements: P166, 32MB RAM, 120MB HD, 4x CD-ROM drive, 1MB video card Recommended: AMD K6-2 or PII 266, 64MB RAM, AGP2x 3D accelerator card. 
1CD ISO packed with rar.
Release date 1998.
