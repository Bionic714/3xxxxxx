AutoPlay Menu Builder v4.2 Build 770

..:: Description ::..
AutoPlay Menu Builder is one of the best tools for creating an automatic playing menu to launch your CDROM. No programming experience is necessary! Just click and drag your mouse a few times, and like magic you can build a menu which will pop up when your CDROM is inserted into the drive. From the menu you can launch applications, open documents, play movies, and do much, much more.
