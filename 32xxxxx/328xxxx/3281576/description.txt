Installation and play

Install by mounting the discs with Alcohol 120% latest version
You will then be required to reboot as per normal with SF3 games
Load up Starfuck 0.83 (included)
Select Starforce 3.4.63.2 blacklist patch
Click Preferences (bottom right) and change language to you language
Change Method of Channels patch to SuperAggressive and save prefs
Click Disable CDROMS
Mount CD1 using Alcohol 120% latest ver
Play! 
No mini image required !
