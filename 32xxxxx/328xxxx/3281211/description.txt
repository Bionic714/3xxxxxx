                his destiny, in the shape of a little man made out of wood. In
                the south of Sweden a zealous father tries to encourage his son,

                influences. Sliced in half for a classic intermission, Four
                Shades of Brown is epic in design and the almost Hopper-like
                visuals. Swooping down from an opening bird's eye view of the
                suburban sprawl, its opening ironic gesture, Four Shades of Brown
                delves deeper in the quirky but often dark view of urban and
                suburban lives, dysfunctional relationships and bizarre families.
