From the sick minds that brought you "Night of the Living Shred" and "Full Metal Edges," Join them in the fight against adversity in their new epic, "Revenge of the Grenerds." For years the Grenerds have been picked on, ridiculed and made the butt of cruel jokes. Join Danny Kass, Kyle Clancy, Colin Langlois, Bryan Iguchi, Gabe Taylor, Shane Flood, Lane Knaack, Sketchy-D and the rest of the Grenerds as they travel the globe turning heads in their quest for Grenerd liberation the world over.
