Ypsilon 5 from Gothenburg, Sweden gives us a set of
solid trance tracks, themes ranging from night-time
stompers like Hologram Infection to true old-school
influenced morning trancers like Beta Pictoris and
massive stormers like Ground Zero and Cosmic Psychosis. 

The album features previously released Paranormal,
completely fresh material and a yet-unreleased older
Ypsilon 5 -track Ground Zero.

Looking for a journey to the world of intensive psy-
trance with acid-sounds, sci-fi speech samples and
loads of thrilling melodies?
