WinZip is an easy way to handle archived files such as zip files and is free to download. WinZip also features built-in support for CAB files and for popular Internet file formats such as TAR, gzip, UUencode, BinHex, and MIME. ARJ, LZH, and ARC. WinZip won the Shareware Industry Award 2000 for Best Overall Utility, the Win100 2000 from Winmag.com, was voted Download of the Millennium on ZDNet.
