The Codex Space Marines contains the complete space Marine army list     which allows you to choose your forces for a battle. Also included is information detailing all the weapons, vehicles, wargear and Chapter Traits used by the Imperial Space Marines. It also has a full-colour section packed with tips and advice on collecting, painting and playing with a Space Marine army.

<b>Notes:</b> This release only has pages 20-52, but it covers all the information you need to know.
