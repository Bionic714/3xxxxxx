MATYU EUROBEAT NON-STOP MEGAMIX HISTORY
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Here's "Matyu eurobeat non-stop megamix vol.1", my first amateur eurobeat mix.
I did this mix a couple of months ago to listen to some of my favorite eurobeat
songs in my car. Since that time, my tastes for eurobeat have changed and my
mixing skills have improved a bit. Right now, I'm working on VOL.2 with some of
the greatest new and old eurobeat songs.

Volume 2 is coming soon!!!

Enjoy!

Matyu
