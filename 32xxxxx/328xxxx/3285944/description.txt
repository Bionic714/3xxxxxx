MATYU EUROBEAT NON-STOP MEGAMIX HISTORY
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Here's "MATYU EUROBEAT NON-STOP MEGAMIX VOL.2", my second eurobeat mix. The mood
of this volume is completly different from the first one. In general, the songs
are less jumpy and I used some trancy songs. But I also used some very fast
paced songs. Get ready for some intense eurobeat! I hope you will like it.

My life got really busy recently, so I may not have time to do a Vol.3 soon.
I hope I'll have some time to do Volume 3 this summer.

Enjoy!

Matyu
