Haul guns, stolen vehicles and other contraband through New Orleans and the surrounding Louisiana countryside in this action/truck driving game. 

A botched job could land you in jail or, even worse, wearing a pair of concrete shoes at the bottom of the Mississippi River

System requirements:      Windows 98/ME/XP
                                         800MHz CPU, 256MB RAM,
                                         32MB 3D video card, DirectX 9.
