    MAXON Interiors 3D for CINEMA 4D                                            
    Interior 3D is the most extensive collection of 3D furniture available

    - designers such as Eames, Mies van der Rohe and Citterio as well as

    far more than a simple collection of CAD symbols.

    of architecture in general, and interior designers as well as trade show    
    and shop designers will benefit from the unrivalled precision.
