Developed by Mirage, the game is an action packed follow up to the 
phenomenally successful Mortyr. Set during World War II and follows 
British Intelligence officer Sven Mortyr, who is sent to his Nordic 
homeland to investigate rumours of an enemy super-weapon. 

Sven is horrified to find his own father has been forced to work 
on the unit, and he must thus use all his covert skills to infiltrate 
the Nazi stronghold and prevent the completion of the weapon and 
rescue his father.
