A Classical Western from 1939 - the birth of John Wayne as a Western hero.
Director is John Ford.
Two Oscars were won by Stagecoach. Thomas Mitchell, as the drunken doctor, won "best supporting actor", Richard Hageman and his associates won one for "best musical score".

The DVD is originally a Region 1 DVD and in NTSC format and has english, french and spanish subtitles.
The DVD is ripped with DVD Decryper. It is testburned with Nero 6 on fortis  DVD-R and played on Panasonic SA-HT70. No shrinking has been made.
All special features are included from the original DVD.
