Hiroki Rikido has a weak constitution. All he does is go to school and come home with his childhood friend, Ayano. One day, Maria, who is a "Private Nurse," suddenly comes into his life. 

Maria tells Hiroki that his mother has requested that she take care of him. She'll live in his apartment for a month.

Hiroki tries to make Maria leave because he doesn't want to lose his carefree life. However, no matter how hard he tries, she just won't leave. 

Hiroki decides to give her a one-week trial period. If his condition improves within a week, he'll let her stay with him for the whole month.

Maria happily accepts his offer.

Ayano silently watches Hiroki with mixed feelings.

This is the beginning of a new life style.
