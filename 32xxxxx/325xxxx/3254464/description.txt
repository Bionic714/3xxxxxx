The  Moonlight  MPEG-2  Encoder Std is the Moonlight components package for 
 media  data  encoding,  multiplexing and dumping. It enables programmers to 
 develop   digital   video  applications  within  the  Microsoft  DirectShow 
 technology. 
           
 Moonlight MPEG-2 Encoder Package components Filters 
           
     * Moonlight MPEG2 Video Encoder Standard 
     * Moonlight MPEG Audio Encoder 
     * Moonlight M71 multiplexer 
     * Moonlight DumpPos 
     * Moonlight Color Space Converter 
     * Moonlight Image Preparator 
     * and other components... 
           
           
 Specifications 
           
 Supported Stream Formats 
           
     * MPEG-2 (ISO/IEC 13818-2) and MPEG-1 (ISO/IEC 11172-2) 
     * MPEG-1/MPEG-2 LSF Audio Layer II / III
