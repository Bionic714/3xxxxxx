Extra material 


Intro
Includes details of the band's touring in Australia, brief snippets from rehearsals for the Australian TV show "Hey Hey It's Saturday", and an interview with Ian Paice, who talks about mud and music during the Sunbury Festival in 1974. 
The evolution of the band 
Ian Gillan talks about the evolution of the band. 
DP Live - Bulgaria 1998, South America 1997 
Includes brief snippets of live versions of "Woman From Tokyo" and "Black Night". Ian Gillan talks about lyrics ("You can sing almost anything as long as it sounds good"). 
Seventh heaven 
Full live version of "Seventh Heaven" recorded during the House of Blues tour in the US 1998. 
DP Commercial Australian TV An ad shown on Ozzie TV. 
MTV Australian Special 
A brief MTV special, followed by full live versions of
Into the fire 
When a blind man cries 
both recorded during the House of Blues tour in the US 1998. 
Ian Gillan's walkabout/Interview at Fraser Island 
Ian Gillan walks on the beaches of Frasier Island, swims a little and talks about writing, the background to the song "Ted The Mechanic", Depp purple's various guitarists, songs & setlists, the people in the business, money, promoters, and the no-shit version of "Any Fule Kno That". He won't talk about golf, though. Includes brief snippets from the rehearsal for the "Hey Hey It's Saturday" TV show, brief glimps of a live version of "Ted The Mechanic" and "Bludsucker", plus a full live version of
Strange kind Of woman 
which was recorded in Australia 1999. 
John Laws Interview 
Roger "I was known as the stinkin' hippie" Glover and Ian "I'm a pussy cat when I'm at home" Gillan interviewed on Ozzie TV about the early years, extended guitar solos, alcolhol ("We were very rarely drunk. We just drank a lot"), endless American tours, depressions, and Ian Gillan's comeback at the Butterfly Ball. 
Ted the mechanic 
Almost full live version recorded in Adelaide during April 1999. 
Roger Glover Interview 
Roger Glover is interviewed by Andrew Jarman for Channel 7 (Australian TV), and talks about music ("It's easy to be simple and silly") and the Ozzie soap "Prisoner". 
"Hey Hey It's Saturday" rehearsals 
Deep Purple rehearses "Smoke On The Water" for an Australian TV show. 
Triple MMM Radio Interview 
Roger Glover and Steve Morse are interviewed in an Australian Radio show. They answer questions from some listeners -- "There's some thing men do that boy's can't" (RG), "We did a deal with the devil a long time ago" (RG), "I was in the same place for thirty years" (SM), "Oral fireworks" (RG).
