-------------------------------------------------

[url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0442933/]Beowulf[/url]  -  (20 good minutes) down - (hell of a good time) up
-- directed by Robert Zemeckis - ( Matchstick Men - Cast Away - Contact - The Polar Express - etc. )
-- Ray Winstone - Anthony Hopkins - Robin Wright Penn - Angelina Jolie - John Malkovich

[url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0757361/]Margot at the Wedding[/url]  -  (excellent film) up - (big thumbs) down
-- written & directed by Noah Baumbach - ( The Squid and the Whale - etc. )
-- Nicole Kidman - Jennifer Jason Leigh - Jack Black

[url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0457419/]Mr. Magorium's Wonder Emporium[/url]  -  (surprised) up - down
-- written & directed by Zach Helm - ( writer of Stranger Than Fiction )
-- Dustin Hoffman - Natalie Portman - Jason Bateman

[url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0461770/]Enchanted[/url]  -  up - up
-- Disney animated / live action film
-- Amy Adams - Susan Sarandon - Patrick Dempsey - Rachel Covey - James Marsden - Timothy Spall

[url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0405336/]Southland Tales[/url]  -  down - (abstract crap) down
-- Dwayne (The Rock) Johnson - Sarah Michelle Gellar - Seann William Scott - Justin Timberlake - many former SNL members

[url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0484740/]Love in the Time of Cholera[/url]  -  (well intentioned disappointment) down - (guilty pleasure) up
-- Javier Bardem - Giovanna Mezzogiorno
