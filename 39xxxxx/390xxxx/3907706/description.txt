This is a compilation of my favorite Finnish metal bands, there's 4 disc, each containing 15 songs, all from different bands. The bitrate of the songs vary from 128 Kbps to 320 Kbps, some are VBR, some are not. The genres presented on this album differ from melodic black and death metal to power and heavy metal, there won't be any crappy nu-metal or emo-shit on this compilation. There's also some Finnish songs in there too, but most lyrics are in English.
The ID3 tags show the original album the song was from, so you won't have problems with Last.fm, and so that you can hunt down the album easier if you like the song and want some more from the same band.
