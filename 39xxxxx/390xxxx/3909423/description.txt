The Original 1966 Movie.

  Michael Caine plays the rakish Alfie who moves from woman to woman. Talking up to the camera 
and down to his sexual conquests, until of course, he is left alone. Shelley Winters plays a 
wealthy woman Alfie seeks to court. Nominated for a Best Picture Academy Award.

Cast includes: Michael Caine, Shelley Winters, Millicent Martin, Julia Foster, Jane Asher. 

Length: 1hr 53min.
