Documentaries about military revolution in Greece, 21 April 1967

Documentaries have greek audio streams without subtitles, so the comments below are in greek language.


Ena afierwma sto kinhma ths 21 Apriliou 1967.

Apoteleitai apo arketes mazemenes ekpompes pou exoun paixtei sthn ellhnikh thleorash.
Autes einai:
1. Ereuna
2. H mhxanh tou xronou (2 episodeia)
3. Fakeloi tou Papaxela



Egw auto pou 8a h8ela na pw einai to e3hs:
Ka8e kybernhsh exei ta 8etika ths kai ta arnhtika ths.
Sthn eptaetia eixame panisxyrh oikonomia, ragdaia anapty3h, kalh astynomeysh kai prolhpsh, oi dhmosies uphresies doulebane kai kamposa alla 8etika.
Sthn eptaetia omws eixame kai arnhtika fainomena kataxrhshs e3ousias, oxi toso apo tous kybernountes, alla kyriws apo autous pou tous sthrizan (astynomikous, dikastikous ktl)

P.x. phgaine o astufulakas sto xwriko kai tou elege: "Kala, exeis kokkino kokkora? Eisai kommounisths?" kai o astufulakas ...etrwge ton kokkora.

Ola auta stadiaka ephreasan arnhtika thn arxika 8etikh proseggish ths "Xountas" apo to Lao.
Kai opws oles oi kubernhseis den kratoun gia panta kai den exoun panta a3ia stelexh (blepe Kupros), etsi ki auth, epese.
