 Hack Into the System?
Coded Arms? takes players into a virtual reality system originally created as a combat training simulator. The system was shut down due to a major flaw, but it continued to evolve on its own, creating a world full of enemies and creatures. Armed with a variety of futuristic and conventional weapons, players will experience intense firefights while delving deeper into the core of the unchecked system.
# Stunning visuals with action packed FPS gameplay
# 30 Intense weapons including pulse rifles, rocket launchers, and grenades
# 6 Expansive game worlds with multiple gameplay levels
# Randomly generated maps provide limitless replay value
# 4 Player multiplayer modes, including Deathmatch, Last Man Standing, Keep the Mark
