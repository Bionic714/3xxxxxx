Beowulf is a 2007 animated epic film directed by Robert Zemeckis, a film adaptation of the Anglo-Saxon epic poem of the same name. It was filmed using a special technique of motion capture similar to that used in Zemeckis' The Polar Express. The film stars Ray Winstone, Anthony Hopkins, Robin Wright Penn, Dominic Keating, Alison Lohman, John Malkovich, Crispin Glover, Brendan Gleeson, and Angelina Jolie.

While some of the film remains true to the poem Beowulf, certain elements were created specifically for the screenplay. Deviations from the original work include portions of the dialogue, the portrayal of Hrothgar, the perceived hedonism in Heorot, the span of time before Beowulf fights the dragon, Grendel's motive for attacking Heorot, and the relationships between Beowulf and Wealtheow, and Beowulf and Grendel's mother.
