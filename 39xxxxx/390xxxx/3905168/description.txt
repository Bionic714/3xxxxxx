Royal Crown Revue - El Toro EP 2007

Tracks:
1: El Toro
2: Won't You Ride With Me
3: Solitary Man
4: Brazil
5: It's a Good Day
6: Rockville

From RCR Website:
Royal Crown Revue proudly announces the release of a brand new CD, entitled "El Toro." It's the band's first release since 1994's "Greetings From Hollywood." This specially priced, 6-song EP includes two brand new RCR originals ("El Toro" and "Won't You Ride With Me"), plus a slamming cover of Neil Diamond's 1966 classic "Solitary Man."
