Product Description
Melody and Max are back with the Hillsong Kids and 15 high-energy praise songs! 
Featuring one hour of live worship, this bouncy DVD includes five different 
worship sets with three songs each, onscreen lyrics, and playback with or 
without lead vocals. Also, kids can learn the actions to some of the songs with 
Max and Melody and the Hillsong Kids team. Your kids will love movin' 'n' 
groovin' with the Hillsong Kids! Perfect for both kids ministry and home use. 
Recommended for ages 12 and under.
