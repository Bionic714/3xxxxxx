Black Sand Synopsis.

When the accident happens on the mountain road, Peter and Emily are relieved that nobody got hurt. As they drive on, strange things start to happen. What has the stillborn child, buried in the late 18th century have to do with them? Or the couple in the cemetery 80 years earlier? When they relive the accident, they are faced with the terrible possibility that 1000 years of love may have come to an end.
