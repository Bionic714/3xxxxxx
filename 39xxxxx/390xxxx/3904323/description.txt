This is the full series (all 4 seasons) of the popular TV show Kenan and Kel (1996-2000). With 61 episodes, this collection provides more then 7 hours of premium entertainment for all ages.

The show centered around a character Kenan Rockmore, a high schooler who works at Rigby's mini grocery store. Kel Kimble is his orange soda-loving friend. Chris Potter is Kenan's boss at. At Kenan's home life there is his mother Sheryl, his bald father Roger, and his Kel-loving sister Kyra. By 1998 the show introduced Sharla. Sharla works with Kenan at Rigby's. Chris hired her to help Kenan out.
