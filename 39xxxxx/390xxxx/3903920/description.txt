MercyMe is an Grammy Award-nominated and Dove Award winning American contemporary Christian band, originally formed in Greenville, Texas in 1994.

All That Is Within Me is the sixth full-length studio album from MercyMe, that is set to be released on November 20, 2007 through INO Records. The first radio single released in support of the album is "God With Us".
