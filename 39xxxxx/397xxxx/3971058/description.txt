The Bhoy Who Would Be King is a fitting tribute to Henrik Larsson's remarkable career as a fully fledged Celtic hero
and a celebration of the exciting Swede's prolific goal scoring record.
Over the past years, Larsson's phenomenal career with Celtic has brought untold joy to millions of Hoops fans worldwide
and this programme charts his progression at Celtic beginning August 1997 through to the 2003/04 Season.
