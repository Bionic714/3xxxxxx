Payal is a young girl who lives in India and studies in a college. She secretly loves another Indian college student named Rajiv. Both meet and find out that they are attracted to each other. On a outdoor picnic, Rajiv gets drunk and tries to make a pass at Payal, and confesses that he does not love her but only said that because of a bet he made with other college friends. Payal is shocked and decides not to see Rajiv anymore. While she goes into a shell after this humiliation, Rajiv now takes a bet to romance another college beauty by the name of Alisha. Will Alisha be another victim?
