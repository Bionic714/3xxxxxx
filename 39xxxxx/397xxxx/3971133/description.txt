Thursday January 10, 2008  --  Commercial edited

Alex welcomes Bev Harris of Black Box Voting to discuss voting irregularities and instances of fraud in the Iowa caucus, the New Hampshire primary and other upcoming elections.

Alex also speaks with Larry Pratt, Executive Director of Gun Owners of American about the Veterans Disarmament Act  and other ongoing encroachments on Americans' right to bear arms.

Alex also welcomes global economics expert Bob Chapman of the International Forecaster to discuss the climbing cost of oil and the likelihood of a US recession.
