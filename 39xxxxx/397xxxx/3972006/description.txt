The Hunt for Red October (1990)

Director:
John McTiernan
Writers (WGA):
Tom Clancy (novel)
Larry Ferguson (screenplay)


Genre:
Action / Adventure / Thriller


Plot Outline: Based on the Best Selling novel by Tom Clancy this film tells the story of Captain Marko Ramius, the skipper of the Soviet Union's newest nuclear sub. Jack Ryan of the CIA gets involved in a tense, tangled hunt for this sub, when Ramius defects, taking the "Red October" with him. The story is an action packed techno-thriller.
