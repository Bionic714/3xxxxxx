REALISH MIX 2007

Yes, the hotly anticipated Realish Mix 2007 is finally out, a mere two or three weeks late.

For those who don't know, the Realish mixes are compilations of the year's best music, drawn mainly from indie rock and pop but with a sprinkling of hip-hop, country, post-rock, top 40, and whatever else catches my ear. Realish mixes from the last two years have been downloaded tens of thousands of times. You can check them out here:
 
http://thepiratebay.org/user/realish
