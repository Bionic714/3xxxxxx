SUGARFOOT 107 Misfire  (12/10/1957)
Mercy Preston schemes to use Sugarfoot to dispose of two men who stand between her and the possession of a rich mine. Her sister Patience attempts to help Sugarfoot.

*Great Cast*: Will Hutchins (Tom "Sugarfoot" Brewster), James Garner (Bret Maverick), Pernell Roberts (Salt River Smith), Connie Stevens (Patience Preston), Eve Brent (Mercy Preston), Gordon Richards (Judge), Phil Tead (Postmaster), Woodrow Chambliss (Boswell Newcombe), Frank Albertson (Sheriff Crabtree)
