ENGLISH:

UltraStar is a game that reminds about Singstar (Playstation). Compete with your friends and find out who can sing in the right key.

UltraStar Deluxe (UltraStar DX) is a version of UltraStar with a better interface and  an extended possibility to play in teams.

You don't need a Singstar microphone to play.



The folder contains following songs:

* Abba - Dancing Queen
* Aerosmith - I Dont Want To Miss A Thing
* Alphaville - Forever Young
* Coldplay - Speed Of Sound
* Dead Smiling Pirates - I 18
* Fools Garden - Lemon Tree
* Foreigner - I Wanna Know What Love Is
* George Michael - Careless Whisper
* Joshua Morin - On The Run
* Lordi - Hard Rock Hallelujah
* Modern Talking - Cheri Cheri Lady
* Queen - I Want To Break Free
* Rednex - Cotton Eye Joe
* Scorpions - Wind Of Change
* Steven Dunston - Northern Star
* The Corrs - The Right Time
