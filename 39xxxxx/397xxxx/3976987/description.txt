(2004) Masaaki Hatsumi has just released rare footage of several hidden ninjutsu weapons such as the shuko, the kyoketsu shoge, shuriken, kakushi, kanide, kusarifundo, sokutoki, senban shuriken, and other hidden weapons.
Contents include:

* How to use the shuko
* How to use the kyoketsu shoge
* How to use the shuriken
* Kakushi Buki (Hidden weapons)
* Sokutoki
* Senban shuriken
* Real techniques using hidden weapons
