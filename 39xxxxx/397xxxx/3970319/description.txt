In a year of thirteen months


Drama from 1978  Rainer Werner Fassbinder 


Volker Spengler    Ingrid Caven. 


In a year of thirteen months:


Volker Spengler gives an extraordinary performance as the transgendered Elvira, a 

man who has become a woman in a desperate bid to please a former lover. Relaying 

various episodes from Elviras life, Fassbinders powerful film is one of his most 

intensely personal.
