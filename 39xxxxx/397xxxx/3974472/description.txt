Lupin, the suave thief who makes a habit of stealing from the rich and keeping the spoils for himself, embarks on a new sequence of heists in this anime TV series. Joined by Jigen, a master marksman, and Goemon, a fierce samurai, Lupin jaunts around the world, lining his pockets and eluding Interpol Inspector Zenigata. But when Lupin's sometime rival Fujiko appears, he must be extra wary of her mischievous traps.
