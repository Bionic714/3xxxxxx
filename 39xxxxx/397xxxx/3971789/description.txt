17. Clown De Mes Malheurs ------------------------------------------------------------LES RITA MITSOUKO was a French pop rock group led by guitarist Fred Chichin and singer Catherine Ringer.  The duo first performed as Rita Mitsouko at Gibus Club, Paris in 1980. They went on to become one of the most successful musical acts of the 1980s in France.
