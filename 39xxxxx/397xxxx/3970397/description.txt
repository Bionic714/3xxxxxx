This is a short timelapse HD clip (1080x720) of a candle burning.

It consists of 883 single pictures put together using AviSynth and VirtualDub.
You need the XVID codec to play this clip - there is no audio included.

What was used to create this clip:
o) Canon EOS 350D
o) Tokina ATX-Pro 100 f/2.8 macro lens
o) Manfrotto 190CL tripod, Manfrotto 141RC head & Manfrotto 454 sliding plate
o) Apple MacBook Pro with Canon EOS Utilities and Adobe Photoshop Lightroom
o) VirtualDub and AviSynth as Frameserver
