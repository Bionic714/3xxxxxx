French harpsichord virtuoso Hughette Dreyfus was born in Mullhouse, Alsace.  She studied the piano under Lazare Levy and Ruggero Gerlin.  She won the first prize in the 1958 International Music Contest in Geneva. She is now a professor at Schola Cantorum de Paris and at Conservatoire National Superieur de Musique de Lyon.
