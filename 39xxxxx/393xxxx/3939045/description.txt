Manos Hadjidakis was one of the most popular Greek composers. Although coming from a small country, he won worldwide acclaim in the '60s for the music he wrote for films like "Never on Sunday" and "Stella". 
Here, as a tribute, K.B. transforms some of Hadjidakis's tunes into electronic and drum 'n' bass chill-out sounds.

It's sweet, melodic, maybe a bit ethnic. Worth the try if you're looking for something different.
