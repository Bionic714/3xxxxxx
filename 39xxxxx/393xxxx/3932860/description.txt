"The First Fight" is the third episode of Samurai Jack's first season. It was shown as a feature length episode as the third part after "The Beginning" and "The Samurai Called Jack", which was then released as "Samurai Jack: The Premiere Movie". The episode aired on August 10, 2001.


[Aku sends an army of insect drones to kill Jack and the canines. Jack sets traps and, with the help of his friends, eliminates the army.]
