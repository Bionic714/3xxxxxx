"The Samurai Called Jack" is the second episode of Samurai Jack's first season. It was shown as a feature length episode with "The Beginning" before it and "The First Fight" after it, which was then released as "Samurai Jack: The Premiere Movie". The episode aired on August 10, 2001.

[Jack finds himself in the future. He goes to the aid of talking archaeologist dogs who are forced to mine jewels by Aku.]
