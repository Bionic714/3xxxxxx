Name: Megapowers

Year: 2007

Language: Portuguese

Summary:
When aliens become to destroy the human race and consume all the energy, only three young warriors are capable to destroy these evil forces - the Mega Powers! Chosen by a mysterious peaceful guy from another galaxy, they will defend the Earth with his incredible powers! Fighting against sand mosters, steel creatures and other dangerous enemies, the Mega Powers know that the battle between good and evil has only begun!
