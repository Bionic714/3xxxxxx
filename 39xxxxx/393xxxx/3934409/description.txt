Requirements

  • Microsoft Windows 2000/XP/Vista
   • 256 MB RAM recommended
   • Intel Pentium 4 2.4 GHz or AMD Athlon 2500+ is recommended
   • Hard Disk: 1 GB (3 GB recommended) for VCD/SVCD/MiniDVD
                10 GB (15 GB recommended) for DVD
   • 1024 X 768 pixels, 16-bit color
   • DV Tape to MPEG-2 (real-time) and Right to Disc Recording: 512 MB RAM
