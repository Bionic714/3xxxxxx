Buffy Season Eight #9 - No Future for You - Part Four

The Slayer population of the world has gone from two to nearly two thousand. Almost five hundred are working with Buffy's European-based organizations in squads or "terrorist cells," according to various military forces. Enter Faith, the pioneer of Slayer-related dirty work, whose talents as a fierce fighter continue to garner the attention of her former Sunnydale compatriots.
