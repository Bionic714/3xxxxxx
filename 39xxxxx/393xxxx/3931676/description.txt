Season 5, Episode 3: A Clean Slate

Original Air Date: 21 September 1994

Josh helps Brandon run for student body president who shines at a debate. But a scandal comes up when an article written the previous year by Josh, but never published, which details Brandon's tutoring of D'Shawn whom took rigged tests surfaces which threatens to tarnish Brandon's reputation. Donna asks Clare (now attending C.U. as a sophomore) to move into the beach front apartment to help pay the rent. But Clare brings a bad snoring habit with her. Meanwhile, Valerie secretly decides to pursue Dylan after finding Brenda's mementos of him in the Walsh garage, and begins hanging out at the pool hall where Dylan also drinks and hangs out.
