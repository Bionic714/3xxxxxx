Celebrate Christmas With Mickey Donald And Friends

Celebrate the happiest time of the year with this delightful animated gift from Mickey, Donald
and all your Disney friends. From Pluto's Christmas Tree to Ferdinand the bull's fun-filled
adventure, this glorious collection of Disney tales is wonderful entertainment for everyone.
Join Donald and his nephews for a hilarious snow day, and tag along with Mickey and Minnie for
a surprising skate date. Enjoy pure Disney fun that's sure to become a Christmas tradition for
you and your family.
