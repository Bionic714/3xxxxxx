The complete collection of Suede's promo videos.

The tracklist includes 'The Drowners', 'Metal Mickey', 'Animal Nitrate', 'So Young', 'Stay Together', 'We Are The Pigs', 'The Wild Ones', 'New Generation', 'Trash', 'Beautiful Ones', 'Saturday Night', 'Lazy', 'Filmstar', 'Electricity', 'She's In Fashion', 'Can't Get Enough' and 'Everything Will Flow'.

DVD extra tracks: Moving, Daddy's Speeding, Shipbuilding, Europe Is Our Playground, Savoir Faire, Simon.

    * Home Movies
