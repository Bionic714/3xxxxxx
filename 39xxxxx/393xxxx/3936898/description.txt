"Tony Hawk's PlayStation Collection" includes:

Tony Hawk's Pro Skater 1 Beta (Unreleased)
Tony Hawk's Pro Skater 1 (Disc 1)
Tony Hawk's Pro Skater 2 (Disc 2)
Tony Hawk's Pro Skater 3 (Disc 3)
Tony Hawk's Pro Skater 4 (Disc 4)

This is originally for the PlayStation, but also works with the PlayStation 2, 

PlayStation 3, and PlayStation Portable.
