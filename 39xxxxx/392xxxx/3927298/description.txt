Director: Richard Roxburgh

Writers: Nick Drake, Raimond Gaita (memoir)

Release Date: 31 May 2007 (Australia)

Genre: Drama

Plot Outline: It tells the story of Romulus, his beautiful wife, Christina, and their struggle in the face of great adversity to bring up their son, Raimond. It is a story of impossible love that ultimately celebrates the unbreakable bond between father and son.

Cast: Eric Bana, Franka Potente, Marton Csokas, Kodi Smit-McPhee
