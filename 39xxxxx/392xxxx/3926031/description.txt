The Vishnu sahasranam is a list of 1,000 names for Vishnu, one of the main forms of God in Hinduism and the supreme personification of Brahman for Vaishnavas (followers of Vishnu). It is also one of the most sacred and commonly chanted stotras in Hinduism.
