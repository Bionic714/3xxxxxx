Adobe Photoshop CS3 actions



What is a Photoshop Action? Photoshop Actions are the recordings of commands that 

can be applied to an image or multiple images. When Photoshop plays an action, it 

applies these recorded commands to an image sequentially. You can store hundreds of 

useful actions on your computer and load them into Photoshop whenever you want to 

apply the commands applied by the action.
