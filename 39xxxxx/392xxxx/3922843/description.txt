Descripcion:
Blackbeards Revenge is an arcade action game on the high seas. Successful pirates are rewarded with money and resources which are looted from defeated enemies sunken ships. From your island base you can use these resources and treasure to build better ships, weapons and cannon balls to turn the tides in your favor for future battles. If youre lucky enough to obtain an enemy ships design plans you can build faster ships with better armor and equip them with more powerful cannons. Now youll be capable of shooting further, faster, and inflicting more damage. Blast your way to victory and riches!
