Jack Coney (Ray Liotta) awakes in the New Mexico desert with no clear memories -- just lots of bruises and flashbacks of violence. Vicky (Gloria Rueben), a reclusive artist, rescues him and nurses him back to health. This superior straight-to-video thriller follows the pair as they try to uncover the secrets of Jack's past. Unfortunately, that past involves a ruthless gangster (Armin Mueller-Stahl) whose goons are soon on the pair's trail
