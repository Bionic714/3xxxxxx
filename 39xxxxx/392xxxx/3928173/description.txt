Uncle Malaria ft. Uncle Carcus
The Complete Works (2000-2007)
Musical styles as varied as Funk, Electro, Metal, IDM, Classical, Hip Hop, Drum N Bass, Robot Rap, Acid Jazz, Soundscapes, and Rock.
Featuring remixes of Gwen Stephanie's "What are you waiting for?" and Marvin Gaye's "Sexual Healing".

Samples from Simpsons, Futurama, Clerks, 50s propaganda, lectures, Charles Manson, Fallout2, Irish folk.
Software used was Cooledit Pro 2, Reason 1.0, Rebirth, Fruity Loops and a vast assortment of DirectX Plugins.

Uncle Carcus and Uncle Malaria are Canadian artists located in Southern Alberta.
