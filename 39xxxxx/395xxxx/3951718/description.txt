A BBC Reporter shows us how he saw Iraq from just before the invasion to the British leaving Basra


Andrew North has been reporting from Iraq since 2003, in this program he examines some of the key turning points, such as the invasion, the insurgence, the descent into sectarian bloodshed, the recent 'surge' of US troops, the British withdrawal from central Basra and the rise of the militias and death squads.
