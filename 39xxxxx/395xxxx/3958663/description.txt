PUZZLEBITS FOR PERSONAL ASSEMBLY
...is the new record from Bangarang, being release for free online over the course of 2008. The album is a full length featuring all brand new songs with special cover art pieces created for each song by breakout visual artists from all over the world. A gallery showcasing the artwork with band and artist commentary, as well as video InfoBurst podcasts, music videos, secret shows, new merchandise, and other such madness will be made available through the group's website (www.bangarang.us), Facebook page, and Myspace beginning on January 9th.
