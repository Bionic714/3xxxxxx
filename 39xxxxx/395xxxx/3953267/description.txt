Deep Purple's Made in Japan

The greatest live album ever recorded by the greatest rock band in history.
?Made in Japan? is one of the most exciting live albums ever recorded. Featuring rare archive performances of the classic line up of Deep Purple live in concert, this is the ultimate review of a landmark in rock history.
Also featured are rare archive interviews with the band themselves, including the views of the man in black himself Ritchie Blackmore and solo performances of material from the album by Ian Gillan.
Features highlights from ?Highway Star?, ?Child in Time?, ?The Mule?, ?Space Truckin??, ?Black Knight?, ?When a Blind Man Cries?, ?Strange Kind of Woman?, ?Lazy?, ?Speed King?, ?Smoke on the Water? and ?Mandrake Root?.
