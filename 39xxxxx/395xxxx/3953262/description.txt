Countymojo - excess 
http://www.countymojo.com/

Country: US
Released: 2006
Genre: Illegal Audio Collage
Style: Experimental
Notes:

Released as almost a b-sides album to APART in 2006.  the voicemail project was primarily constructed from listeners calling a toll free number and leaving an opinion of a online listening party held in early 2006.

NOT your run-of-the-mill audio collage.  Often compared to Negativland.
