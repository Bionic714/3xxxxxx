Be Mine, Valentine is the first EP released by Colorado band Drop Dead, Gorgeous. "Knife Vs. Face: Round I" and "Well, I Never Knew You Were So Much Fun" were later re-recorded for their first full-length album, In Vogue. Contains six tracks, plus album artwork.
