Age of Empires III: The Asian Dynasties is the second expansion pack to the award-winning Age of Empires III. It was developed by Ensemble Studios, the developer of the existing Age of Empires series and Big Huge Games, the developer of Rise of Nations.[7] This expansion pack follows the release of the first expansion pack, Age of Empires III: The War Chiefs. This expansion adds the ability to play as three new civilizations: The Chinese, the Indians, as well as the Japanese. Players also now have the ability to play in maps representing areas of Asia, in addition to the previously added New World maps.[7]

Age of Empires III: The Asian Dynasties went to gold status on September 28, 2007.[1] The demo version of the game was released on October 4, 2007.[8] Microsoft Game Studios initially released the game onto store shelves throughout North America on October 23, 2007,[1] around the tenth anniversary of the first installment of the Age of Empires series.[9]
