Everything's Watched, Everyone's Watching is the second full-length album from Californian experimental band Heavy Heavy Low Low. Contains eleven tracks (the bonus track is attatched to "Party Girls"), plus album artwork:

1. "This Is Really Testing the Patience I've Never Had" - 0:33
2. "A, S, V, L, N" - 1:27
3. "Mall-Nutrition" - 1:55
4. "Are You Okay, Kiddo?" - 2:17
5. "I Forgot 2 and a Half Days" - 2:05
6. "Texas Chainsaw Mascer-uh" - 1:15
7. "Kids, Kids, Kids" - 1:43
8. "Buddy, You're Making No Sense" - 2:27
9. "There's a Bat" - 1:13
10. "Eating the Porridge and Killing the Bears" - 1:38
11. "Party Girls" (plus bonus track) - 5:37
