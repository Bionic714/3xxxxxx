This is a fan made movie using clips from various season 6 episodes. The story revolves around Lex's secret lab, "33.1", which conducts experiments on various people with special abilities. It's up to Clark, Chloe, Green Arrow, Aquaman, Cyborg, and Impulse to save them. 

We also see the softer side of Lex, as his love for Lana, and their relationship blooms before our eyes. Nut all isn't rosy, as Lana starts to go down a dark path with Lex, that she may never return from.

Lois, the vigilant truth seeker, falls in love with Oliver Queen (aka Green Arrow), all the while, trying to unmask the Green Arrow, who she thinks is a menace to metropolis.

This file is iTunes and iPod compatable.
