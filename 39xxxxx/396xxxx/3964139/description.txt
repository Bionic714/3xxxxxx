Tripping The Rift - Season 3, Episode 11

"Extreme Take-Over"

Original air date:  December 27th/2007 (Teletoon, Canada) 

Bob's quote:  "Eat your heart out, Millennium Falcon." 

Summary:  Chode enters Bob (and the crew) in a space race called "The fifty million Kronig Flaming Collosus".  In an attempt to cheat himself into first place in the race, he gets Bob modified in a chop-shop.  But he soon finds out that he got more modifications done to Bob than he had wanted.
