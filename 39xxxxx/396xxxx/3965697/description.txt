Best of The Corrs is the first of The Corrs' compilation albums featuring previously released songs as well as new tracks. The new single that was only released and featured in this complication is 'Would You Be Happier'. After six months from the album's release, it turned multi-platinum worldwide.

Released: November 5, 2001
Recorded: 1994-2001
Genre: Celtic folk-rock
Producer: David Foster, Mitchell Froom, Oliver Leiber, Robert John Lange
