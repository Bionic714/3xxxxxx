Season 1, Episode 1: Pilot
Original Air Date: 17 September 1972

Original Air Date: 1 October 1972

Original Air Date: 8 October 1972
When Hawkeye is appointed Chief Surgeon over Frank Burns, Burns and Houlihan go over Col. Blake\'s head to a general to protest the decision.

Original Air Date: 15 October 1972
Hawkeye is outraged when a visiting sergeant brings his \'moose\' to camp - a young Korean woman he has bought to keep as his personal servant.

Original Air Date: 22 October 1972
A major selects the 4077th as the site for a documentary featuring \'false heroics\' about Mash\'s in Korea. After destroying the film Hawkeye and Trapper make their own movie as a replacement.

Season 1, Episode 7: Bananas, Crackers and Nuts
