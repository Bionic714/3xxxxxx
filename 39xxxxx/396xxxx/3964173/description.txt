Floored by Love is a light-hearted and rather simple bagatelle. It is a lesbian love comedy, without too much ingenuity, and a rather short one (45 mins approx). Quite nice, though, so if you are also into these kinds of things, you might like it too, perhaps. Beware of one (1) rather lousy actor.
