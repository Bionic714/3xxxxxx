Bitter tears of Petra von Kant 



Drama from 1972  Rainer Werner Fassbinder 


Margit Carstensen och Hanna Schygulla. 


The Film: 

Petra von Kant is a successful fashion designer. Arrogant, bitter and twisted after her divorce, she lives with her assistant Marlene, who silently suffers Petra?s domination.When Karin enters her life she instantly falls in love with her and invites her to move in, promising to make her a famous model. But when Karin leaves her, Petra becomes irrationally jealous and hysterical.
