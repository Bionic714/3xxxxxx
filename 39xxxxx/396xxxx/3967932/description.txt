Retrospective of legendary rock guitarist Jimi Hendrix with
electrifying live performances and rare interviews. Tracklisting
includes: 'Purple Haze', 'Wild Thing' - Blackpool, November 25, 1967,
'Hear My Train A Comin'' - London, December 19, 1967, 'The Wind Cries
Mary', 'Purple Haze' - Stockholm, May 24, 1967, 'Red House',
'Sunshine of Your Love' - Stockholm, January 9, 1969, 'Hey Joe'
(original promotional film) - London, January 1967, 'Wild Thing' -
Paris, October 9, 1967 and 'Dolly Dagger' - music video from 1997.
