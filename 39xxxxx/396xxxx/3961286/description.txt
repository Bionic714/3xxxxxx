Enemy Ace chronicles the adventures of German World War 2 Flying Ace, Hans Van Hammer. As a World War I pilot flying for the Germans, his stories told the German side of the war, in which Hans von Hammer was a man of honor and chivalry, a flying knight in his Fokker Dr.I, but he was haunted by his duties and the constant death surrounding them.
