America - The Best Of Musikladen Live 1975

MusikLaden, also known as Beat Club, was German television's leading rock music series in the 1960s and '70s, featuring many top acts from both Europe and the U.S. This video features a full 45-minute performance by the folk-rock group America taped for MusikLaden in 1975, featuring many of their biggest hits. Selections include "Horse With No Name," "Ventura Highway," "I Need You," "Lonely People," and "Don't Cross the River."
