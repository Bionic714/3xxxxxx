Pack 14 of the Silver Age DC Comics Chronology.

Dates range from Oct 1989 - Nov 1989.

Three new series are started in this pack:  Batman:Legends of the Dark Knight, Green Lantern:Emerald Dawn and Deadman:Love After Death.  There are also several one-shot specials:  Epicurus the Sage, Gotham By Gaslight, Ring of the Nibelung, Wasteland GN and Wild Dog Special.
