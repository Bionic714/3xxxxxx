Panzer IV: Heavy Tank (2005) 


The Panzer IV was one of Germany's most feared weapons of World War II. Initially designed as a support tank, its massive armor and devastating cannon eventually made it the tank of choice for meeting Allied armed forces. Here, viewers will get to know the ins and outs of the Panzer IV, the last of which wasn't destroyed until 1967, when a Syrian Panzer IV was defeated at the Golan Heights by the Israeli army.


Length: 60 minutes
