Intermediate Bonsai - A Course Syllabus
By Thomas L. Zane

The principal use of this Syllabus, is as a workbook to accompany hands on learning
sessions for the intermediate student of bonsai. Instruction is offered on the basic art and aesthetics of bonsai, the design and maintenance of various styles of bonsai, the practice of several special techniques used in creating a bonsai, and finally on the effective display of finished trees.
