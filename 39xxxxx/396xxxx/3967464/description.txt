
Querelle 



Drama from 1982 Rainer Werner Fassbinder 


Brad Davis och Franco Nero. 


The Film: 

Brad Davis (Midnight Express, Chariots Of Fire) stars as Querelle, an enigmatic, drug-dealing sailor on shore leave in the port of Brest. 

Amidst the sultry, highly charged atmosphere, he embarks on a journey of sexual self-discovery.
