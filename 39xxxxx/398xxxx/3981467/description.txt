artist: Fusanosuke Kondo
album: "A Low Down Dirty Shame"
genre: Blues-Rock

This mini-album was released in 1994. All songs included in it were written by Kondo who was one of most talented bluesman in Japan. It would be great if you could enjoy yellow-skined blues with this.

02 My Last Will
03 Red Sky Blues

produed by Fusanosuke Kondo
