This is TheComet's 2003-2007 collection, 445 tracks of some of the best indie music out there.

All tracks are licensed under Creative Commons and are available for free at http://thecomet.newgrounds.com/

From Last.fm:

TheComet (or DJ Comet) is an independant artist hailing from Queensbury, NY. At only 18, he has produced over 400 breakbeat, jungle, industrial, and dnb tracks using almost nothing but his computer. All of his music is freely distributed through Newgrounds, where he has recieved many awards for his music and flash animations.
