Klaus Wunderlich. 
For people with musically fine-tuned ears and broad accept of music.


In 1985 Klaus were already doing more of the production himself and playing drums and bass. Still on the organ but different brand namely Wersi, a German make. Apart from the Wersi Helios and Beta he is using a Pianostar, some synth-bass and drums.

As the title hints, this is music suitable for dancing neatly arranged in the recognizable Wunderlich style. 

Types of dances in the order of the tracks are:
01 Foxtrot, 02 Tango, 03 Samba, 04 Slow Foxtrot, 05 Jive, 06 Waltz, 07 Paso Doble, 08 Slow Waltz, 09 Disco, 10 Rhumba, 11 Quickstep and 12 Cha-Cha.


There are several of these tracks in lower quality around on the Internet, but not in 320kbit encoded with Fraunhofer from CD. As clear and crisp as mp3 can get.
