Story :

A young writer Chu Xun (Lee) faces the supernatural when events from her fictional horror novel begin to unfold in the real world. A mysterious woman who is actually the heroine of her book appears before her, which complicates matters. One day, Chu decides to follow the woman into the other world, in which she experience real and pure terror.
