Klaus Wunderlich. 
For people with musically fine-tuned ears and broad accept of music.


All Time Favourites is an album from 1971 where Klaus, as always multi-tracking, in addition to the Hammond is playing the piano as well. It a neat collection of songs in dancing tempo.
The mp3s are recorded from an LP in very good condition and encoded in VBR with Fraunhofer.

Types of dances are in order of track:
01 Foxtrot, 02 Beguine, 03 Cha-Cha, 04 Bounce, 05 Twist, 06 Slow foxtrot, 07 Tango, 08 Bossa Nova, 09 Shuffle Foxtrot and 10 English Waltz.
