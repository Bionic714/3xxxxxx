                       Description
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Firing Line 2007 pays tribute to the skill and determination of freelance 
cameramen and women who go into hostile environments and whose courage and 
initiative led them to deliver the best photojournalism of the past year.

The program includes scenes from the most outstanding footage and interviews 
with the photojournalists who put themselves at risk to bring the news to the 
world.  It will also includes the winners of the Rory Peck Award and celebrates 
the work of the Rory Peck Trust which helps injured cameramen/women or their 
families in the event of their deaths.
