Pokemon Battle Revolution is the first Pokemon game on Nintendo\'s Wii home console.

To play the .DSP files you will need the in_cube plugin. in_cube is a Winamp plugin intended to play streamed audio formats found in Nintendo Gamecube and Wii games video games.
You can download the latest version of the plugin at their website: http://www.hcs64.com/in_cube.html

The torrent contains the MP3, FLAC and DSP versions of the OST. Just set your bittorrent client to download the version you want.
