"Pet Vet 3D Animal Hospital" Description: It's all about diagnosing and helping out animals. "Pet Vet 3D Animal Hospital" is for children who like games, animals, and maybe even have an interest in being a vet. Animals need to be examined and diagnosed. Shopping, playing, and even riding the horses are a part of the vet's day.
"Pet Vet 3D Animal Hospital" Features: 
Horses, cats, dogs rabbits, ponies, and pigs are the patients of this animal hospital. 
Examine animals to discover their symptoms. 
Diagnose and prescribe a remedy to heal the pets. 
Shop for treats and pet supplies. 
Train and teach the animals tricks. 
Hire people to help take care of animals.
