Pet Vet 3D: Wild Animal Hospital is a super new SIM specifically targeted at young children with an interest in pet clinics. The game will really intrigue animal lovers, especially very young children fascinated by the goings on at their local pet clinic. This new Wild Animal Hospital SIM is the sequel to the successful Pet Vet 3D: Animal Hospital also from Viva Media, and in this sequel, the focus is more on exotic animals, than the domestic pets included in the first Pet Vet 3D: Animal Hospital SIM.
