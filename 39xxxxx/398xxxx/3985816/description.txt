205. Big Brother    10/11/1965  
The 918 lands at Mogador in North Africa to refuel and re-arm and find that the senior officer is Joe Gallagher's brother, Lieutenant Col. Preston "Pres" Gallagher

Guest star: Ted Markland (Sergeant), Robert Biheller (Sergeant Oscar Bessy), Ken Lynch (Master Sgt. Orland), Harry Millard (Captain Strader), Trevor Cuming (Scout Officer), Robert Sorrells (Staff Sgt.
