VAGGELIS KARIPIS (percussionist)

Born in Athens in 1971, Vaggelis Karipis entered the world of percussions at the age of sixteen. He was an autodidact. Later on he decided that he wanted to work on Greek traditional music, and so he studied oud  and Byzantine Music.

He has collaborated with distinguish Greek composers and singers, such as Elefteria Arvanitaki, Dionysis Savvopoulos, George Dalaras, Domna Samiou, Alkinoos Ioanidis, Chronis Aidonidis, and he has performed in many concerts and inside and outside Greece (Womad, Sfinx, Meinz).
