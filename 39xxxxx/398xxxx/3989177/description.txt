Strawberry Shortcake returns with all her fantastic furry pals in BEST PETS YET! Pupcake is a little orphaned dog in "Here Comes Pupcake," but when Strawberry takes him in, not everyone is happy! Her spoiled and jealous housecat Custard is resolved to make him feel unwelcome; will she learn that there's enough love to go around? Then, in Peppermint's Pet Peeve," Peppermint Fizz has a lesson to learn about cheating when her pet, Cola Chameleon, wins a pet competition by dubious methods. Turns out, though, that "winning" isn't necessary to have fun with friends!

Audio Languages: English, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese
