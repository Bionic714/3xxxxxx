Team Fortress 2 and CounterStrike Source Lamer Sprays
-----------------------------------------------------


Installation:
-------------

Place the .VTF and VMT texturefiles in:


TF2
---

(your dir)\Steam\steamapps\(your username)\team fortress 2\tf\materials\VGUI\logos
and
(your dir)\Steam\steamapps\(your username)\team fortress 2\tf\materials\VGUI\logos\UI


CSS
---

(your dir)\Steam\steamapps\(your username)\Counterstrike Sorce\css\materials\VGUI\logos
and
(your dir)\Steam\steamapps\(your username)\Counterstrike Sorce\css\materials\VGUI\logos\UI
