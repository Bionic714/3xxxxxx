This is a .bin image that is ready to be burned with your image burning tool onto a CD-r.  It included SNES-station, which is a Super Nintendo emulator for the ps2, and over 400 ROM's.  A modded ps2 or swap disk is required to play.

For more information on the Super Nintendo emulator and how to create your own CD, go here http://snes-station.gamebase.ca/
