Jacques Audiard combines disability, insecurity, love, revenge, and intensity to form his modern thriller Sur mes l?vres. The film is set in the unwelcoming offices and dirty underworld of Paris in 2002. It follows a storyline where a lonely woman, burdened by disability and lack of respect, takes comfort in a younger man who enters her life. The man is by no means perfect, but neither is the woman. The two make sacrifices for one another, and together they advance through scandalous operations while falling in love along the way.

The woman/heroine in this movie, Carla, is introduced immediately with a shot of her putting her earpiece in. It isn't until she faints from the exhaustion of picking up after her unpleasant coworkers that Carla accepts the boss's offer of hiring an intern to lighten her load. Carla's loneliness is expressed by the gender specific criteria she uses to advertise for the position of her new assistant.
