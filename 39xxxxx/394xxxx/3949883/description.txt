Another 4 CDs worth of excellent end-of-year material. Here are a bunch of live tracks that you may not have heard before. The first two Cds are from concerts, the third is from sessions and the last is a compilation of live covers from concerts and sessions. And who do you have to thank for this wonderful mix of live musical delights = Shankly, of course.
