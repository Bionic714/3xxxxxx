We support schooling of poor rural and tribal girls in India since 1977. This is an effective way of eradicating poverty and global ecological crisis, ensuring peace and human rights (especially rights of women and future generations). This 15 min film of BOCS Foundation presents life of these jungle schools. See: http://bocs.eu

1977 ota tamogatjuk szegeny falusi es torzsi lanyok iskolaztatasat Indiaban. Ez az egyik leghatekonyabb cselekves a szegenyseg es a globalis okologiai valsag enyhiteseben, a beke es az emberi jogok (kulonosen a nok es jovo generaciok jogai) erdekeben. A BOCS Alapitvany ebben a 15 perces kisfilmben bemutatja ezeknek a dzsungel-iskolaknak az eletet. Bovebben: http://bocs.eu
