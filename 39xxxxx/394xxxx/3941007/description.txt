LoveIsNotMyOnlyCrime was formed in Gothenburg (Sweden) in 2005 and soon found their very own style somewhere in between eighties fuzzbox indie and the nineties emo scene.
This is their limited edition debut 3\" CD, released by Wonderland Records on December 24, 2007. Enjoy!
