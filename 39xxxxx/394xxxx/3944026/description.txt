Das Ich

 is a German electronic music group formed in 1989. The group, fronted by Stefan Ackermann and Bruno Kramm were one of the founders of a movement in the early 1990s, which was called "Neue Deutsche Todeskunst" (New German Death Art). The German term das Ich makes reference to freudian psychology idea of ego.
