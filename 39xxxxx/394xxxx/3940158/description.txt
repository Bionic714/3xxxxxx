Rasta Finale

After a season of ups and downs and a tense elimination in Miami, it's down to New York's final two suitors. They accompany New York to the tropics of Jamaica for the final showdown. Cliff-diving, horse-back riding, heated, passionate dinners and men literally climbing the walls of their room make up the heart of this can't miss episode. Who will New York choose to be her man? Sister Patterson makes a unexpected appearance to grill the two suitors, just to make sure her daughter picks the right one. But will New York finally listen?
