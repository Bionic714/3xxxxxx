Neuroactive is one of the most successful Finnish technopop bands on the international scene. Neuroactive was formed in 1991 by J.Tuohimaa, V.Rainne and V.Brusi. Neuroactive had their first record deal with Cyberware Productions in 1994 which lead to their first release "Morphology" within the same year.The first album was mainly influenced by electro and industrial techno and most of the tracks were taken from old demos which were made during the band's early days. In 1995 Neuroactive released the EP "Neuron". It got very positive feedback from European press and audience.
