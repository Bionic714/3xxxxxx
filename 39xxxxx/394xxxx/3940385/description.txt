Luftwaffe Training Units and their Aircraft 1935-1945
92 pages 
Publisher: Hikoki Publications (January 2003) 
Language: English 
ISBN-10: 0951989928 

An illustrated account of the training of Luftwaffe personnel during the decade leading up to the end of WWII with descriptions of the aircraft used. Information is provided on the various flying schools set up and the bases from which they flew.
