Hillsong Live Worship for Kids 
Max, Melody & Hillsong Kids team 

Jesus Is My Superhero is the first ever kids worship album from Hillsong, and 
features live, energetic praise and beautiful worship. 



Song List: Superhero, King Of Majesty, I\'m Really Happy, Never Give Up, Blessed, 
Oh How I Love You, Anthem Of Praise, Alive, You\'re The One, Get Up And Dance, 
Breathe, Night Song, Did You Konw, Your Eyes, Superhero (reprise) 

Description
The atmosphere was electric, as children lifted their hands shouting \"Jesus 
You\'re My Superhero!\" This was the setting for the recording of the first ever 
Hillsong worship album for kids, over two mornings at KIDSONG during Hillsong 
Conference 2004. Max & Melody and the Hillsong Kids team led well over a 
thousand children in worship. With 15 songs of energetic praise and beautiful 
worship, this album will be a blessing to adults and kids alike.
