Alexandre Dumas, The Three Musketeers 

In The Three Musketeers we follow the heroic adventures of the handsome young Gascon D?Artagnan, whose life is changed irrevocably when he moves to Paris in 1626 and throws in his lot with the dauntless musketeers Athos, Aramis and Porthos.

Read by Bill Homewood

A familiar reader on Naxos AudioBooks, Bill Homewood is well known for his innumerable television shows and leading credits in the West End, major theaters and the Royal Shakespeare Company. He has also directed theater on both sides of the Atlantic, and his various writing credits include Theatrical Letters, published by Marginalia.
