Here comes Yat-Kha, the most famous rock band from Tuva, reknown for its lead singer and his amazing throat singing. If you don't know them yet, you really should check them out.

The albums are:
Yenisei Punk (1995), debut album for Yat-Kha as a rock band
Tundra's Ghosts (1997), more electronic remixes by Ivan Sokolovsky
Dalai Beldiri (1999)
Aldyn Dashka (2000)
tuva.rock (2003)
ReCovers (2005), covers of rock band in the unique style of Yat-Kha, you gotta listen to that!

Enjoy
