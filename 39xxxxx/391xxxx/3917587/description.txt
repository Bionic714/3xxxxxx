This is the pilot manga that started off the hit franchise: DEATH NOTE

Wrote by: Tsugumi Ohba
Illustrated by: Takeshi Obata

-55 image files (1 file per 1 page of the manga)
-Completely translated into English
-Black and white

For anyone who hasn't heard of Death Note (!), this is a quick summary:
A high school student, Yagami Light, who is near the top of all his classes, finds a Death Note. After testing it, he realizes that this supernatural notebook has the ability to kill whoevers name is wrote in it.

This pilot manga shows the events that lead up to Volume 1 of the released mangas.

Enjoy ^^
