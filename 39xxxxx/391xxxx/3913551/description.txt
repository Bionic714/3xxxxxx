The inmates of a German World War II prisoner of war camp conduct an espionage and sabotage campaign right under the noses of their warders.


Hogan's Heroes was a sitcom about allied POW's held in the fictitious prisoner of war camp - Stalag 13 (somewhere in Germany) - during WW II. It was shown on CBS from 1965-1971 running for 168 episodes. This torrent includes season 1 and 4.
