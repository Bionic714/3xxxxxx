From Wikipedia: Thriller - En Grym Film (Thriller - A Cruel Picture, also known as They Call Her One Eye, Hooker's Revenge, Thriller) is a Swedish movie from 1973 written and directed by Bo Arne Vibenius. The original length was 107 minutes. It is known to be the first film to be totally banned by the Swedish movie censorship board. It was cut down to 104 minutes and then 86 minutes, but still banned. It was finally released after being cut down to 82 minutes. In the USA it had also been cut to 82 minutes. dubbed in English.
