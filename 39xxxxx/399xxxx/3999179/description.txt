Air Date : 30-Jan-1978 

Writer : Terry Nation 

Director : Michael E. Briant 

Synopsis :The Liberator is trapped in a web around an unknown planet after being sabotaged by Cally. The web 
was created by two genetically engineered humanoids, created by exiled beings from Cally's home planet. They 
want Blake's help to commit genocide against the Decimas, another genetically created race.
