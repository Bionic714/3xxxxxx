There's only about 24% of the songs listed in the torrent, but its 24% out of 382 songs (about 90 songs). Also note that the songs each have a box next to them that looks either like a [ ], [x], or a [X]. The songs with an empty box are not in this torrent, the songs with a little "x" are in the torrrent, but are not the EXACT radio cut/remix used in DJ Inphinity's mixes (or I'm not 100% that they are), and the songs with a large "X" next to them are the official song that DJ Inphinity used in his remix.
