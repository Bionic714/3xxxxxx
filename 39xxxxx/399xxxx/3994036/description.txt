Klaus Wunderlich. 
For people with musically fine-tuned ears and broad accept of music.


Kauf' dir einen bunten Luftballon is from approx. 1963 and rare of it's kind. Not many albums from Klaus Wunderlich features singing. This one does. Singers featured are Sonja Michaels and the Centauris.
Taking into consideration that this album has been recording from an LP the quality is not too bad.

Many thanks goes to the ripper.
