This Martin Scorsese film depicts the Janus-like quality of Las Vegas--it has a glittering, 
glamorous face, as well as a brutal, cruel one. Ace Rothstein and Nicky Santoro, mobsters

of the 1970's and '80's are revealed. Ace is the smooth operator of the Tangiers casino,
