Hacao Linux 2.16 Pro is a small fast installable LiveCD operating system, localized for Vietnam.

It is based on Puppy Linux 2.16 but comes with extra features like office & multimedia software.

It is also installed on the Intel Classmate low-cost laptop now being sold in Vietnam.
