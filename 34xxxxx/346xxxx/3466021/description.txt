PC MACLAN? gives you easy, streamlined access between Macs and PCs. Start a letter on your Mac and transfer it to your PC laptop to finish on the plane. Making a DVD on your Mac? Import your vacation photos from your Windows desktop.

PC MACLAN? brings both worlds together seamlessly, without jeopardizing home network security. PC MACLAN? also includes a powerful print server, so you can use your printer - in either direction - regardless of the computer to which it's connected. All it takes is a click of the mouse.
