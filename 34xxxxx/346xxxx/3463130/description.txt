Something is out for blood in the jungles of Latin America feasting on chickens, goats and cows and some believe it to be a blood-sucking alien. But what is it, an alien pet left behind or a strange CIA genetics experiment? Known as the Chupacabra - Spanish for "goat-sucker" - this creature has fascinated scientists for decades. See what they discover when an actual hair from this strange beast is sent to Dr. Leslie Weis at Trent University for DNA analysis and what the tests reveal.
