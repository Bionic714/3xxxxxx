Those cats at Diggler never rest! This is the second fab installment in their "Affairs" series - and what a corker it is!

Taking it's title from the Schwabing district of Munich (that in the late 60's belonged to the beatniks, the existentialists and the other hip young things - the 'long term students'), Schwabing Affairs is a compilation of groovy and easy tunes taken from the soundtracks of the movies being produced there in the 60's and 70's.

There's tracks from movies such as Engelchen Macht Weiter Hoppe, Hoppe Reiter (Up The Establishment!), Der Kerl Liebt Mich und Das Soil ich Glauben?, Mädchen, Mädchen (Girls, Girls), Bengelchen Liebt Kreuz und Quer (24-Hour Lover), Jet Generation (How Girls Love Men of Today) and more.

Check Track 11 (Leave This Lesbian World) for the crazy backward-beat thing going on - brilliant!
