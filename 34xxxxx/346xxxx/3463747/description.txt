33. A Little Romance 
First aired: 1/13/1989
Writer: Bob Fraser, Rob Dames 
Director: John Bowab 
Guest star: John Aprea (Nick Katsopolis), Andrea Barber (Kimmy Gibbler), Lori Loughlin (Rebecca Donaldson) , Hilary Wayne (Herself), Liza Greer (Heather), Dee Marcus (Violet), Robin Greer (Crystal), Jonathan Brandis (Michael Montfort) 

The "Ugly Duckling" story is not always the right story to tell. Rebecca gets jealous at the charity auction she and Danny are hosting because a very rude woman is bidding on Jesse.
