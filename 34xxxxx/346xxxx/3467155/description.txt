Geneticist, broadcaster and environmentalist David Suzuki shares some insight on how humans are now shaping the future of our planet. He's hosting a new radio series which explores earth as is our nursery, living room, garbage bin and laboratory.

Part of Suzuki's series explores voluntary simplicity, a movement toward a better quality of life with a smaller impact on the environment. He cites the New Road Map Foundation (www.slnet.com/cip/nrm) in Seatle as an example of the simple life.
