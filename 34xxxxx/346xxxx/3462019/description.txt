Pardon my Backfire (1953)

    Director: Jules White
    Cast: Benny Rubin, Frank Sully, Phil Arnold, Fred Kelsey

The Three Stooges are auto mechanics who need money so they can marry their girls. When some escaped convicts pull into their garage, the boys manage to capture them and use the reward money to marry their sweethearts.

One of two stooge shorts filmed in 3D.
