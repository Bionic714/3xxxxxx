Homer Simpson, This is Your Wife

This is a Simpson's episode written and partially voiced by Ricky Gervais, the co-creator and co-writter of the original British version of "The Office", HBO's "Extras", and the hugely popular podcast "The Ricky Gervais Show".  Encoded in Xvid and prepped by Squirrel.

Homer wants a plasma screen tv when he see's the one Lenny just bought.  He becomes involved with a wife swapping reality show to pay for it.
