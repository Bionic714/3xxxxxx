Trent Reznor, Peter Murphy, Jeordie White, Atticus Ross: Live In Boston [6_23_06]

Radio Show 3

On June 23rd, 2006, Trent Reznor of Nine Inch Nails and Peter Murphy of Bauhaus, along with Jeordie White (NIN) and Atticus Ross, performed four songs for a small group of radio winners in Boston.  The unique set was broadcast live on several radio stations in the Boston area.  These are 320kbps MP3 soundboard-quality recordings.  Multimedia from the performance can be found at http://www.nin.com/current/radioshows

Track listing:

01. Reptile (originally by Nine Inch Nails)
02. Warm Leatherette (originally by The Normal)
03. Strange Kind Of Love (originally by Peter Murphy)
04. Nightclubbing (originally by Iggy Pop)
