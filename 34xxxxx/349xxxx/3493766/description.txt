
VLC Media Player 0.8.5 for Windows - incl 14 extra skins (Release date: May 6, 2006).

It supports a large number of multimedia formats, without the need for additional codecs. The VLC Player needs a little CPU power.
VLC (initially VideoLAN Client) is a highly portable multimedia player for various audio and video formats (MPEG-1, MPEG-2, MPEG-4, DivX, avi, wmv, wma, mp3, ogg, ...) as well as DVDs, VCDs, and various streaming protocols.
In the content of the files is the Windows self-extracting package and 14 extra skins.


SKIN Notes:
To use the skins, place the ".vlt" files in your VLC\Skins folder (...\VideoLAN\VLC\skins).
Then start the VLC Media Player, select:  > Settings > Switch Interface > Skins2 mode. Then simply RIGHT CLICK ON TOP of the Player and > Select Skin > e. g. MediaPlayer.
Additional support:  http://www.videolan.org/support
