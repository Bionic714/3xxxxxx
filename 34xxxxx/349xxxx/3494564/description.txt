Buckaroo Banzai, a jack-of-all-trades/renaissance man/genius/adventurer, successfully tests a device allowing a fast vehicle to pass through solid matter across the eighth dimension.  Unfortunately there are villainous aliens exiled on Earth who see this device as a means to return to their homeworld, which, in turn, threatens Earth with destruction should they succeed...

With Peter Weller, Ellen Barkin, Jeff Goldblum, Christopher Lloyd...
