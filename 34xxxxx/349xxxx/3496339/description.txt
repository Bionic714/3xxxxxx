Silly low budget science fiction parody with Robert Urich playing a swashbuckling space pirate in a universe in which water is the most precious resource, which is monopolized by the evil Templars.  After being captured and nearly castrated he is hired by a princess he previously kidnapped to start looking for her father and the legendary "Seventh World" (Earth) rumored to be the last place in the cosmos with plenty of water left on it...
