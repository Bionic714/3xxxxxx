This is the english translation of Vladan Vlajkovic's explosive book Military secret (originally Vojna tajna) (2004) which in 2 parts documents some of the most secret arrangements which occured in Serbia under the rule of Slobodan Milosevic. Among other unique information it contains transcripts of secret meetings of Serbia's high military command and its analysis. Vlajkovic is Serbia's military intelligence officer and has been imprisoned for releasing this documents and his book banned in Serbia. More than 400 pages of interesting reading. A must read for everyone.
