In mid-2006 Bob Saget was featured as the focus of a rap song by Jamie Kennedy in his comedy-quasi-reality show "Blowin' Up". The title of the song was "Rollin' With Saget," it featured much of the foul language and comedy that Saget had previously been criticized for.
