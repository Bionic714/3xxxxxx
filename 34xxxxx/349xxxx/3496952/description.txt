MACHINA II/The Friends & Enemies of Modern Music is a (concept) album by The Smashing Pumpkins released on September 5th, 2000. It is a sequel to MACHINA/The Machines of God and is the sixth album from the Pumpkins. It is a limited pressing of only 25 hand-cut, hand-numbered, vinyl records. It was never commercially released, instead copies were given to close friends of the band with instructions tocirculate the new material as quickly as possible. It consists of three EPs full of B-Sides and alternate versions, and one LP being the actual album.
