A.K.A.-It's A Wiley World! is a romantic comedy about the larger than life, Pat, aka ?The Hawk?, Wiley. Pat, very stylish in his white pants, funky shirts and seven inch chin bush, is trying desperately to find a girlfriend while at the same time get his entertainment career off the ground. When Pat meets Tiffany he forgets about his best friend Buddy and his aspiring entertainment career until he is confronted by his band, The Polly Wogs. After he re-evaluates his situation Pat decides to pursue what will bring him his ultimate happiness.
