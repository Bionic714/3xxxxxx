Roll into action with 10 Pin: Champions Alley, the easy to play bowling game that lets you play alone or with up to seven friends.
- Play alone or with friends.
- Choose from a range of exuberant characters.
- Pick from game types from quick practice to full tournament.
- Face colourful opponents each with their own strengths and weaknesses.
- For up to eight players (no extra controllers required)!
Combining ease of play and multi-player battles, 10 Pin: Champions Alley is a perfect party game for kids and adults alike, equally likely to be enjoyed after an evening out or on a rainy afternoon.
