                                            English
     
                Captured on camera, on the 5th of May 1980, the British ArmyÆs elite Special Air Service
                stormed the Iranian Embassy in London, where hostages were being held by Iraqi terrorists.
                Within ten minutes, the terrorists were killed, and the remaining hostages rescued. Who 
                were these black-clad men in balaclavas, who resolved the conflict with such ruthless 
                efficiency? This programme examines the SAS, from its conception as a shall, 
                deep-penetration raiding force led by Commando Office David Stirling in the deserts of Libya
                and Egypt in World War II, to its work on the modern day battlefields in the Falklands 
                and Gulf wars. Today the SAS is among the best special services in the world. Its men are
                the toughest of the tough, and those who have served in it over the 60 years since its 
                inception have lived and fought unswervingly under the motto on their 
                badges: 'Who Dares Wins'.  
 
                                            Swedish       

                Fangade av kameran den 5 maj 1980 stormar den brittiska armens elitforband 
                Special Air Service (SAS) den iranska ambassaden i London, dar irakiska terrorister 
                tagit gisslan. Tio minuter senare har terroristerna dodats och gisslan befriats. 
                Vilka var de svartkladda maskerade mannen som loste uppdraget med en sadan skoningslos 
                effektivitet. Detta program berattar hela historien om SAS. anda fran starten under 
                kommandoofficer David Stirling da den lilla styrkan spred skrack bakom de tyska linjerna
                i den libyska och i den egyptiska oknen, till styrkans insats pa nutida slagfalt pa 
                Falklandsoarna och i gulfkriget 19901991. I dag tillhor SAS de yppersta av varldens
                elitforband. Mannen ar de hardaste av de harda. De som tjanat SAS under de 60 aren 
                sedan tillkomsten under andra varldskriget har levat och ofortrutet kampat enligt mottot
                pa baskern: Who Dares Wins. Den som vagar vinner.
