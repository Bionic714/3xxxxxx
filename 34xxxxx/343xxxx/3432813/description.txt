RELEASE

Blue - A Short Film (23 minutes, DivX codec)

Created by Christopher Mullins, Aaron Webster, and James Anderson, this 23-minute
long, completely CG-generated short film, is, according to the official site of
the creators (http://www.xenobi.com): "A sci-fi adventure about a small robot who 
wakes up all alone on a space station and gets into more trouble than he can 
handle."

The short has won several film festival awards since its release in late 2003, and
you can find out more information about 'Blue: A Short Film' at its official
website: http://www.blueshortfilm.com/main.html
