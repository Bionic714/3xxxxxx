Pigface's 1991 debut, for collectors and newcomers alike (regardless of which category you fall into, anyone who wouldn't mind uploading their "Preaching To The Perverted: The Best Of Pigface" would be very much appreciated!!) 

For those who don't know, Pigface is an industrial "supergroup" featuring an ever-changing lineup of personnel, in the studio and on tour. This incarnation of Pigface is: 

? Martin Atkins (founder; Killing Joke, Ministry, Public Image Limited) 
? William Rieflin (founder; Ministry)
? Paul Barker (Ministry, Revolting Cocks) 
? Chris Connelly (Ministry, Revolting Cocks) 
? En Esch (KMFDM, Excessive Force) 
? Ogre (Skinny Puppy, Ohgr) 
? Trent Reznor (NIN) 
? Matt Schultz 
? William Tucker 
? David Yow (The Jesus Lizard, Scratch Acid). 
Additional assistance by Steve Albini (Nirvana, The Pixies) and Kurt Moore.
