From Director/Producer Aaron Greer of www.nexthorizonfilms.com

James Bond meets Star Wars in this explosive action thriller from Next Horizon Films. What if there was more to Ronald Reagan?s program than what the public was told, and the Star Wars technology was developed?  When it is stolen, CIA agents Ryan Davis and Kate Park are sent to recover the technology.  Opposing them is the evil Shane Veers, who will stop at nothing to bring about the destruction of the United States.

Starring Andrew Reid, Angel Tarver, Patti Herron, and Brian Hollett.  And Co-Starring Laszlo Kovacs, Dan Hickey, Ali Hamka, and Steve Martin.
