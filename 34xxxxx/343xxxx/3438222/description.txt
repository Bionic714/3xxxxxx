Dragon's Lair (c) by Starcom (1983)                 

SUMMARY:
Dragon's Lair! The fantasy adventure where you become a valiant knight on a quest to rescue the fair princess from the clutches of an evil dragon.
You control the actions of a daring adventurer finding his way through the castle of a dark wizard who has enchanted it with treacherous monsters and obstacles. In the mysterious caverns below the castle, your odyssey continues against the awesome forces that oppose your efforts to reach the dragon's lair.
Lead on, adventurer. Your quest awaits! 


				RELEASE INFO 
               
The laser game arcade with original animation quality compressed in one cd using TMPGEnc Plus 2.5 Templates included so you can build your own single-cd laser game and share


				INSTALL INFO
Burn the autorunner cd and play, no need to copy any files over the cd itself
(if autorun is deactivated, run DaphneLoader.exe or Lair.bat in root directory)


				KEYS CONFIGURATION
				
5 and 6 insert coins | 1 and 2 start playing
Arrows control the movements of Dirk the Daring
Space has Dirk use his sword
Esc goes back to desktop
