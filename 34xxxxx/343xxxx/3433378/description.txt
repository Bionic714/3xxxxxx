In a remote town the feared gang, ?Hell Black Heaven?, are devising a terrible plan. 

Operation ?Gang Snatch? will capture and put in jail all the most powerful hoods on the patch so that Black Hell Heaven can control the whole area. Shin, the main character in the story, meets and befriends Ginji just after he has been captured, together, the two resolve to wage a personal war to destroy the Hell Black Heaven gang. But its not that simple, along the way Shin and his friends will encounter various enemy gang members ready to do whatever it takes to stop them.
