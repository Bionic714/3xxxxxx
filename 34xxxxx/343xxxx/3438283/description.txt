Italian progressive/spymphonic rock at it's best

This is great stuff, and shows a truly mature band for the 30+ years they have been recording. The transition is seamless from one track to the next, and there's sitar, violin, funky organ jamming, and smooth vocal delivery. For a band that's been around as long as Le Orme, this sounds fresh and inspired/inspiring. Hats off to these lads.
