The second official expansion pack. 
  
Discover fantastic coastal areas with cliffs, tidelands, dikes and 
lighthouses. Find diversified gaming fun in epic battles and peaceful 
build-up missions. Experience the fondly animated world of The Settlers more
intensively, in which you can now interact with animals. 

For the first time, three evil characters are playable in single player 
campaigns: Kerberos, Varg and Mary de Mortfichet.
