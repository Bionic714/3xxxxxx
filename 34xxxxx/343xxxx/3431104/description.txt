Pirates of the Caribbean (Xbox) 
Pirates of the Caribbean is inspired by the Disney film of the same name. The game will offer a 17th century adventure based on the animated movie with "stunning graphics, deep role-playing elements and thrilling quests". Players take to the high seas as a mercenary for hire, a peaceful and profitable merchant or a swashbuckling pirate on a quest to make money by hook or by crook.
