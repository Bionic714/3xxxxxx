Ape Escape Academy gives you the chance to train apes and prepare them to take over the world! The villain of previous Ape Escape games, Specter, keeps failing at his attempt to conquer because of a few inept apes. To rectify his problem, he decides to form the Ape Escape Academy. Serving as principal, he'll train apes to follow his will -- but when it comes to trouble and disobedience, these monkeys are pros.

Features: 

- As many as 40 mini-games that test your skills with hockey, golf, dodge ball karate and more. 
- Exciting multiplayer action for up to four people. 
- Multiple mini-games challenge you to practice harder and earn the high scores. 
- Collect all the Monkey Statue trophies as you proceed through your academic years.
