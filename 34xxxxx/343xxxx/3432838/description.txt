In this action game you can become a real paparazzi doing photo sessions with beautiful models. 
They poses according to your requests. 
Try to catch their eye! Remember that each moment could be the right moment to take the perfect picture!
A lot of other paparazzi take part in photo sessions too! It is not easy to find a good location to take pictures from. 
Do your best to get the models attention: jump, dance, call her. 
Try to catch her eye and get her in the best poses.

FEATURES: 
- Three beautiful models striking poses just for you! 
- Dont let your rival beat you! 
- Use the points you earn to buy new photo equipment (a wide-angle, telephoto lens or even a tripod) 
- While you are learning you can use an automatic camera, but switch to a manual camera if you are a pro!
