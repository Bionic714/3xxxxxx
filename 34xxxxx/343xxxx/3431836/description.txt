Based on the popular Tough comic, featured in Young Jump magazine. Tough: Dark Fight features an original story which takes place in the two years between the original Koukou Tekken-den Tough comic (published between 1993 and 2003) and the later Tough reprisal series. Fans of the series will be able to experience the happenings of those two missing years. The game features a number of characters, including eight who havent been featured in the comic.
