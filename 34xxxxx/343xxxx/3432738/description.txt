Dimension in Fear is a film directed by legendary Ted V. Mikels (who directed Astro-Zombies, Corpse Grinders and Girl in Gold Boots). 

Plot: Escaped murderer Cal Lewis wants revenge for those responsible for him being put away. Enroute from Texas to Las Vegas he crosses paths with young TV news reporter Deidre Holland who quickly becomes his target.

Starring Ron Jason, Nikki West. Legendary actresses Dolores Fuller (Ed Wood's ex-girlfriend) and Liz Renay also appear as well as Mikels himself.
