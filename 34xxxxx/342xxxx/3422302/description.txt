Legend of Galactic Heroes is an epic space opera, based on a series of science fiction novels by Yoshiki Tanaka. It tells the story of the epic struggle between the Galactic Empire (a dynastic monarchy based on 19th century Prussia) and the Free Planets Alliance, a nominal democracy. The scope of LotGH is epic, with hundreds of characters with complex relationships and space battles on a grand scale involving thousands of ships in precise formations.
