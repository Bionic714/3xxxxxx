
Super Strong God Live Worship for Kids


The second live worship album for kids!
Join Max & Melody and the Hillsong Kids team with 15 new songs, full of life, energy and heartfelt praise. Recorded live at Kidsong World, your kids will love it!

The DVD includes SPECIAL FEATURES such as...

*Live Worship!
*Visualisation ? sing along with the words on the screen, plus your choice of a radio mix, or music mix only!
*Actions ? Learn the actions to some of the songs, with Max & Melody and the Hillsong Kids team!
*Behind the Scenes Documentary (a look at Kidsong World BTS)

Release Date: November 2005
