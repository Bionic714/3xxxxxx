Two contract killers cross paths in the middle of the same job and realize they
are childhood friends. Together they take a break from killing and visit the 
small island they once called home. After reflecting on their past lives they 
decided to team up and use their talents in killing for good... much to the 
upset of the crime syndicates.  

More a thematic extrapolation to the first 'Dead or Alive' than a sequel, this
