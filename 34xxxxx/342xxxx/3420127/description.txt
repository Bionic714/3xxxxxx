-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
				Game Description
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

You are a criminal, you are pushed for money, and you need to get it
urgently by all means. Create your own team for stealing or jacking
vehicles, robbing banks and shops, picking pockets or even fighting
other gangs! And don't forget to watch the cops, they are your main
trouble.

You lose the game when you are arrested for the third time or eventually
killed. To win the game, you need to repay your debts and leave off
your illegal business before this happens. The more cash you get,
the more score you gain.
