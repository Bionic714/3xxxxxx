The sequel to the great SNES game Secret of Mana (in Japan called Seiken Densetsu) which until recently only has been available in japanese. It has now been translated by the following people:
 
Text translation by Lina`chan, Nuku-nuku & SoM2Freak
ROM patch design by Neill Corlett

All the patching of the ROM has been done so just plug it into your emulator and enjoy this RPG-classic!
