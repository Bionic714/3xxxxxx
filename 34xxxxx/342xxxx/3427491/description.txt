Story:
Taking place after the events of Transformers Victory: The Cybertron Victory Saber
has been seriously injured while rescuing Kai and Emusa from the planet Feminia.
And now a new Destron emperor of Darkness has appeared: Violenjaiger!
He resurrects 9 of the strongest Destron Combiners and Giants. Their mission:
to create a planet called the Destron Zone. In order to do this, they need to steal
the Super Energy - Energon Z, and also to acquire the Zodiac - a mysterious energy
that was said to have created the very universe. Now it's up the Powered Masters:
Dai Atlas and Sonic Bomber to stop them!
