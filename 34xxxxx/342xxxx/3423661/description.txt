VMware VirtualCenter is virtual infrastructure management software for   
  businesses that want to build cost-effective next generation computing   
  environments and optimized data centers. VirtualCenter provides a central
  point of control for your data center's virtual computing resources,     
  driving flexibility, efficiency and responsiveness.                      
  VMware VirtualCenter allows you to:                                      
                                                                           
  * Manage hundreds of servers from a single console                       
  * Instantly provision new servers with standardized templates            
  * Secure the environment with robust access control                      
  * Perform hardware maintenance with zero downtime
