A graphic novel about the vampire Lestat, known from the Interview With The Vampire and Queen of The Damned movies. The movies are number one and three in The Vampire Chronicles, and this is number two, where you'll get to learn Lestat. Where was he born, how did he grow up, how did he become a vampire and more.

With 358 pages in full color, you will have something to read during those slow x-mas days ;)

Read with CDisplay or open with WinRAR (or similiar program) and unzip the pictures to a folder for viewing.
