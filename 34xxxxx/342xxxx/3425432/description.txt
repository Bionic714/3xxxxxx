The newest issue of Weekly Shounen JUMP revealed that Square Enix will release the newest addition to its popular mech series Front Mission in Japan on December 29th. Previously, Front Mission 5: Scars of the War was only listed for a December release in Japan. The games protagonist is Fen Walter, a soldier serving in the USN military. Heroine Lin Wainright is a female officer yielding from the same institution as Fen and cares about him. Front Mission 5 will feature a lot of dramatic event scenes focusing not only on the war, but also about the relationship between Fen and Lin as wel as the soldiers everyday life.
