Nein Absolut Nicht
Musictrade release MT006
Released: December 29, 2005
Lame HVBR, including hi-res cover artwork.

Built on the soundworld from a single episode of the German police tv series 'Der Alte', energized by retro disco, funk and reggae beats and spiced up with intriquing speech synthesizer and field recording material, this is a joyful, genre-transcending album that will lure you both to dance and to listen.
