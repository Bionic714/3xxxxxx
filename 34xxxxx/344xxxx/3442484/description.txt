TrackMania Installation:
1. Mount TrackManiaTrackmania.iMMERSiON.ShareReactor.bin and install.
2. Execute TrackManiatrackmania_powerup_setup.exe to upgrade to Power Up!.
3. Execute TrackManiatmoriginal_frompowerup_setup.exe to upgrade to Original.
4. Mount TrackManiatpumitrackmania.mds and play!
5. Use these serial keys when asked: 9WTE-YY6D-RRDK-AKMY-79E
                                     H9BR-MA6P-G7HH-4M4G-B8T

TrackMania Sunrise Installation:
1. Mount TrackMania Sunrisetmsunrise_patched.mds and install.
2. Execute TrackMania Sunrisetmsextreme_fromsunrise_setup.exe to upgrade to eXtreme.
3. Mount TrackMania Sunrisetmsunrise_patched.mds and play!

TrackMania Nations Installation:
1. Execute TrackMania Nationstrackmania_nations_eswc.exe to install.
2. Play!

TrackMania Sunrise is protected by StarForce 3.6, you may need to unplug all your IDE drivers and mount the image with Daemon Tools v4 to play.
