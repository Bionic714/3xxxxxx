Plato Video Converter is a powerful video conversion program.It supports converting video files from formats including AVI,DivX,XviD,MPEG,WMV, MOV To AVI,MPEG4,DivX,XviD,MPEG1,MPEG2,WMV,RealMedia video format files.
Key Features:

(1)Convert MPEG,WMV,MOV files to MEPG4,DivX,Xvid format files
(2)Convert AVI,MPEG,WMV,MOV files to RM files
(3)Convert AVI,MPEG,MOV files to WMV files
(4)Save the video frame to Images files
(5)Support BMP, EMF, GIF, J2K, JPEG, PCX, PNG, RAS, TGA, TIFF, WMF image Format
(6) Setting Beginning point and Ending point to Convert
