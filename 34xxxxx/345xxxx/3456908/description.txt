When council clerk Martin Barrett is found shot dead, Barnaby and Jones discovered he was an expert blackmailer. As they try and find which of his victims hated him enough to kill him, the trail leads to the sleepy Fennacombe Bay. Why are Richard and Maggie Florian spending so much time at sea and could police committee chairman Sir John Waverley have a scandal in his past years? With the help of a ex-policeman turned fisherman, Barnaby takes to the waves to find out.
