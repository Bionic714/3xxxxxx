	 A-one Video To Audio Convertor is a program to extract audio from video files . Key Features: (1)It supports AVI,DivX,Xvid,MPEG,WMV,MOV format video files (2)Extract audio from video files and save as MP3/WAV/WMA/OGG format audio files (3)Set the beginning and ending piont to extract (4)Capture video frames to Image files with format BMP, EMF, GIF, J2K, JPEG, PCX, PNG, RAS, TGA, TIFF, WMF
