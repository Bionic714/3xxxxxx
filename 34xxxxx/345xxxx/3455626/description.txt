In September 2005, DJ Yoda performed his first ground-breaking DVD scratch show to a packed out movie theatre in the heart of London.
The result is captured on this DVD.

Features:-

Star Wars, the Muppet Show, Beat Street, Tron, Gladiator, Big Trouble In Little China, The Wedding Singer, Rushmore, Back to the Future, Shogun Assassin, Beverly Hills Cop, Class of Nuke 'Em High, Crouching Tiger, Hidden Dragon, Transformers, Scarface, Nas - 'It Ain't Hard To Tell', the original Batman TV series, Enter The Dragon, Eddie Murphy routine, Don't Stop 'Til You Get Enough video, Old School, The Running Man, Texas Chainsaw Massacre, Gremlins, Titan A.E., Ghostbusters, Billie Jean music video, DMC winning sets, Kindergarten Cop, , Deliverance, O Brother, Where Art Thou?, Beavis and Butthead Do America, 

All mixed up and re-scored with plenty of classic funk and soul breaks, 80s classics, Roots Manuva, Dr. Dre, Snoop Dogg, Blackalicious, East Flatbush Project, Theme tunes, some electro, big beat, Chemical Brother's 'Push the Button'.
