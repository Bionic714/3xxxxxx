The Lingus are an indie rock band from Northern Ireland, formed inSeptember 2003.They create an uplifting melodic sound, blending driving guitars,soaring vocals and delicate harmonies.Their latest EP was released in March 2005 to critical acclaim from theNorthern Irish media and received airplay on BBC Radio Ulster, DowntownRadio, Cool FM and Steve Lamacq on BBC 6 Music.The band have played many venues across Northern Ireland and beyond.Live highlights include recent performances at the BelFEST 2005festival and the NI:NG weekend in the Brixton Windmill.Their songs have been featured on various unsigned compilationsincluding PSB 6, released by UK indie label Smalltown America.
