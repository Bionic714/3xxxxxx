A lone fighter, a brash sorcerer, a hot-tempered rogue ?

Three warriors, brought together by an unexplainable force, embark on a fateful journey that will continuously test their extraordinary battle skills.

Use all three to defeat enemies from beyond this world in Demon Stone?, a cinema-style action-adventure game set in the Forgotten Realms®. 



Features  
 
 




Thrilling pick-up-and-play combat: Enjoy hand-to-hand battles and melee brawls with the fighter, ranged and projectile power with the sorcerer, and sexy stealth attacks with the rogue. 
Sprawling story: By N.Y. Times bestselling author R.A. Salvatore. 
Top-notch voice performances: By Patrick Stewart and Michael Clarke Duncan. 
Unique camera angles and video: Delivering an exceptional fantasy-movie experience. 
Beautiful and ominous Forgotten Realms environments: From the Gemspark Mines to the Jungles of Chult to the Underdark and beyond. 
Dungeons & Dragons® creatures: Including Bugbears, Slaadi, and Yuan-tis. 
A host of choices: Expansive range of combo moves, skill upgrades, weapons, armor, and more.
