Judge Dredd: Dredd vs. Death (Xbox) 


Judge Dredd: Dredd Vs. Death is first-person action game with 11 chapters and more than two dozen levels of gameplay. Based on the comic book (which was subsequently made into a movie starring Sly Stallone), Judge Dredd takes place in the far future. Society is on the verge of chaos and only a select group of elite "judges" can keep Mega-City One under control. You take the role of Judge Dredd, the city's top judge who vows to clean up the streets by any means required. A break-out at the city's penitentiary happens, unleashing a group of evil civilians on to the streets, Judge Dredd's plans for steadiness take a fatal twist. Features 50 plus characters including zombies, crooks and demons. Multiplayer allows up to four players in split-screen mode.
