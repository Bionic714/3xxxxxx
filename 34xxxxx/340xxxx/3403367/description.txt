Microsoft Visual Studio Enterprise Architect includes advanced  Visio-based
  modeling solutions for developers. In  addition  to  all  the  features  in
  Microsoft  Office  Visio  Professional  2003,  with  Visio  for  Enterprise
  Architects you can perform round-trip engineering on software with the  UML
  Model diagram, and on databases with the Database, ER, and ORM Source Model
  diagrams.
