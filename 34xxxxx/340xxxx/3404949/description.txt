MorphMan 4.0 is the newest release of the most powerful morphing           
     application for PC. This version introduces layers which allow for         
     creation of sophisticated morphing effects and easier integration          
     with video composition tools. Use full power of fast morphing              
     algorithms, onion skin interface, live preview, vector shape tools,        
     edge detection, and motion estimation for creation of hi-end               
     photo-to-photo and video-to-video morphing.
