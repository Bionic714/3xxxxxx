-=HIM - Wings Of A Butterfly [CD Single Archive]=-

/Main Info
This torrent collects each of the tracks released on the two CD singles for 'HIM - Wings Of A Butterfly' in the UK.

/CD1
Track 1: HIM - Wings Of A Butterfly
Track 2: HIM - Poison Heart

/CD2
Track 1: HIM - Wings Of A Butterfly
Track 2: HIM - And Love Said No (616 Version)
Track 3: HIM - Vampire Heart (Live)

/Misc. Info
According to the sleeve, Vampire Heart (Live) was recorded at the Donnington Download 2005 festival.
Each track was encoded using LAME with the '-b 192' command.
