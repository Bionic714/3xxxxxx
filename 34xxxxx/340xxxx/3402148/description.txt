Terminator 3: The Redemption (Xbox) 


Set inside and outside the events of the movie, Terminator 3: Redemption puts you in the boots of Arnold Schwarzenegger's reprogrammed killing machine. The game features vehicle and character based combat and exploration from a third-person perspective in a gigantic 3D Terminator world -- commandeer vehicles and lay waste to the H/K machines after you as you seek to accomplish your mission: ensure the fate of John Connor at all costs. 


Game Info

Developer Paradigm Entertainment  
Publisher Atari  
Genre Action 
Release Date September 9, 2004  
ESRB Teen for Language, Partial Nudity, Violence
