    notes:

These are the first 3 demo tapes, from Red Warszawa.
They are Recorded from 1987 to 1995, and contains more than 3 hours of
silly rock in the classical not-so-good demo quality.

All tracks are taken from Red Warszawa's own MP3-releases of the tapes,
and has been cut into isolated songs, and the silence between the songs
have been removed.
Sorry, but it can't really get any better with these old tapes as source.

Simply a masterpiece, and a milestone in the musical history of Denmark!
