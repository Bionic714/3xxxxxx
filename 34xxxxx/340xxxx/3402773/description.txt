A rare concertrecording from the german musicprogram "Rockpalast". The concert took place in april, 1980 in Grugahalle as part of the 6. Rockpalast-rocknight. Very good show with a lot af classic Z Z Top tunes.

Musicians:

Billy Gibbons - Gitarre, Gesang
Dusty Hill - Bass, Gesang
Frank Beard - Schlagzeug
