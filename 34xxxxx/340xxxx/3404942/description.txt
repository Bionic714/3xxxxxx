The children begin to tire of Gene as the audition process continues. Gene is invited to perform for a group of local pensioners at Christs Hospital's weekly lunchtime recital, and a KISS tribute band performs for Gene and the school in the theatre.

The final members of the band, a keyboard player and bass guitarist, are picked -- but not all of the children are happy as Gene fails to notice the disappointment of those not picked for the line up.
