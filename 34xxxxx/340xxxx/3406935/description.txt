The Matrix: Path of Neo (Xbox) 


The Matrix: Path of Neo is the follow-up to 2003's hit action videogame based on The Matrix trilogy of films. Confirmed to have been on the drawing board by Atari in June of 2003, further information on the title has yet to surface. Given development times and the expected arrival of next generation consoles in the next two years, however, the game is speculated to see its release sometime in 2005.
