Crime Life: Gang Wars (Xbox) 
Developed by Hothouse Creations for Konami, this turf wars game follows the gangs of Grand Central City, an urban jungle steeped in violence. Detroit rap crew D12 lends its music and likenesses to this brutal game of life on the streets. The game stars Tre, the young leader of the Outlawz, once Grand Central City's most powerful gang. Gamers maneuver Tre through the dangerous streets, using his fists and brains to protect his crew and build his reputation. The player must protect his own people from exploitation and abuse, and defend his crew from the violence and treachery that permeates the society he lives in.
