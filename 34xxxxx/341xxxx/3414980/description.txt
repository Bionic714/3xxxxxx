              Based on the blockbuster online multiplayer war                   
              Battlefield franchise, EA and developer Digital                   
              Illusions are taking the battle to another realm                  
              with a modern-war era edition somewhat similar to                 
              Battlefield 2 for PCs but developed exclusively for               
              console gamers. Players from the United States,                   
              China and the Middle East Coalition will all be                   
              represented in harrowing battles of up to 24                      
              players. Command tanks, choppers and any other of                 
              more than 30 military vehicles, and make use of                   
              more than 70 kinds of weapons (including                          
              heat-seaking missles, laser designated bombs and                  
              other modern military hardware.)
