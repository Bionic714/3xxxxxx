Trace of Genius: 
An Anthology of Music Written or Inspired by Brian Wilson 
(The title was borrowed from a phrase in The Talking Mules/Rev Arthur Belling's song contribution - "Maladies Forlorn") 

The CD features submissions from at 
least four countries on three continents...! 

Frankly, with the amount of passion and talent 
evident on each of the recordings, the resulting 
compilation effortlessly became a truly great album... 

Thank you to everyone that contributed to 
and supported the production of this project... 

Bob Phillips
