Description
Soccer fans will get a chance to revisit some of the most exciting moments in FIFA World Cup History in this three-hour extravaganza. Highlights include impressive plays by star players like David Beckham, Pele, Ronaldo, Zinedine Zinane, Michel Platini, Johan Cruyff, and more. FIFA FEVER lets soccer fans relive the best games, goals, free-kicks, and saves from 17 of the FIFA World Cup finals. Also includeed are key moments from four FIFA Women's World Cups, and highlights from Youth Soccer tournaments.
