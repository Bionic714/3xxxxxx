The Korean girl group S.E.S. was together from 1997 to 2002 The members were: Sea (known as Bada), Eugene (real name, Yoo-Jin Kim) and Shoo. Current ages: Bada, 25; Eugene and Shoo, 24. They received a lifetime achievement award at the 2005 Korean Music Video Festival, held Sunday, November 27 in Seoul. They performed one of their old hits, "Oh My Love".
