Bruce Cockburn (Pronounced "Coburn") is a Canadian folk/rock guitarist and singer/songwriter. He has recorded over 25 albums, and written songs ranging from sophisticated and soulful folk music to jazz-influenced rock to rock and roll. He is a member of the Canadian Music Hall of Fame, the Canadian Broadcast Hall of Fame, and is an Officer in the Order of Canada.
"Anything, Anytime, Anywhere" was released in 2002, and is Cockburns first official greatest hits album.
