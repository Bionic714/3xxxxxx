Firewall Builder (FWBuilder) is a simple tool for building complicated firewall rulesets. You can build rulesets for : IPFW, Iptables, Ipchains, FWSM, ipfilter, PF, PIX and for many OSs like Linux, FreeBSD, CiscoFWSM, MacOSX, OpenBSD, NetBSD, Solaris, and even for Linksys routers. With fwbuilder everything is easyer and faster.
