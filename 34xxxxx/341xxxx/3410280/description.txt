Pac-Man World 3 (Xbox) 


Celebrate Pac-Man's 25th anniversary with the latest in the popular series, Pac-Man World 3. Help Pac-Man uncover Erwin's evil plot and save the real world from the collapsing Spectral world. Fortunately, Pac-Man is not alone this time - Pinky and Clyde, the classic Ghosts, will help him along his journey. Pac-Man World 3 will have an arsenal of power-ups that will let Pac-Man be victorious and save everybody from Erwin's evil plans.
