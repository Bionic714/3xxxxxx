This is the King James Version of the entire Holy Bible read by Alexander Scourby. There is no music in the background or anything else. The Bible is simply read.

The MP3 files in this archive are encoded at the best possible qulity, but still small enough, so that all of the Holy Bible can fit on one CD. The encoding that fit this requirement is 20Kbps 16KHz. Also, the entire KJV bible in word 97 format is in this archive.
