ByteOMeter is a handy utility for monitoring your internet traffic.

It visualizes network activity in real-time with smooth scrolling graphs and shows current upload and download speeds in bytes per second.

It comes with two different graph types, 20 different colour themes and also allows you make your own themes to best suit your desktop.

ByteOMeter enables remote monitoring of your connection so you can check in on it from any other computer on the net through its browser.
