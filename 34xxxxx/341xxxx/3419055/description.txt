Who is Mister PiX?

Mister PiX is a powerful yet simple to use program that will search for pictures on the Internet and in Newsgroups! If you?ve ever tried to find pictures of flowers, birds, girls, stamps...you know how tedious it can be to sift through the vast amount of material unrelated to your search. With Mister PiX you will no longer have to view each and every web page or go through all the messages in Newsgroups one-by-one looking for the pictures they contain. Let Mister PiX do the work for you...!
