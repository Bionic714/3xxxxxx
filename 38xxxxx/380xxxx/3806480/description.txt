Another Life - Memories from Nothing

This is the debut album of this Swedish band from Västervik/Gamleby, the style is Progressive Metal.

The record was told to be released early in 2007 on their label VIC Records

Bandmembers:
Vocals - Andreas Karlsson
Drums - Peter Damin
Guitar - Pär Fransson
Guitar - Fredrik Petterson
Bass - Joakin Diener
Keyboards - Dan Swanö
