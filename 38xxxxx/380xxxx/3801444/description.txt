This study guide has been developed specifically for the Florida Criminal Justice Basic Abilities Tests (CJBAT). Whether you are preparing for the position of law enforcement officer or correctional officer, this guide will provide you with valuable information about the various cognitive abilities upon which you will be tested, the types of questions that you will encounter on the examinations, as well as sample test questions and practice exams to give you a good idea of what to expect when you take the CJBAT. This study guide will also offer you instructions and rules for the day of test administration and proven test-taking strategies to help you do your best.
