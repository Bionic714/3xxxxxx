Program Info: 
http://solveigmm.com/?Products&id=VideoSplitter
Solveig Multimedia Video Splitter is a powerful tool to perform fast AVI, WMV, ASF video trimming tasks without any quality loss. It has intuitive user-friendly interface and allows splitting a video file in few mouse clicks. SolveigMM Video Splitter allows AVI to ASF remultiplexing, WMV and AVI demultiplexing(saving separately video and audio parts) with or without simultaneous trimming.
