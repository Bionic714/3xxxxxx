Asleep In The Park are an awesome Aussie rock band from Melbourne. This is their recent EP which has been described as "Stadium Rock on a Beer Budget!".

The band have released the EP under a creative commons license in order to get their music out to as many people as possible. So please download and spread this as far as you can!

See http://www.asleepinthepark.com/ to find out more about this band.
