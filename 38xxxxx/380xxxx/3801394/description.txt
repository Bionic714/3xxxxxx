Scotflight Scenery for Flight Simulator X: Vol 1 - Highlands & Islands

Looking to get away from big city airports and crowded skies? If so, then the beauty and tranquility of Scotland's Highlands and Islands is the place.

With Scotflight for FSX: Highlands & Islands, the best of this beautiful and remote part of the world is now available in FSX. You can fly from one of the many small commercial airports or island grass strips in the area, from Unst in the far North of Shetland to Islay in the South West, or to the remote isolation of St Kilda heliport far out in the Atlantic.
