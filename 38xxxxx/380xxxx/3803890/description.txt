Darren Silverman, Wayne Lefessier and J.D.McNugent have been best friends since the fifth grade. Enter Judith, a cold-hearted beauty who digs her claws into sweet natured Darren. She snatches him from his friends and even breaks up their cover band. J.D. and Wayne try every imaginable scheme to save Darren - and their friendship - but fail miserably. When they find out Darren has been brainwashed into marrying Judith, the dim - witted duo decide to kidnap her and reunite Darren with his long-lost love before she becomes a nun.

Starring:

Jason Biggs, Steve Zahn, Jack Black and Amanda Peet.
