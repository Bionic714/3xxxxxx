WELCOME TO HAPPYLAND is a strange, comic short film that depicts a near-future where the porcine mascot of a Euro-Disney-style theme park is kidnapped at gun-point by a disaffected, French self-styled ?artiste?.
It was written and directed by Emily Maclean, shot by Darrett Sanders and features the work of actors Christina Nefaris and William Salyers.
