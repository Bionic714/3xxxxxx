Britney Spears Greatest Hits: My Prerogative DVD Video (2004)

Region: Region 1 DVD

Summary
This DVD features every music video Britney Spears has ever filmed up to her 2004 release of her Greatest Hits CD. This DVD also has special features, bloopers, karaoke options and alternate takes of selected videos.
