Great poker run. Lots of girls and boats all partying. Some of the fastest boats in the world from all over the county. Its Mardi Gras on the water at harmon!


The Lake Cumberland Poker Run is a contest involving 200 boats running 100+MPH to five different sites on Lake Cumberland. At each stop the boats pick up a sealed envelope with a playing card inside. At the end of the Run, all cards are turned in and the best hand wins! There are prizes for the top four poker hands. With a Wild party on the water at the end.
