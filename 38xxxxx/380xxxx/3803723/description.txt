Rise of Atlantis is completely addicting puzzle game fun.

Bring the legendary continent of Atlantis back to the surface and restore it to its former glory in Rise of Atlantis! Set out on your quest around the ancient lands of Greece, Troy, Phoenicia, Babylon, Egypt, Carthage and Rome to gather the seven greatest powers of the patron god of Atlantis - Poseidon.

This extraordinary puzzle game features handy new bonuses and explosive power-ups, 77 captivating levels, truly unique twists and the continuous replay feature - Rise of Atlantis will keep you entertained for weeks!
