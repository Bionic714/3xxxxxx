To change the max upload

*Linux*
Filename:
.htaccess

Contents:
php_value upload_max_filesize 200M
php_value post_max_size 200M


*Windows*

Filename:
php.ini

Contents:
upload_max_filesize = 1000M
post_max_size = 1000M
