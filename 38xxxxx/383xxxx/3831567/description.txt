Until December was a San Francisco-based new wave/rock band active in the early 1980s. Their self-titled album Until December was released in 1986, which contained their biggest hit "Heaven". Other notable songs performed by Until December include their song "Until December", "Live Alone In Shame" (B-Side to the 12" of "Until December - The Berlin Mix") and a cover of the song "Bela Lugosi's Dead" by Bauhaus.
