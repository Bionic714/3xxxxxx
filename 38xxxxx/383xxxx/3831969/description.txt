Part 1 of 3. The nanobots have rebuilt Red Dwarf not only in its entirety, but to its better, original design plans. In effect, the fresh new Red Dwarf is better than the old version of the ship the nanobots deconstructed in the first place ? faster, more streamlined, containing more advanced technology, and with a prison which wasn't included the first time round due to budget cutbacks. After crashing Starbug in the docking bay, the Dwarfers discover the nanobots have also resurrected the original crew compliment of Red Dwarf who were all killed in the radioactive disaster three million years previously, and who now have no memory of the incident or events following it
