The 170th album in the everlasing Super Eurobeat series.  This is a two-disc set with full-disc mixes done by DJ Boss (Y&Co.) on the first disc, and DJ ONE (S.G-Sonic) on the second.  Disc 1 is the Legend of Top 50 (the voted top 50 greatest older SEB songs) and Disc 2 is the New Legend of Top 50 (voted top 50 greatest recent songs).
