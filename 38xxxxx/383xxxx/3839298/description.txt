Enjoy stunning scenery in the mountains of Utah on the Heber Valley Railroad, winter on the high desertat the fantastically preserved Nevada Northern Railroad, and heavy, mainline railroading on the Ohio Central System. If you like steam railroading, you'll love the great steam action in this program.

Features:

Enjoy 4-8-4 #6325 as she storms along the Ohio Central mainline

Thrill to the mountain vistas and 2-8-0 #618 on the Heber Valley

Step back in time as 2-8-0 #98 re-creates a mine run on the Nevada Northern


Running time Approx. 90 mins.
