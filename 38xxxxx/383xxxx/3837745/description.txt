Car Alarm 

Kim tries to fix an old car her dad gives to her, but soon hands the job over to the Tweebs for a very long contract. Meanwhile, Shego and Motor Ed join forces to steal Project Kepler from the space center. They modify it and plan to go hypersonic and destroy the Earth. Kim finds her new car unable to keep up with the Kepler and has the Tweebs modify it again. Kim now must jump onto a speeding rocket to save the day.
