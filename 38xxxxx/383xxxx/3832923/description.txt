Magik is the title given to a trance mix-compilation series by Tijs Verwest under his most well known pseudonym DJ Ti?sto. The series, which has had seven installments starting in 1997 and ending in 2001, has been released by Ti?sto on Black Hole Recordings

Installments

There have been seven installments in the Magik series.

Magik One: First Flight
Magik Two: Story of the Fall
Magik Three: Far From Earth
Magik Four: A New Adventure
Magik Five: Heaven Beyond
Magik Six: Live In Amsterdam
Magik Seven: Live in Los Angeles
