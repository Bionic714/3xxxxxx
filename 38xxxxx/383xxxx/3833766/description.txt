Culture Clash was the second album released by Sacred Spirit, a musical project by Austrian musician Claus Zundel. Sacred Spirits music is dominated by a Native American theme. For each Sacred Spirit album sold, a donation is made to the Native American Rights Fund.

This second album is dominated more by a blues singing theme, than by the Sacred Spirit typical Native American theme. With the change of theme the name also change for the american release to Indigo Spirit.
