Gandhi is a multi-award-winning biopic film about the life of Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi, often known as Mahatma Gandhi, who was leader of the nonviolent resistance movement against British colonial rule in India during the first half of the 20th century. The film was directed by Richard Attenborough and stars Ben Kingsley as Gandhi, a role for which he won the Academy Award for Best Actor. It was an international co-production between production companies in India and the UK.
