
Cities Divided 3 August 2007

India and Pakistan 07 

Navdip Dhariwal talks to the winners and 
the losers from the current economic boom 
in India. Delhi is experiencing unprecedented 
prosperity, while Kolkata is still having 
to support sixteen thousand people in one of 
the oldest refugee camps in the world. The 
effects of Partition on the economies of 
India and Pakistan are still being felt.

Part of the India and Pakistan 07 season.
