Experience fast paced hunting action in pursuit of trophy bucks in 24 states and provinces across North America. Cabelas Trophy Bucks starts you at the most critical and exciting part of each mission where you will be immediately presented with a challenge. You will hunt 11 species of trophy deer including Whitetail, Rocky Mountain Mule Deer, Sitka, Desert Mule Deer, Columbian Black Tail and more. Experience all new hunts for waterfowl, upland birds, small games, and varmints including calling in turkeys from a blind, scaring up pheasants with a hunting dog and long range varmint hunting. Compete in 24 shooting competitions based on real-life tournaments. Your well-honed hunting instinct, keen observation and quick decision making are essential on your quest to bag that trophy buck of a lifetime!
