Explore the world of Super Collapse 3 In this exciting new Quest, your journey will take you through ten different realms with many stops along the way. Visit the shop in each town to purchase powerup items to help you in your quest. For a distraction, take in ten entertaining new games in the casino of each town. You can play the classic Collapse games that you know and love, along with three new challenging modes. Challenge yourself to the new Slider, Continuous and Countdown modes. How long can you survive.
