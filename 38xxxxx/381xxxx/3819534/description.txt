Jet Li's final movie in which he will be the main character. The movie is about Huo Yin Jia (Master YinJia) and his quest to become powerful. Striving to become the number 1 fighter in all of Tiensen, YinJia loses touch with his roots, loses friends, family, and becomes nothing but a shell of a man, good for nothing more than fighting. The real struggle begins when he must fight not for glory, but to retake everything he lost because of his ignorange in the past.
