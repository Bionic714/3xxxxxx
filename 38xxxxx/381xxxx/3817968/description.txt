Hail to the Thief (subtitled The Gloaming) is the sixth studio album by English rock band Radiohead, released on 9 June 2003 in the United Kingdom and June 10, 2003, in the United States. It debuted at number one in the UK and at number three in the United States. As of January 2007, Hail to the Thief has sold 932,000 units in the U.S.
