Star Wars - The Empire Strikes Backyard...

... is an action parody, full length film, the first Star Wars fanfilm produced by Backyard Productions (www.backyardproductions.co.uk) in the UK. This is the full film, and extras (trailers, mockumentary etc). Originally premiered in 1999, and since receiving critial acclaim at TheForce.Net Star Wars Fan site (http://www.theforce.net/fanfilms/).

Enjoy!!
