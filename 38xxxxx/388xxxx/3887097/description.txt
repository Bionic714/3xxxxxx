Electroteque
Fossil Fueled Transportation
Musictrade Field Recordings Vol I
Musictrade release MTFR001
Released: November 3, 2007
320 kbps CBR / Lame HVBR mp3 encoding


With this release Musictrade will start a series of themed field recording collections made by members of Musictrade.

The recordings in this collection cover the travels of Dan Rossi aka Electroteque over the years anywhere from cities to remote forest regions of Australia in his search of Tasmanian Devils in the wild. Everything in this collection is somehow connected to fossil fueled transportation and machinery in our everyday lives - the industrial aspects, the ambient noises and the soundscapes associated with it.
