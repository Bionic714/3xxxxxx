Heart of Glass_1976_West Germany .
Herz aus Glas (Original Foreign title).

Written, directed and produced by Werner Herzog.
Herbert Achternbush-----Book Author.
Jorg Schmidt-Reitwein-----Cinematographer.
Popol Vuh-----Composer.

A very strange and controversial film, really one of a kind. It's enough to say that all the actors but one played their roles under hypnosis.
Some may find it mesmerizing and haunting, while others cannot sit till the end. Wish you to be among the former. Enjoy!
