Another fine American tradition finds its way to the PlayStation 2. In Lowrider you will be able to cruise the streets in a lowrider car, customize it, race with it, and sweeten its design. Drive 18 different vehicles and use 18 categories of car upgrades. Custom paint your car with a custom paint tool. Install major hydraulics and big sound systems. Compete against a friend in tournaments, including Hop, Bed-Dance and Unlimited. Lowride the streets of San Diego, San Francisco, Los Angeles and Las Vegas.
