Description: Explorer: Ultimate Cat [TV-PG] 

Equipped with 3-inch-long canines and retractable claws like switchblades, big cats are among the fiercest and most fascinating beasts on the planet capable of taking down fleet-footed antelope or two-ton elephants. Explorer crawls through African grasslands and Indian swamps to track and reveal the ultimate engineering and ambush tactics of lions, tigers and leopards.
