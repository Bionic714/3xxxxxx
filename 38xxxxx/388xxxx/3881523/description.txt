Zwyrd's Homegrown Cuts - JG Thirlwell/Foetus Projects


Here are my "Fan Mixes" of tracks/CDs by Industrial/Noise/Avantgarde genius J.G. Thirlwell aka Jim Foetus aka Clint Ruin aka Frank Want aka DJ Otefsu aka Manorexia aka Steroid Maximus aka Baby Zizanie (with Jim Coleman) aka Wiseblood (with Roli Mosiman).

All elements/samples are sampled from the CDs/LPs, with some live and additional stuff from different sources.

All files are in lossless FLAC format, although not all source samples are lossless.
These tracks are also available as 128k mp3s on my homepage: http://weird.gmxhome.de/PAGES/Sounds.htm
