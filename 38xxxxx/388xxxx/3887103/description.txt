                       Description
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Molecular biology, stem cells, genomics: these are the cutting-edge fields 
where the controversial work is happening. In The Man with the Golden Cells, 
something as sci-fi as the cryogenic preservation of cell lines is just another 
FedEx delivery for the people who drive this industry.

The Man with the Golden Cells travels the troubling road from John Moore's case 
through to today's vibrant global trade of the human body: in Singapore, in 
Boston, in Paris, in Toronto, the very genes that make up the human body are 
there to be bought and sold.
