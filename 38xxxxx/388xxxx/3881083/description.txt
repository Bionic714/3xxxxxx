SS-Lieutenant Colonel Helmut von Schreader concieves an unusual plan to escape the aftermath of the Second World War by disguising himself as a Jewish Concentration Camp victim. Under the alias of Ben Grossman, von Screader is liberated from the camps and immigrates to Palastine where he joins the Zionist movement. Twenty five years later, living as a hero of Isreal and a General in the Isreali Army, the underground SS orginization ODESSA locates Von Schreader and orders to him to again serve the cause of the Third Reich
