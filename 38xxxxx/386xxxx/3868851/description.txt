Contains material (interviews, shows and road trips) dated back from 1988 with old guitarist Hillel Slovak and drummer Jack Irons, till the latest show in the Netherlands at Goffertpark Nijmegen June 26, 2007.

Has Dutch subtitles and commentary, nonetheless very fun to watch for everyone everywhere!
