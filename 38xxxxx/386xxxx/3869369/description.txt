"RACERS REVEALED" is an Access All Areas pass to the official racing teams for The Amazing Race Asia season 2.

The overwhelming success of the first season of The Race, which was seen, heard and discussed in newspapers, magazines, on radio, TV, in Internet forums and around water coolers across Asia inspired the producers to create this Exclusive Special.

The world's most exciting reality TV race in Asia last year drew an estimated viewing audience in excess of 15 million per episode, from all over the region - Singapore, Japan, Bangladesh, Brunei, China, Hong Kong, India, Indonesia, Macau, Malaysia, Mongolia, Nepal, Pakistan, Papua New Guinea, Philippines, South Korea, Sri Lanka, Taiwan, Thailand and Vietnam.
