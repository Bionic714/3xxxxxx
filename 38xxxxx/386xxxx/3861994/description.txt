Directed by Kiefer Sutherland, this 1997 film features Sutherland, Mykelti Williamson, Vincent Gallo, Kevin Pollack, Kim Dickens and Grace Phillips.
It was Sutherland's directorial debut.
A drug deal goes wrong causing a foursome, led by a lunatic killer, to go on the run. The police and mob are on their heels, while the bandits have to deal with hostages they've taken.
