Soundtrack to the drama thriller 'Miami Vice' (2006), Michael Mann's film version of the seminal 80s police series. Also includes the instrumental version of the Jay-Z and Linkin Park collaboration 'Numb/Encore', used in the theatrical trailers.

All tracks in MP3 format, mostly at 320 k.b.p.s.. Please note that this compilation varies from the official C.D. soundtrack release.
