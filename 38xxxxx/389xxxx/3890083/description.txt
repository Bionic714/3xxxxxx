This DVD contains just about every skratch technique known and many you've never heard or seen before! All the basics to new intricate upside down, diagonal combos in different patterns variations, rhythms, tempos, flavors and other fun stuff! All vinyl manipulations in easy to find alphabetical order!


Plus many guest skratchers like Tyra from Saigon, Presto-1, world champion DJ Rafik, Earwaxxx, Z-trip, Primo, world champion DJ 8-Ball and many others!
