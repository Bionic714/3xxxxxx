Line Kill Spirits aka Line Shooting Spirits for Windows 98(SE), Me, 2000, and XP. Released December 2005 by GPSC. It is a doujin fighting game. The text in this game is partially in Japanese, but it is still very playable.

Use WinRAR to extract the files and Alcohol 120% to mount and install.
