Synopsis
--------
Revisit the classic routines of comedian-pianist Victor Borge in highlights from his years of comical television. Includes "Introducing Mozart," "My Favorite Barber," "Introducing Marylyn Mulvey," "Aria From 'Rigor Mortis' by Joe Green," and "The Timid Page Turner."
