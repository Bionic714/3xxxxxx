Texture patch for Resident Evil 4 (PC) created by Albert Marin Garau. It's compatible with all versions of the game. Since the original textures suck you NEED this patch, it makes the game look a lot better.

It's really easy to install, too. Just open Resident_Evil_4_Pc_-_Text1.exe and extract the files in your Resident Evil 4/Biohazard 4 folder.
