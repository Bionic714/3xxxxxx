Live recording of Implacable performing "Master" at the NordicFest 2007 http://nordicfest.no metal festival at SubScene, Oslo, Norway on 2007-11-09.

together with: Mortification, In Vain, My Silent Wake, Once Dead, Barren Cross, Darkwater, The Weakening, Strangeland, Strength of Purpose and Soniferous.



Implacable is a brutal DOOM metal band hailing from western Norway, haunting the underground with low frequency noises since 1995, contrasting the subsonic death metal grind and downtuned guitars distorted beyond redemption with soaring organs and angelic female vocals.



This release is made and supported 100% by the band, please support underground DOOM metal by distributing it as much as possible, download our other releases and get your bones massaged by attending our concerts!

Filmed by Marek Stawiasz & Implacable, edited by Implacable.
