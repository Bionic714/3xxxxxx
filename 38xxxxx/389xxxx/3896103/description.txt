    The  posthumously  released  King of  Hearts  collects  a handful  of
    Orbison's  final  vocal tracks  along  with a  few  demos and  non-LP
    singles, including  the Jeff  Lynne-produced "Heartbreak  Radio." The
    highlight, however, is an amazing duet of "Crying" recorded with k.d.
    lang.

    http://royorbison.musiccitynetworks.com
    http://www.myspace.com/royorbison

    Tracks:

    01. You're The One
    02. Heartbreak Radio
    03. We'll Take The Night
    04. Crying (Duet With K.D. Lang)
    05. After The Love Has Gone
    06. Love In Time
    07. I Drove All Night
    08. Wild Hearts Run Out Of Time
    09. Coming Home
    10. Careless Heart (Original Demo)
    11. Life Fades Away (Bonus Track)
