NoIntro patch by Vincent Viau (BurningSheep)
Special thanks to NetSpider for the Idx decrpytion logic

1) Go to your hg:l data folder (usually C:\Program Files\Flagship Studios\Hellgate London\Data)
2) Backup these files:
	hellgate_movies000.idx
	hellgate_movieslow000.idx
	hellgate_movieshigh000.idx
3) Replace the old files with the ones in the archive.

This will remove the intro videos without affecting the rest of the game's videos.
