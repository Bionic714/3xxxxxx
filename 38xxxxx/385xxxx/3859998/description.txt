Supperclub Presents One World was Mixed and Compiled by Resident DJ'S Jurr, Roelove and Alan Luna with Highly Inspirational Input from their Colleagues from Rome, San Francisco and Nomads, Resulting in an Even More Cosmopolitan Vibe. Listening to the First Disc, "la Salle Neige", is Like Taking a Warm Bath. It's Comforting Yet Stimulating. The Beautiful Sounds Are Mesmerising and Indulgent. The Second Disc of the Set, "la Bar Rouge", Offers Cutting Edge Tracks that Will Make You Shake Your Booty Until You Are Out of Breath. Both Discs Include Many Unreleased Tracks from the Likes of Water Feat. Fay Lovski, Tevlon (An Alan Luna Production) DJ Roelove and Barth Hendrix, Stefania Dipierro. They Are Accompanied by V.i.p.'s Like Electro Coco, Lazybatusu, Thievery Corporation, Angel Tears, Moby, DJ Sneak and Kings of Tomorrow.
