Writers (WGA):

Jeb Stuart (screenplay) ...

Genre:
Action / Drama / Mystery / Thriller

Plot Summary: Bobby Earl is facing the electric chair for the murder of a young girl. Eight years after the crime he calls in Paul Armstrong, a professor of law, to help prove his innocence. Armstrong quickly uncovers some overlooked evidence to present to the local police, but they aren't interested - Bobby was their killer.
