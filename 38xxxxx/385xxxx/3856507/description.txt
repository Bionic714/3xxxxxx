Release Notes                                                                
2minds are shauli and miki ifrah two brothers from northern israel. shauli    
is 26 years old and miki is 16 years old. they started to create trance music 
in late 2004. before the 2minds project was formed shauli was named d.j hobit 
and played as a d.j in many nature partyis in isreal and also in clubs like  
the "red-room" one of the biggest clubs in israel. there music style moves    
from full-on to psychodelic. 2minds tracks came out on sevrel compilations    
in sweden,india,mexico and more. they are currently working on the new album.
