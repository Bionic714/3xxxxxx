PES 2008 Final Widescreen Resolution fix By Z

- Extract the resolution you wante (xxxxsettings.exe) to your PES2008 directory 
and then delete the origeonal one and rename the one you put in there.
then run it, choose the resolution and your done :).


For example
"if you want 1360x768"
copy '1360settings' to pes folder, delete 'settings' then rename '1360settings' to 'settings'.


PES Rules

1152settings = 1152x864
1360settings = 1360x768
1440settings = 1440x900
