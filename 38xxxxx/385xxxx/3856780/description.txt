Excellent Science Fiction Movie From the early 80\'s.  

The stage is set for havoc and terror when a twelve-man research team finds an 

alien being that has fallen from the sky and has been buried for over 100,000 

years. 
 
Genres: Science Fiction/Fantasy, Suspense/Horror and Thriller 
 
Running Time: 1 hr. 48 min. 
 
Release Date: June 25th, 1982 
 
MPAA Rating: R 
 
Distributors: Universal Pictures 

 
Starring: Kurt Russell, Wilford Brimley, T. K. Carter, David Clennon, Keith David  

Directed by: John Carpenter
