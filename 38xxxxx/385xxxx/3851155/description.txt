DVD made up of videos from You Tube.  It is designed to be a simple thing that you can hand to someone so that they can watch it and find out about Ron Paul.  You can download the iso, image it to a dvd disc and make copies.

This version has been updated from the previous version and includes the following videos:

A New Hope
Stop Dreaming
PBS Interview
Candidates at Google
One Voice

Total run time is approx 1hr 44 min.
