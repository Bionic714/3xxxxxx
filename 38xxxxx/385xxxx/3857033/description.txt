The first part in the new Epic British Sci-Fi Fan Film, 'A Tale of Two Galaxies'. Set in both the universes of Star Trek and Star Wars, this action packed fan film plays homage to those science fiction classics in a fantastic new adventure. 


Produced by a young crew in England, Strait Jacket Productions have produced an amazing film on next to nothing. This short film showcases their CGI, editing, set design and even tribbble wrangling skills.
