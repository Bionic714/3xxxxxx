* What's a ppApp? - 
The idea behind a ppApp is to seperate your apps from your OS as much as possiable while keeping them easily integrated/accessable to use. 
They exists on any fixed HDD and when you run the Generator tool or use installer to add new ones, it'll create Shortcuts, File types, register dll/ocx and run a script. 

* What are the Benefits of ppApps? - 
- No extra time needed after OS install installing/updating and configuring each app. 
- Each app will be Updated (even after install). 
- Each will have the settings always set how you like them. 
- No reason to worry yourself with backing things up before you format/install your PC. 
- Each app will be pre cracked and ready to run. 
- No messing about finding where on your pc you've stored the latest version and the working Crack. 
- No Sorting the Start Menu every OS you install. 
- Really easy to create a ppApp to include with your ppApps installer 
- Migrating your apps to another persons Install/collection only involves copying a single folder. 
- Less time Defragging as the apps never move location.
