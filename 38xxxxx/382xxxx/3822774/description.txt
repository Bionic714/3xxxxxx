Feyruna - Fairy Forest

Fly across the beautiful landscapes of the Fairy Forest and use your mighty fairy magic spells to defeat all minions of evil! Friendly creatures like Butterflies, Ghost Lights and enchanted Glow Worms are there to help you drive away the dark forces. You will be rewarded with enchanted artifacts and will unlock three magical mini-games as well! There are 60 amazing levels, all leading to a fantastical ending! So, begin today to restore Feyruna to the peaceful world that it once was!
