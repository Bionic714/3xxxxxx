Scavenger

One human, one droid and a Scavenger class space ship! Choose from 5 distinctive game modes to test your piloting skills as you overcome the Icarus's decaying gravity fields. Rotate, thrust and shoot your way through 80 sectors, collecting treasures, battling aliens and unlocking your way to freedom. Scavenger blends action based gameplay with carefully designed, challenging levels to create a shoot 'em up experience like no other!
