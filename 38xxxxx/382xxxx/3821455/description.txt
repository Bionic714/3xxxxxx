 Snowy's back and the hunt for more treasure is on! Tired of his restaurant business, he dives into a new journey in an ancient castle of mystery! The castle is full of treasures as well as all kinds of foes. Snowy will have to make his way through 85 adventure-filled levels from castle environs and luxurious halls to secret vaults! But he has lots of new tricks up his sleeve! Join him on his latest quest in Snowy Treasure Hunter 3!
