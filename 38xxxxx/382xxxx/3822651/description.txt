Plot: Local store owners seek the help of Jackie to protect them from a greedy Chinese extortion ring. Tempers reach the boiling point when Jackie discovers that the crime kingpin behind the extortion scheme killed his father years before. Jackie seeks revenge. 
 
Overview: Jackie Chan made his leading role debut in 1974 with a film called "Cub Tiger From Kwantung". The production company soon went out of business and Jackie's film debut was put on the shelf, until he hit international superstardom. After re-shooting new footage with a double, the film was finally released as "Master With Cracked Fingers".
