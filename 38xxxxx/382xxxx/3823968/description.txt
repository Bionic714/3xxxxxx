Real Germany 2 

(North-East Germany)
Enjoy visual flying in MS Flight Simulator 2004. This add-on brings the original scenery of Berlin/Brandenburg, Saxony-Anhalt and northern Saxony, an area of 52.8000 qkm, right into the flight simulator. 

This area is being displayed with completely new terrain textures in a resolution of 4m/Pixel, based on aerial photographs. Navigate by visual landmarks such as forests, lakes, rivers and motorways. An elevation model with a resolution of 80m is also included in this package. Real Germany 2 is compatible to the German Airports sceneries.
