Published in Nick Mag Presents Avatar - The Last Airbender.

3 short full-color comics that happen between Book 2 and Book 3.

1 - The Bridge - The plan to capture a Fire Nation ship is put to the test at a new bridge installed at The Serpent's Pass.

2 - It's Only Natural - Sokka tries to teach the Earth King's bear how to survive in the wild.

3 - Going Home Again - In order to convince Zuko to return to the Fire Nation with her, Azula schemes to have him and Mai fall in love.

Also invludes a couple bonus comics that are rather amusing.
