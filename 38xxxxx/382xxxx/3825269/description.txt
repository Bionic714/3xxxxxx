Acclaimed tenor Luciano Pavarotti prepares for retirement in this intimate BBC documentary narrated by Sir Ian McKellen.  As his career draws to a close and he tours the world giving his final performances, the seventy-year-old Pavarotti reflects on his life and times and just what means to be one of the world's greatest tenors. ~ Jason Buchanan, All Movie Guide

Includes clips of Pavarotti's earliest performances of Tosca, La Boheme, Rigoletto, and more.

Run Time: 88 minutes
