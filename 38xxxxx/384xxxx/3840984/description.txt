if.... is a 1968 cult film by British director Lindsay Anderson about an armed rebellion at a British public school. The film is associated with the 1960s counterculture movement; it was filmed at the time of the student uprisings in Paris in May 1968, and it includes controversial statements such as "There's no such thing as a wrong war. Violence and revolution are the only pure acts" and features surrealist sequences throughout. On its release in the UK it was given an X certificate
