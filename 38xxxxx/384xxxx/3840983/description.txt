A update patch named F1 Challenge 2007 was created by fans of EA F1 Challenge 
99-02. 

THIS IS A FULL GAME WITH THIS PATCH and you can play without the older version 
installed.

This game contains:
- 2007 season informations and settings
- 2007 17 tracks and 3 bonus tracks
- 2007 cars and racers with real strenght
- New real tracks of China, Barcelona and Fuji
- New sounds
- Better graphics
- New tires

This game contains a different language. 
To change to ENGLISH, just erase Options\Game.dic and Options\Telem.dic files.
