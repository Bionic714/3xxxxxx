   Amost unusual softcore movie. A Brazilian production, this version is english-dubbed. The star is a fine young wench named Xuxa who later went on to host a very popular childrens show in Brazil. When she became very famous and wealthy she bought up all the rights to the movie and had it pulled from distribution. It was available for a while on VHS but has never been released on DVD. It probably never will be and the few VHS copies are almost impossible to find. It is a serious well-produced piece of erotica with a most unusual theme. I will try to keep seeding this for all serious collectors.   theempty1
