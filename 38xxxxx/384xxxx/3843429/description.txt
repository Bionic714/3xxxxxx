Three astronauts flying the X-20 DynaSoar into space for the first time disappear from radar on a test flight, then reappear.However, all is not as it seems upon their return to Earth. Forbes tells his story to Gart, who says he does not know any person named Harrington. Then Forbes looks in the mirror, sees no reflection and runs out of the room.
