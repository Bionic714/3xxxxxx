  for his unrivaled brilliance and obsession with Thermodynamics, Advanced
  Propulsion, and Quantum Electrodynamics (QED). Despite his increasing

  Physicist of a classified Time Travel research program. Over a decade later,
  Krone's program had successfully developed the world's first time-traveling
  suit technology. However, as the program's equally secretive decision-makers
  demanded this technology be adapted for military applications, Krone's
  feelings of self-entitlement and anger grew. He secretly desired a new

  experienced without outside interference. The combination of Krone's mind

  existence - an existence where a man of science could finally operate
  without restrictions and do so within a place where science itself was
  properly respected regardless of the effects on humanity. It is now up to
  you to trace Krone's last known appearances and stop his tyrannical rule
  over Alpha District. In a twisted world where scientific accomplishments
  reign, it's up to you to don the Beta Suit and Master Time to Become the
