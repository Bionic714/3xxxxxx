In Search of Noah's Ark (1976)

A Sunn Classics investigation into the theory that Mt. Ararat in Turkey is the final resting place of Noah's Ark.  Most of the evidence presented is either anecdotal or obsolete, having been dis-proven years ago. 

Much of the counter evidence they don't tell you can be found here:
