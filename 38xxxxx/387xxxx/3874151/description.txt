[Snowboard 2008] CAPITA's First Kiss

Be a part of the rule-breaking CAPiTA team with The CAPiTA Movie DVD. 
Their first feature film promises to be nothing but another creative 
release of emotion and truth, with snowboarding as the common thread 
that links the artists, poets, free-thinkers and seekers at CAPiTA. 
Become a CAPiTAlist, and immerse yourself in this super scary 
corporation with the CAPiTA Movie DVD.

An artistic snowboarding film conceptualized, filmed, produced, and
edited by CAPiTA team riders and friends on borrowed cameras, and 
discount film, FIRST KISS showcases true D.I.Y. spirit through the 
personalities and riding of some of the industry's most unique, 
creative, and talented snowboarders.

Featuring: TJ Schneider, Tyler Lepore, Dustin Craven, Dan Brisse, 
Jonas Carlson, Scott Shaw, Andrew Burns, Phil Jacques and more...
