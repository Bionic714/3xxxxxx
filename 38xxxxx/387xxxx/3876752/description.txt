Information
------------------------------

Two documentaries about african meerkats. First broadcasted in 1987 and the follow up in 1996.


1)  United 
The tale of a gang of meerkats who, despite a hot and hostile landscape, manage to devise a way of coming out on top - by team work.

2)  Divided 
Following up from Meerkats United 17 years ago,this programme again looks at the life of the Meerkat.Tonight's film is set in idyllic African landscape and follows the Addo Gang(a family of Meerkats) and their dealings with various animal neighbours.
