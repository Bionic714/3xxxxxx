A DVD-full of Swedish music. As much a gift to foreign friends as an experiment to make sure non-ASCII filenames do survive through bittorrent. Enjoy!

The songs are rar'ed in order to make it less of a mess for your bittorrent-client - your unarchiving program must be able to deal with UTF-8-encoded filenames and an environment configured to use unicode (under unix that's LANG="en_US.UTF-8").

Watch everything tagged with "NonASCII" for the future cultural benefit of Unicode and please comment how you liked the music and whether it worked for you.
