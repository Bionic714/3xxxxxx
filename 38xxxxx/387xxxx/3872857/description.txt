Introspective artist Blake is buckling under the weight of fame, professional obligations and a mounting feeling of isolation. Many people are looking for Blake - his friends, his managers and record label, even a private detective - but he does not want to be found. In the haze of his final hours, Blake will seek release...

Very much a companion piece to the experimental feel of Gerry, Gus Van Sant looks at the mythological dimensions to the final moments of rock stars like Kurt Cobain.

Anamorphic, 5.1 AAC Dolby Surround, Swedish Subtitles
