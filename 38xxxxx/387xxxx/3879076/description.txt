Rick Wakeman - The Mellotron Story

Keyboard veteran Rick Wakeman narrates the history of what Jean Michel Jarre
called "The craziest instrument you could imagine" - the Mellotron. Featuring plenty of sound clips including the first ever 'Tron on record and interviews
with Paul McCartney and other musicians such as Mike Pinder, who worked at the Mellotron factory before going full time with The Moody Blues.

This upload is ripped from a cassette recording of the original BBC radio
broadcast from January 1998. Sound quality is not hi-fi but clear and listenable.
