 Game Notes:
 ~~~~~~~~~~
 Corsairs Gold comprises the complete version of Corsairs and the Corsairs
 Extension "The New Conquerors". Launch yourself on a thrilling quest to
 the four corners of the globe, with new andventures in every port...
 By force of arms or by profitable trade, accumulate wealth for yourself 
 and for your country. 

 A strong fighter and subtle negotiator, your sense of strategy will be 
 your only weapon to forge your reputation. Maybe you will become even more 
 admired than your King and more feared than the most ferocious pirates!
 
 Features:
 ~~~~~~~~
 * More than 50 missions
 * 4 Complete Campaigns
 * Play as 2 New Nations (Spain & Holland) 
 * 21 New Missions
 * 5 Open-Ended Missions
 * Total 37 types of units, including aircraft, boats and heroes
 * About 17 types of buildings in each race
 * About 10 absolutely different upgrades for each race
 * Ability to control units of different races during one mission
 * Scenario and unit editor
