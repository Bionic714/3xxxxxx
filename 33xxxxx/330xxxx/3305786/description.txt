Hey Arnold! The Movie (Hey Arnold! Filmen)

Year: 2002
Runtime: 1hour 13min (approx.)
Director: Tuck Tucker
Screen: Widescreen 1.78:1 Anamorphic

Review: 
A heartless industrialist has an evil plan: to level Arnold's neighbourhood and build a huge shopping mall. With time running out, someone needs to save the day. And that someone is Arnold. With Helga, his pigtailed nemesis who secretly loves him, and Gerald, his cool best friend, Arnold takes a stand to proves the real meaning of progress - finding new joys in the old neighborhood he calls "home".

Languages: English, Swedish, Norwegian, Danish.
Subtitles: English, Swedish, Norwegian, Danish, Icelandic, Finnish, Dutch, Spanish, Italian.
