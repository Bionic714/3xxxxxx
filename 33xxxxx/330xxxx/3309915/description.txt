When the motion picture studios had their own symphony orchestras to record movie soundtracks the greatest instrumentalist in the world were in Los Angeles, California, USA. This is a recording from that era with some of the world's greatest French Horn virtuosi...The Horn Club of Los Angeles. Performers include George Hyde, Vincent de Rubertis, Gene Sherry, Leon Donfray, Jack Cave, Sinclair Lott, Richard Perissi, Wendell Hoss, Herman Lebow, Arthur Frantz, Hy Markowitz, Charles Peel, Gale Robinson, James Decker, Joe Eger, Alan Robinson, and Vincent de Rosa.
