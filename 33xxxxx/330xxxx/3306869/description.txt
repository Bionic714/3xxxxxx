Command & Conquer Generals
for Mac OS, version 1.0


System Requirements

Mac OS X 10.2.6 or later
PowerPC G4/G5, 1 GHz or faster
256 MB of RAM
1.6 GB free disk space
3D Graphics Acceleration required (ATI Radeon 7500/nVidia GeForce 2 or better, 32 MB of VRAM)
DVD drive required to install and play
Network play Mac to Mac only, GameRanger supported
