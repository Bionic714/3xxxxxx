PocketMusic Player Bundle will turn your Pocket PC device into a powerful MP3, OGG, WMA player.

PocketMusic Bundle is compatible with Pocket PC 2002 and Windows Mobile 2003 devices. Also there is a special support for Windows Mobile 2003 Second Edition (native hi-resolution devices and different screen orientation support).

Features:
- WMA, OGG, MP1, MP2, MP3, Audible files support
- Alarm feature - wake up with your favorite music!
- Feature of playing music files from the local network folders
- HTTP streaming audio support for MP3 and Ogg internet radio broadcast. Title streaming also supported (SHOUTcast, IceCast).
- Audible audio books support
- Extended player control with hardware buttons
