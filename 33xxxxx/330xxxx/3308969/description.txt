LANGUAGES: ENGLISH, SPANISH, FRENCH.

As seasoned Coast Guard veteran Tom Hansen, you've seen the sea swallow plenty of victims. But when you board a seemingly abandoned ship during a ferocious Arctic storm, nothing can prepare you for the horrors lurking beneath the ship's bloodstained decks.

•••  Fight your way through a blood-soaked nightmare straight out of a horror movie.

••• Confront fiendishly intelligent and unrelentingly hostile enemies as the ship's deck lurches constantly under your feet.

•••  Fight for your life with a staggering array of weaponry. 

•••  Take advantage of the ship's unpredictable environment to lure your enemies into fatal traps.
