LANGUAGES: ENGLISH, SPANISH, GERMAN, FRENCH, ITALIAN 

Driver 3 is an action-packed driving adventure game that recreates the 
excitement of a Hollywood blockbuster and plays like an interactive movie. 
Driver 3 stays true to its roots, incorporating the cinematic gameplay and 
gritty street crime of its predecessors with ground breaking graphics and 
three mammoth wide open city environments.
