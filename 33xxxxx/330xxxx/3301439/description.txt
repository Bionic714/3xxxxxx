Ported from the arcade, Virtua Tennis has realistic 3D environments and fluid animations. Featured are 8 of the top internationally ranked men's players. Volley with three of your friends on hard, clay, grass, and carpet court surfaces.

Features:
# Featuring Venus and Serena Williams
# Smash, lob, and volley in Tournament, Exhibition, and World Circuit Modes
# Select from 16 players, both male and female, each with unique strengths and weaknesses
# Create and develop custom players in World Circuit Mode
# Compete on clay, grass, carpet, asphalt, and concrete courts
# Singles, Doubles, and Mixed Doubles matches
# Link up to 4 players in singles or doubles play
