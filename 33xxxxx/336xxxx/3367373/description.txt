This was voted 'best Judge Dredd story' in a 2002 issue of 'Judge Dredd magazine'.

This is a classic story about America, a country whose values and geography have long gone, since the development of the mega-cities, and the 'instant justice' dispensed by the Judges. This is also the story of one young girl who still believes in those values and fights for them. Events change the courseof her life, which lead her to inevitable conflict with the Judges...
