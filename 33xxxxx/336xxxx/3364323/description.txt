Lyricsgrabber is a windows software program designed to find and download the lyrics (song text) of your choice. When using this program you will have access to over a million different lyrics collected from various up to date sources on the internet. Lyricsgrabber can be configured in many different ways to fit your needs, it can even automatically retrieve the lyrics from the very popular music player Winamp. The wide variety of useful settings along with the slim and simple interface, is what makes it a one of a kind software who few dare to compete with. Lyricsgrabber will easily find almost any lyric for almost any song within a second of pressing the search button.
