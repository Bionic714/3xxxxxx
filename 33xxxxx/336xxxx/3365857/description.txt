The New Wave Of British Heavy Metal Revisited ?
In 1990, Lars put out a sampler NWOBHM bands that influenced him and his music. The double album was called: 'NWOBHM - Revisited' 
Diamond Head, Sweet Savage, Saxon, White Spirit, Raven, Paralex, Def Leppard, Weapon, Samson, Hollow Ground, Girlschool, Witchfynde, AIIZ, Witchfinder General, Black Axe, Iron Maiden, Jaguar, Tygers Of Pan Tang, Gaskin, Sledgehammer, Venom, Angelwitch, Trespass, Holocaust, Vardis, Blitzkrieg, Dragster, Fist.
