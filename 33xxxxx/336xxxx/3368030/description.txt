Martin Mystere is a story based on Dorian Grays recurring dreams: everlasting youth and immortality.

Prof. Eulemberg and important researcher of M.I.T., was found murdered.
Unknown to anybody somebody is working on an incredible machine, capable of changing the worlds
destiny...Somewhere, an old sick and cirrhosis tramp reveals he is "I'm Martin Mystere", but of course
this is impossible!

Time is running out and there isn't a rational explanation, this could be the most incredible and
dangerous adventure for Martin Mystere!
