This third-person action-shooter features all-new brutal disarm moves, a spherical slow-motion diving system, and an arsenal of deadly weapons, including Shadow himself.

An investigation into a Grant City judge's kidnapping unfolds a labyrinth of crime, corruption, and betrayal ... 

 Features  
 
 

More Shadow: Now you can use the K-9 Shadow to attack enemies in real time. 
More disarms: More than 28 brutal disarm moves means there are even more ways to "take out the trash." 
Spherical slow-motion diving: New freedom means Jack can shift in all directions, from one enemy target to the next, taking them all out in dramatic slow motion. 
Huge new arsenal of weapons: Including shotguns, UZIs, rocket launchers, swords, and meat cleavers to name just a few. 
360-degree brawling: Featuring a multitude of kicks, punches, throws, and other moves.
