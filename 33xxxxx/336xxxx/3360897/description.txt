  Big Mutha Truckers 2 begins with Ma Jackson being taken into police custody
  for tax evasion. The only way she can win her case is by hiring Cousin Jacob
  the most "fancy-talkin' legal fella" this side of Booger's Canyon. Players
  will be challenged to locate six jurors and raise the cash needed to bribe
  them to let Ma off by trucking, trading, wheeling and dealing. 

  Features: 

  - Arcade style gameplay allows players to take any path they wish to their
    desired locations, avoid enemy attacks, successfully negotiate hostile 
    terrain and make their deliveries in time. 

  - Players can choose from four "unique" characters including Bobbie-Sue,
    Cletus, Earl and Rawkus each with their own attributes and customized 
    rigs. 

  - Receive warnings and updates from other drives using a dynamic CB system. 

  - Haul various types of cargo that includes anything from diamonds to hogs
    to earn money.
 
  - Battle bikers, avoid rock falls and out-run the corrupt police force.
 
  - Customize your rig with upgrades from the garage. 

  - Players can throw the weight of their massive 18-wheeler around to
    discover new routes, hidden items and smash cars. 

  - Tune in to unique "Truckin" radio stations as you drive, including 
    official licensed music from Puddle of Mudd, John Fogerty, and Willie
    Nelson.
