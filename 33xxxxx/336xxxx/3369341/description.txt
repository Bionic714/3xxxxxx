27 clips featuring various matches and goals of thirty times champion of Bulgaria CSKA Sofia in Cup of European Champions, Cup Winners' Cup and UEFA Cup since early sixties till now.

some of these are:
both undecided 1/2 finals in CEC 1967 against Inter Milan that lead to a 3rd match in Bologne
dethroning of current european champion Ajax Amsterdam in 1973. an overtime 2-0 in Sofia.
dethroning of current european champion Liverpool in 1982. another overtime 2-0 win in Sofia
all 4 home goals of 1/2 final victory in 1982 CEC against Bayern Munich.
goals from both 1989 semi-finals against Barcelona in 1989.
and another famous win against Juventus, this time in 1994, one of the worst years for the club.
