Here comes almost everything released by the Swedish medieval music ensemble Vox Vulgaris:

* The album "The Shape of Medieval Music to Come" (2003)

* Live recordings from Medeltidsveckan, Visby, 2002

* Live recordings from Lye Church, Medeltidsveckan 2004 (including previously unreleased songs like "Esek bayrami" and "Gloria")

* A hard-to-describe remix of the hit "Rokatanc"!
