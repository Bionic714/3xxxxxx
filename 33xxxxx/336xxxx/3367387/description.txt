Perhaps some of you have heard or seen last years documentary Dig! about the relationship/rivalry between the Dandy Warhols and The Brian Jonestown Massacre.  BJTM formed around 1990 in San Francisco and have had a rotating cast of members all centering around Anton Newcombe, their incredibly talented and sometimes irritating frontman.  This is 20 of my favorite tracks of theirs, all pretty rockin' with a bit of a 60's pyschedelic, drone rock sound.
