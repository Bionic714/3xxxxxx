'Super Position' is the debut album of Artax.

Artax consists of Lars Bergval, born in a small town in mid Sweden 1981. He has several releases for labels such as Spiral Trax, Transient Records, Third Eye Records, Yellow Sunshine Recordings, Stone Age Records, Digital Psionics, Turbo Trance Records, SunDance Records and Glowing Flame.

The album contains 9 unreleased tracks, all of them are very specific and unique.
