A Collection of Mods for the wonderful game Uplink: Hacker Elite

FBI Mod: Adds an FBI database with jailbreaks and Physical Security.
CMD Mod: Adds a Central Medical Database... Hack someone to death!
Simply Uplink: Mod based on ease of use. Makes the game a MUCH smoother experience.
Uplink, The Mod: Community based mod. Agent names are Uplink Forum usernames, etc.
uMP3: Adds an MP3 player to Uplink
Uplink_S01: Increased InterNIC security/missions, GCD improved. Similar to FBImod.

Plus many new LANs, gateways and Themes! (Includes Patch 1.31 and a trainer)
