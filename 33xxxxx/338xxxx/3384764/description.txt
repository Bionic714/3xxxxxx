Games World Series 2 BTV Episode 05

This is the Fifth episode of BTV which is the Reviews, Features & Cheats program of Games World in Series 2. In this episode the stuio is taken over by Anti-Games protesters and Barry & Violet Blade take a visit to Virgin USA to take a look at upcoming games including Demolition Man on the 3DO & Greed on the Megadrive.

Starring:
Alex Verrey as Big Boy Barry
& David Walliams as Leslie Luncheonmeat.

The show was recorded long ago in 1994 and is reflected in the quality, you have been warned. :)
