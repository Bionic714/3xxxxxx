Game Master, one of the best UK TV Video Games shows of the early 90s. 

This is the 5th episode from Season 1 of Games Master, the show it self is a mixed bag of Reviews, Features and Games playing challenges, consoles that were being played at the time were, Super Nintendo, Mega Drive, Amiga, CDI, Jaguar etc. 

Show Cast
---------
Presenter: Dominic Diamond
Games Master: Patrick Moore


Season 1 of Games Master was aired in the UK in 1992.
