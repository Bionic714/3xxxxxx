CrackSearcher v1.0

Crack Searcher is a handy little app that searches various sites for any cracks, patches or serials that you may need. It is a standalone app and requires no installation, and just remember that any cracks you download will be saved to a folder named 'Cracks' that is created inside the folder you ran the program from (ie C:Program FilesCrack SearcherCracks). Crack Searcher has a simple and easy to use interface and saves you the time and effort of navigating through numerous crack sites or the hassle of installing search bars.

Enjoy....
