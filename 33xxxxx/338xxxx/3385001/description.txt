Altom are Alexis Cornet and Thomas Dubreuil from Paris.

They started making music 4 years ago and their first album was called 'Hologram', released in summer 2002 on Neurobiotic Records.

Since then they played many parties all around the world including big events in Mexico, Brasil and Japan and they released tracks on all the most important labels around such as Tip, Spirit Zone, Spun, Maia, Liquid and Solid and of course Neurobiotic and they gained some impressive feedbacks and undoubtful reputation.

Now available their second album called 'Groove control', which promises to set the new boundaries of psychedelic trance.
