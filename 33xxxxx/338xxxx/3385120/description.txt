He produced some major hits and remixes that swept the trance skies like a comet.

Astrix is Avi Shmailov from Israel.

He started making trance music as a teenager. Then he changed direction and was newly discovered when he collaborated with Alien Project on his debut album Midnight Sun, released in 2001. Since then he released uite a few tracks with Alien Project, Atomic Pulse, Xerox, omestic, Nomad and on his own.
