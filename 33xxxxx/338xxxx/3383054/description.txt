The full text and pictures of the award winning '10 days in September' article published in the Washington Post.
This Article vividly describes the events taking place in the Whitehouse after the 911 attacks on New York.
Compiled from a series of intervies with members of the Bush administration, by author Bob Woodward, the article gives a great insight into the political atmosphere of the time and the response by Bush's Cabinet.
A fantastic read!

Also includes some great follow-up articles, discussions with the author and interview transcripts.
