Christoph Drouillet has been at the forefront of the psy-trance scene since the beginning.

His project Transwave with Dado (Synthetic) released 3 albums and 7 singles and enjoyed huge success as one of the most high powered projects of the time.

In 1998 Christoph formed 3DVision records in Paris and then as well his new project Absolum. With this project he earned a reputation as creating huge dancefloor tracks that push the sonic and production boundaries.
