 Features  
 
 

Turok: Evolution raises the bar on first-person shooters with groundbreaking gameplay, "living" interactive environments, intense dinosaur hunting, advanced enemy AI, and stunning graphics. 
Flight sequences enable players to soar through the skies on a winged Pteranodon. 
Destructible objects and environments: shoot down trees and collapse massive rock spires onto enemies. 
Groundbreaking Squad Dynamics System (S.D.S.) creates incredibly realistic enemy and allied squad AI. 
All-new weapons including Variable Payload Cruise Missiles, Gravity Disrupter Beams, and the Swarm Bore, each with unique traits and damage. 
32 different types of prehistoric and indigenous creatures come to life with frightening realism. 
Stealth and action based gameplay directed by context-sensitive, mission-based sequences. 
Fantastic multiplayer action developed by a dedicated team of designers, programmers and artists. 
Lush, living jungle environments: plants move as dinosaurs brush against them and trees sway in the wind.
