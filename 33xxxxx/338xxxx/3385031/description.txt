This is truly a unique fusion of electronic styles.

Artifakt Album 'Artifakts' the first album relases for Timeocde and S.A. trance.

12 tracks of finely crafted psychedelic music with influences from techno, electro, classical, kwaito, and hip-hop (featuring Denver from Moodphase5).

'Artifakts' represents the cutting edge of the South African sound, an exceptional balance of full- on dance floor tracks and more abstract listening material.
