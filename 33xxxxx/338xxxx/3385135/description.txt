Xmurki (2005)
Russian Premiere: 26.05.2005
Genre: Comedy/Thriller
Director: Aleksei Balabanov

Translation

This new film from one of the leading Russian directors, Aleksei Balabanov,
returns us to the mid 90's. This film is an instant classic. A suitcase of
heroin causes a loud bloody mess to erupt in a provincial town at the heart
of Russia. No one is spared, no one! Drama reminiscent of the director's
highly acclaimed "Brother 1 and 2" is crafted by the director into a finely
woven black comedy. 

Please check out http://www.zhmurki.ru/ for more info! The beautiful
