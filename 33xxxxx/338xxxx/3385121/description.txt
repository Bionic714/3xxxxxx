Astrix is Avi Shmailov from Israel, one of the biggest headliners in the trance scene.

After a year and a half of work, Astrix finished his last touches on his brand new second album - 'Artcore'.

This album approaches a wider audience with various styles of Trance: psychedelic, uplifting, rock'ish and ambient.

It is a mature album with a high level of production.

The album includes a collaboration track with Israels leading trance act Infected Mushroom!
