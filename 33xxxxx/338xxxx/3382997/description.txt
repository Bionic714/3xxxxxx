Part 5: The Plains Part I - All Our Relations
Centuries before the age of the horse, the Crow, Comanche, Sioux, Kiowa, and Arapaho roamed the endless grasslands of the Great Plains. The Buffalo people lived and died by the sacredness of the bison, hunted on foot with the aid of dogs, and maintained a respectful harmony between man and earth. Hear of their stories preserved in petroglyphs and rock and cave etchings.
