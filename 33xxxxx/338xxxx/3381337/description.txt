A Sound Of Thunder 
Starring: Edward Burns, Ben Kingsley, Catherine McCormack, Corey Johnson, Jemima Rooper 
Directed by: Peter Hyams
Release date: 15-Sept

A story about a game hunter (Burns) who goes on a time-traveling safari owned and operated by Kingsley's character to hunt dinosaurs in the prehistoric era. When he kills a butterfly, he unknowingly sets off a chain reaction that will erase humanity from existence. A team of experts must return back in time and replace the butterfly
