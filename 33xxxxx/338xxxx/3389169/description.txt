The Golden Book Of Chemistry Experiments
How To Set Up A Home Laboratory - Over 200 Simple Experiments
by Robert Brent, 1960.

This fantastic little book provides a priceless education in the field of Chemistry for both teenagers and adults. No technical experience required. It is fully illustrated and packed with information that enables the average person to manufacture many important and useful chemicals at home.

Trust me, even if you're not interested in Chemistry, just download it and give it to your kids. They don't make books like this anymore.
