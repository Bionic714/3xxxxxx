The most influential rock instrumental guitarists of the past decade Joe Satriani, Eric Johnson and Steve Vai joined forces as G3 for a U.S. tour in October of 1996. It was an unprecedented event, as never before had these three premier guitarists appeared and toured together. G3 Live In Concert was recorded on November 2nd, 1996, at the Northrop Auditorium in Minneapolis, MN. Capturing the blistering performance of each band from the tour, the DVD features select performances of all three guitarists, as well as the evening?s final where Joe Satriani, Eric Johnson, and Steve Vai played together on stage for the first time.
