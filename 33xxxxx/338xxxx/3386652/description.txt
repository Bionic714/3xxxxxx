With New NetOp Remote Control v8.0 you can easily reach any Windows, Mac OS X, Linux, Sun Solaris, CE device or legacy OS/2 and DOS PC from your desktop, Pocket PC, Internet Browser, or now, even a USB Flash Drive! View the remote PC's screen, control its keyboard and mouse, synchronize files, inventory its hardware and software, launch applications or chat with someone at the remote PC -- just as if you were seated at that computer.

NetOp Remote Control is the only remote control application available to offer the NetOp Security Server, NetOp Gateway Server, and NetOp Name Server modules. Each module is designed to provide system administrators with a highly advanced means of configuring the security, communication, and access to the NetOp Guests and Hosts on their network.
