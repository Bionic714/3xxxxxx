Four albums and Two EPs from the groundbreaking (and now sadly ended) Swedish Hardcore band Breach.

Albums:
Friction (1994)
It's Me God (1997)
Venom (1999)
Kollapse (2001)

EPs:
Old Songs Vs. New Beats (1996)
Godbox (2000)

If you like it, go buy these CDs. It's nothing without the real deal.
