2 complete albums by a great European band.  Includes "Falling On Deaf Ears" (2002, Deep Elm Records), and "Temper the Wind to the Shorn Lamb" (2003 Sally Forth Records).  Also included are 2 bonus tracks, "Unsuitable for Any Melody But Yours" (from Deep Elm Unreleased Vol. 1) and "Racing the Mosaic" (from The Emo Diaries Chapter 7: Me Against the World).
