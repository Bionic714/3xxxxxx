Hitlerjunge Quex (1933)

This german film tells the story of a boy at odds with his abusive and unemployed communist father. At a communist camp, the boy is disturbed by his comrades and joins the national socialists. Ultimately a group of Left-wing radicals hunts him down. Based on K.A. Schenzinger's novel. In German WITHOUT English subtitles.
