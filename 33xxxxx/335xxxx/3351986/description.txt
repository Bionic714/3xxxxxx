Road to Jenin
2003 Documentary
Directed by Pierre Rehov
English, Arabic, Hebrew and French with English subtitles
52 minutes

Documents the media-inflamed, widely-acknowledged myth of a massacre in Jenin during Israel's invasion of the West Bank in 2002.  Images, footage, and interviews with both soldiers and Palestininans expose the purpose of inventing the story, as well as the consequences of the media hysteria.
