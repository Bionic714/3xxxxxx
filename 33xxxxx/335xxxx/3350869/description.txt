WORMS Blast
--------------------

Worms Blast is a totally different type of Worms game than you might be used to.

Features:
60 wild and varied missions using oodles of diverse gameplay features.
8 captivating multiplayer modes for playing against either a human or AI. 
Tutorial section to train and charm even the shyest beginner. 
10 addictive gamemodes drenched in design magic to keep you coming back for more. 
9 outrageous characters to choose from, each with their own set of skills. 
Jam packed with over 20 special weapons, utilities and gadgets.
