Description:
Mc Det has been involved with the scene since day dot. Afters years and years of effortless hard work, tearing up dancefloors across the UK and abroad, Det touches down with a new concept in an album format. It features his own smasher ‘Flava 4 Raver’ and also includes new and older material featuring vocals from the scenes leading Mc’s.
It shows just how well the MC can complement the beats and it’s sure to put a smile on your face.

Times Two Recordings finally present their highly anticapated compilation album, ‘Knights of the Mc’s’.
Original long standing veteran Mc Det steps up alongside MIC legends Fats, Dynamite, Shabba D, Fearless, Ragga Twins, IC3 and Skibadee. Brockie, Hype, Ed Solo, Sylo and Probe and Sketch and Code provide the beats.

Tracks include Hype’s all time classic ‘Peace, Love and Unity’ (Ft Mc Fat’s), Brockie’s ‘My Echo’ (Ft Mc Det), Sketch and Codes ‘You Dun Know’ and exclusives like ‘My name’ by Mc Dynamite and Shabba and Dets ‘Say What Your Sayin’.
