Episode 6 : 4th October 2004.
On the back of his success with seminal urban hip-hop outfit, Raspberry Cordial, John travels to New York City and auditions for the world famous Harlem Gospel Choir... but will his rendition of Amazing Grace make the grade? Back in Australia, John attempts to broker a peace deal between prominent Australian Palestinian and Israeli activists and uses an unlikely celebrity as the mediator. Finally, buoyed on by rumours of vampires in Malawi's parliament, John Travels to Canberra to test the vampire-ness of Australian politicians.
