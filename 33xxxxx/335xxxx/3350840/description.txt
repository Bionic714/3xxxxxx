Real Alternative is a codec designed for playing RMVB files, which normally require Real Player, with any video player.
With Real Alternative instaklled you will be able to play RealPlayer files with your regular media player.

It supports the following file formats:

    * RealAudio (.ra .rpm)
    * RealMedia (.rm .ram .rmvb .rpx .smi .smil)
    * RealText (.rt)
    * ReadPix (.rp)
    * RealMedia content embedded in webpages

Enjoy!
