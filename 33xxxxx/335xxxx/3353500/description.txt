Exit Strategy, version .9
Derek Arndt, BatteryAcid.org (derek@batteryacid.org)

Finally, it happened, you have become God. Before you get a chance to wallow in your own greatness you realize people are quickly killing themselves upon hearing the news. These are your people, you have little choice now but to use your power to catch and save your people, one at a time.

With some impressive physics and frenzy gameplay you'll be finding yourself playing this game again and again!
