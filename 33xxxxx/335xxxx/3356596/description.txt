Recently, the world has experienced some unusual and destructive weather. The media blame global warming, but influential voices around the world are expressing scepticism about the link between the burning of fossil fuels and climate change. By placing recent climatic history in the context of 4 billion years on Earth, The Day the Oceans Boiled examines the real strength of the scientific arguements behind the global warming hypothesis; and investigates just how great the risk of a flip to an entirely new, and possibly uninhabitable climate really is.
