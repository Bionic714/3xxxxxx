Kawabe Chieco is best known for her roles of Osaka Naru in PGSM and Sailor Mercury in SeraMyu.

The following is the music video to her smash single, "Be your girl" from her debut album, "Brilliance." The song was used as the ending theme to the Anime, "Elfen Lied." Chieco has a very versatile voice and she gets to really rock out in this song.
