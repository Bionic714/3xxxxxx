Format: Divx
Original: All that she wants by Ace of Base
Covered by: WIZO (Sindelfingen, Germany)
Year: Early 1990s
Cost to make: 1 crate of beer (made by some friends of wizo of a recently founded regional tv broadcaster)
Other:
WIZOs version of the song was not only a chartsuccess in scandinavia, it was also the only one ever approved by Ace of Base themselves. 
It was later sold to Polydor in Hamburg for lots of money, which was later invested into a bus, which was used to travel to even more concerts.
