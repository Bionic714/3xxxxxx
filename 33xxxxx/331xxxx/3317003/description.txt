OnyX is a utility of maintenance, of optimization and personalization for Mac OS X. It also makes it possible to configure certain hidden parameters of Finder, Dock, Safari, to remove a certain number of files and folders that become cumbersome, preview the different logs and CrashReporter, and more...

Product Requirements:
•Mac OS X 10.3(Panther) or higher for v1.4.0(Tiger) or higher
•Mac OS X 10.2.x(Jaguar) for v1.3.1
•BSD subsystem installed (in any case)
