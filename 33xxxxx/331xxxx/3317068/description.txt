<p><i><b>A Arte do Violão</b></i> is a series of 26 radio programs originally broadcast by <b>Radio Cultura FM</b> a station in São Paulo, Brazil.  Hosted by internationally acclaimed Brazilian guitarist <b>Fábio Zanon</b> it offers a sophisticated overview of the world of <b>Classical Guitar</b> from  <b>Tarrega</b> to the <b>Assads</b>. 
</p>

<p>This is CD #4 of a set of four.  It contains MP3 versions of programs 22-26 along with transcripts and further information on the series.</p>

<p>All audio presentations are in  <b><i>Portuguese</b></i>.
