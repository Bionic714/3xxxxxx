Xilisoft Audio Converter

The audio converter provides an easy and completed way to convert between all of the popular audio formats.

Features:

* Convert between MP3, M4A, WAV, WMA, MP4, MP2, VQF, APE, OGG, AAC and FLAC

* Convert AVI, MPEG, WMV, ASF to MP3, WAV, WMA, MP4, M4A, MP2, VQF, APE, OGG, AAC and FLAC;

* Support batch conversion;

* Copy ID3 tag between audio formats (reserve ID3 tag during conversion);

* When converting to MP3, bitrate, VBR or CBR, stereo or mono are selectable;

* You can select the compression method, sample rate, bitrate, stereo or mono when converting to WAV;

* Allow users to select whether delete the original files.
