alliances in a ruthless quest for Confederate gold. Leone is sometimes 
underrated as a director, but the excellent resolution on this digital 
video disc should enhance appreciation of his considerable photographic 
talent and gorgeous widescreen compositions. Ennio Morricone's jokey 
score is justifiably famous. The digital video disc includes about a 
quarter-hour of footage not seen in the original release.
