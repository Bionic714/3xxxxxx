dvdSanta is an All-in-One software that helps you copy, convert and create DVD movies. It can convert video files (include DVD videos) into DVD format, can turn your digital photos into DVD movies with motions and background music, can transfer your digital camcorder tapes directly into DVD video, can burn DVD videos onto DVD-R, DVD-RW, DVD+R or DVD+RW discs. It's extremely easy to use and lightning fast.
