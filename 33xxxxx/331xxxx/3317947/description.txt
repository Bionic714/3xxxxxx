The Day Today (19 Jan-23 Feb 1994) 

A fine satire spoofing current affairs TV programmes and featuring a team relatively 
new to television. Both the radio and TV shows were produced by whizkid Armando Iannucci 
and featured a number of recurring characters, most notably the sports correspondent 
Alan Partridge, later to be granted his own TV show.
