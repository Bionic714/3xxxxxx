          Game Infos:

          Drake is an intense third-person action-shooter set in the
          comic-book-styled, supernatural backdrop of Hong Kong and 
          serving up big doses of double-fisted shooting, fire 
          breathing and Kung-Fu fighting. Gamers are thrust into the
          role of lead character, Drake, a member of the 99 Dragons
          Clan, who has vowed to avenge the murder of his gang and
          recover a stolen ancient artefact. This powerful artefact
          has the ability to trap the souls of the living and 
          resurrect those of the dead, holding the potential to become
          the ultimate weapon of evil. Brought back from the dead to
          right these wrongs, Drake carries out his mission with an
          arsenal of supernatural weapons, with martial arts and with
          firearms galore.
