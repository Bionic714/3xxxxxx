                                 Release Notes
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
An expanded reissue of Soft Black Stars, CURRENT 93's most haunting, personal, 
and enduring album. Originally releaseed on Durtro in 1998, the album was 
recorded live in a small cottage in Ireland with DAVID TIBET on vocals, MAJA 
ELLIOTT on piano, and PETR VASTL on ghost violin; MICHAEL CASHMORE and STEVEN 
STAPLETON (NURSE WITH WOUND) were also present. Includes all the tracks from the 
original CD release, as well as the alternate mix of "Chewing On Shadows" that 
was used on the double-LP version, plus a previously unreleased acoustic version 
of the same track.
