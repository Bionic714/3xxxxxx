This HBO Original Comedy Series follows the adventures of the young & famous Vincent Chase and his three friends from back home in Queens, NY. Drama is Vince's step-brother, and he is forever trying to use this family tie to become a star himself. Turtle is simply enjoying the plush life they lead in Vince's over-sized Los Angeles mansion. Eric remains a true friend to Vince, looking out for his friend wherever he can. Meanwhile, Vince's agent, Ari, is realizing that in order to look out for the best interest of his big star (and his own credibility), he must maintain a good relationship with all four friends.
