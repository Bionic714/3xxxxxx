BRAGUDA takes human form and chances upon the old lady who is the keeper of the White Gem. BRAGUDA recognizes her even in her old age. It is AIO!

BRAGUDA takes AIO to her lair, and a showdown between them proves too much for the aged AIO.

BRAGUDA decides to keep AIO hostage, in order to lure DARNA to her.

AIO wonders how DARNA will come, when NARDA has already returned to her the White Gem.

Meanwhile, VALENTINA has tricked LENLEN into believing they are friends. VALENTINA plans to use her to find out the human identity of DARNA!
