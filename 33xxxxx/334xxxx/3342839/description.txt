Comedy
Set in the 1930's, an honest, goodhearted man is forced to turn to a life of crime to finance his neurotic mother's skyrocketing medical bills.
Cast: Michael Keaton, Joe Piscopo, Marilu Henner, Maureen Stapleton, Peter Boyle 
Cameo Roles: Dom DeLuise, Danny DeVito, Ray Walston, Dick Butkus, Alan Hale Jr.
