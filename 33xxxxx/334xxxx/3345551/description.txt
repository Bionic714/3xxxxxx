Total Commander is a file manager replacement for Windows.
 Total Commander supports ZIP, ARJ, LHA, RAR, UC2, TAR, GZ, CAB, ACE, 
 internal ZIP packer, enhanced search, quick view, drag & drop from & 
 to explorer/desktop, ftp-client, direct access to the Network Neighbor-hood,
 directory history + hotlist, UUencode/decode, split/combine, compare files, 
 synchronize dirs, copy and pack in background, image viewer, 
 parallel port PC to PC connection, multi - rename tool.
