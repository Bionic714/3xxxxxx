The new Enemies of the Imperium and Armies of the Imperium Interactive army lists help you choose and design official Codex army lists. Developed from the popular Space Marine Interactive Army list, the new Interactive army lists now include far more armies to select from and an easier to use interface.
The new Interactive army lists are split in to two volumes, Enemies of the Imperium and Armies of the Imperium.
