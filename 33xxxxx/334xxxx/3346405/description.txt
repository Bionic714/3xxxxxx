~~~~ The fun continues....[VOL. 2] ~~~~
-------------------------------------------
Thanks for downloading my second Eurobeat Counterattack megamix.  After the success of my first megamix (still available for download - link can be found at http://www.bahamutforever.net in the "Eurobeat Nation" forum), I aimed to make this mix even more polished with more interesting transitions.  I also tried to use some of my favourite lesser-known Eurobeat tracks (such as "GONNA BE THE ONE" and "LISTEN TO MY MISTERY") as I still feel there are so many great Eurobeat tracks beyond the SEB series and Initial D that people should get the chance to hear.  You will also notice the intro that I compiled for this megamix - the background music was taken from "Future Girls" by Smile.dk with the robotic voice strands from the start of "Rockey Dance" by Eurobeat Girls.  Anyway, that's enough talk for now - I hope you enjoy this 2nd megamix as much as the first, and rest assured that preparations for Counterattack 3 are well underway!
