Would you like to be able to compress an entire 9GB DVD to 1 single 

file about 1GB-3GB (depending on the DVD and your settings) in size, 

keeping menus, bonus features, multiple audio and subtitle tracks as 

well as video Angles? Well students from the university of Aarhus, 

Danmark and St. Petersburg State Electrotechnical University "LETI", 

Russia have released a new Compression format specifically geared 

towards the compression of complete DVDs without loss of any features 

or noticeable quality called ratDVD.
