ARGUMENTO: Nueva York. Año 2020. Tres de los cuatro jinetes del apocalipsis ya han azotado la Tierra, y el cuarto está a punto de hacerlo. La protagonista Angie Prophet, mitad humana mitad ángel, tendrá que salvar a la humanidad en este juego de acción en primera persona.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 PsychoToxic places gamers in the shoes of heroine Angie Prophet
 who, unbeknownst to her, must save mankind from impending nuclear
 Armageddon. The year is 2022, the place is New York City and as
 Angie's role as savior unfolds so too does her understanding of
 why she is the only one that stands in the way of the Fourth
 Horseman of the Apocalypse and the destruction of civilization.
 Throughout the game, Angie continues to discover a series of
 unique abilities, such as invisibility, the slowing of time and
 the infiltration of other's dreams, all of which aid her progress.



 :: FEATURES ::

 + Highly interactive environment with nearly everything being
   destructible

 + Features 29 richly detailed levels, eight of which are unique
   "dream stages" that take Angie inside the dreams (and
   nightmares) of other characters in the game

 + Graphics powered by the Vulpine Vision Graphics Engine

 + An impressive array of weapons, ranging from small arms to
   large ADWs (area damage weaponry)

 + Supernatural powers, including invisibility, slowing of time
   and mental telepathy

 + A high tension story that holds players' attention from
   beginning to end
