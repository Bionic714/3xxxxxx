  1. Unrar.
  2. Replace gta_sa.exe with our fixed.
  3. Open gta_sa.reg with a Text-editor
  4. Replace the String 'Documents and Settings' with the one on your system
     language.
  5. Run gta_sa.reg.
  6. You may need to restart your machine.
  7. Fire up the game.
