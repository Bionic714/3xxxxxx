Two people start new jobs on the same day -- with the unusual goal of getting fired by 3 pm. The contestants try their best to drive their unsuspecting coworkers and bosses crazy while hidden cameras capture it all. The contestant who gets fired closest to the 3:00 pm deadline, but does not go past it, is declared the winner, and gets the prize of $25,000.
