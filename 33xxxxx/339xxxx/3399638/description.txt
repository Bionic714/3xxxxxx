defective films 2005 release - DERELICTICA

After a successful season last year with their ?Promo Copy? dvd, The Defective films crew is back at it again for 2005. This years film "DERELICTICA" features a lot of the riders from last year with a few fresh faces mixed in. Riders included are Lauri Heiskari, Chris Engelsman, Eddie Wall, Hampus Mosesson, Jakob Wilhelmson, Joni Malmi, Alex Auchu, Mikey Rencz, Simon Chamberlain, Travis Kennedy, Jake Blauvelt, Reno, Andreas Wiig, Fredu Sirvio and Mitch Reed.
