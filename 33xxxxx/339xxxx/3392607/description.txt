A Swedish shortfilm with a nice ending. 
With english subtitles.

A macabre story about a waitress (Regina Lund) who day-dreams about becoming a big Hollywood star. But the reality of working at a small diner where the guests and the Chef mistreat her, is far from her dreams. One day she is blamed for letting a rat into the restaurant and is forced to clean the whole place from top to bottom. She decides that enough is enough and plans her revenge...

Today's Special - Death!

Runtime: 12 min
