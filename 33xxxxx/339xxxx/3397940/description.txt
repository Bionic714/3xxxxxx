Shattered Union (Xbox) 


Set atop the smoldering ruins of a fictional United States torn apart by civil war, Shattered Union is an action-packed turn-based strategy game for console and PC. Gamers can play through the single player campaign to reunite the country by force or online in one-on-one matches.
