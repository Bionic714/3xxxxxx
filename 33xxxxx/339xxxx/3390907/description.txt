  Brothers In Arms Road to Hill 30? introduced you to the men of the 101st 
  Airborne and established the franchise as the most authentic WWII shooter 
  ever made. Brothers In Arms Earned In Blood? delivers even more of the 
  authenticity, squad combat, and compelling story the franchise is famous 
  for and includes many exciting new missions and features designed to make 
  the game even more intense. 

  Brothers In Arms Earned In Blood is the next chapter of the true story of 
  the 101st Airborne. As Sgt. Joe "Red" Hartsock, you must travel beyond Hill 30 
  and lead your squad of paratroopers through some of the most intense missions 
  of WWII as you fight for the liberation of all of Normandy.
