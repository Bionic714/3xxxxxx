To install and play F.E.A.R.:
1)First mount image with deamon latest version(3.47) , then install as you would any other game. 
put in serial and finish instalation. 
2)Then  download virtualCD install this. right click on the icon in taskbar and click hide virtual Drives. 
3)Go to the F.E.A.R. icon on your desk, right click on the icon -> propitets -> Compability -> Make this program Compability to Windows 2000.
4)Then mount image in deamon and play.
