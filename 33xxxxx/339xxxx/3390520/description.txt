This contains four compilation CDs of Pokémon music. These four CDs get together the majority of vocal songs produced for Pokémon, and in general give a great overview of Pokémon music. The CDs included are...

Pocket Monsters Movie Anime Theme Song Collection 1998-2003
Pocket Monsters TV Anime Theme Song Collection 1997-2003
Pokemon Best Collection
Pokemon Best Vocal Collection 2
