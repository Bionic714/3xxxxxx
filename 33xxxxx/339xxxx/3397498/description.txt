Degrassi: The Next Generation Episode 5X03 "Death Of A Disco Dancer"
First aired: 10/3/2005
Guest star: Julie Khaner (Unviersity Lady), Marc Donato (Derek)

Paige has her entire future planned out; she is going to the best university in Canada, Banting, and she will live with Matt and everything will be perfect. That is until Matt comes back changed and soon things go from bad to worse when Alex convinces her to get stoned with her and soon Paige's entire life starts to unravel. Meanwhile Jimmy is proud to be the new basketball coach but a new member, Derek., poses trouble.
