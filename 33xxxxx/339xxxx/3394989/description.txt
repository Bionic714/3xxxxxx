Full Version of VSO DivxToDVD 1.99.20.60 Latest.

DivxToDVD is a 1 click solution to convert and burn your movie files to a compatible
DVD.                                                                                
Supports most popular format such Divx, Xvid, MOV, VOB, Mpeg, Mpeg4, AVI, WMV, DV   
and stream formats.                                                                   
It converts your files into a compliant DVD Video set of files and burns it on a    
DVD media.                                                                          
The aspect ratio can be automatically selected or forced to a specific format.       
The program works for NTSC and PAL video formats and creates chapters automatically.
Multiple audio tracks are supported.                                                
The version 2 uses a completely rewritten interface with subtitles support and a lot
of new settings.
