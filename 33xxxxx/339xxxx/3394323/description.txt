(sv)
60-talspop så som Residents minns den.
Trots all nazi-referenser så har skivan
ingenting med nazism att göra.
Vad Residents i själva verket vill säga
med allt det här är att musikindustrin
var lika fascistisk på 60-talet som den är idag.

Ett annat syfte med denna skiva kan vara att ta
ner musiken på jorden och satirisera över likheter
mellan att dyrka popstjärnor som gudar och blint följa
politiska ledare.

(eng)
60's pop as the Residents remember it.
Despite all the nazi imagery, this album
has nothing to do with nazism.
What the Residents are saying is simply that
the music industry were just as fascist in
the 60's as it is today.

Another possibly purpose of this album might be
to bring the music down to earth and to satirize
the similarities between worshipping popstars as
gods and to blindly follow political leaders.

As a bonus, I've included a cover of Rolling Stones'
old hit "Satisfaction", which appear on some reissues
of this album.
