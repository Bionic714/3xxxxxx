This is the DVD video supplement disc to the double Super Audio CD album of Roger Waters' opera in three acts, CA IRA.

The DVD is titled The Making of CA IRA, directed by Adrian Maben, featuring a behind-the-scenes look at Roger Waters opera, from concerpt to completion, together with interview footage of Roger Waters, the soloists, the musicians and the cast of CA IRA.

Uncompressed 1:1 dvd copy. Running time is about 53 minutes.
