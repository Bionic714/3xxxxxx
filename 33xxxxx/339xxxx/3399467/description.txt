Audio Editor Gold v7.5.7
Audio Editor Gold is a visual audio editor and recorder software solution, which supports many advanced and powerful operations with audio data.

You can use Audio Editor Gold to record your own music, voice, or other audio, edit it, mix it with other audio or musical parts, add effects like Reverb, Chorus, and Echo, and master it so that you can burn it to a CD, post it on the World Wide Web, or e-mail it.
