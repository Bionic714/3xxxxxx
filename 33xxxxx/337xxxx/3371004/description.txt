  Winter X Games Snowboarding 2002 - The most authentic snowboarding 
  game returns with more arcade fun and snowboarding action! Try to win
  a gold medal in on of four authentic Winter X Games events, or go
  anywhere in the free ride mode, where you can go for big kickers and
  twisted rails to your heart's content.

 

  This game gives snowboard fans the best mix of authentic X Games 
  snowboarding with arcade style action. Pull your best tricks in 30 
  levels with more arcade style kickers and twisted rails in different
  locations around the world. Live the life of a pro boarder with the
  super deep Snowboarder mode where you have to learn new tricks to get
  sponsored and earn the big bucks in your quest to compete at the X
  Games. 

 

  The game features 13 pro boarders, including Peter Line, Todd
  Richards, Shannon Dunn, Danny Kass, JP Walker, and Tina Basich. Most
  of the snowboarders were at the Winter X Games VI in Aspen, throwing
  down some incredible runs and generally having a good time on the
  slopes. Congratulations to Barrett Christy for winning a silver medal
  in Women's Slopestyle, and to Todd Richards, who won a bronze medal
  winner in Men's Slopestyle. Huge props go out to Shannon Dunn, Danny
  Kass, Peter Line and Kevin Jones for their stellar performances as
  well. 
 
 
  * Ride over 30 arcade style courses with tons of giant rails, big air
    jumps, and split levels 

 
  * New Snowboarder mode! Create your own rider, learn tricks and gains
    sponsorship in the deepest mode ever 

 
  * Learn new tricks and build your charisma points in order to get
    noticed by sponsors 

 
  * Fly around the world for competitions, film shoots and heli-boarding
    trios in your quest
