Ibiza United 2005 Format: Mp3 Ripeado: By Geniolisto2 This Ibiza summer official has recopilatorio. Tuesday 28 of June "IBIZA UNITED" is put on sale, recopilatorio without precedents been born of the union of the five better clubs of the White Island: Amnesia, the Divine one, Eden, Privilege and Space. "IBIZA UNITED" is a double CD more DVD with house more impressive of the moment in Ibiza. Or what is the same, with house that marks tendency from Ibiza and that will be listened to in the rest of the world in a very immediate future. "IBIZA UNITED" also includes also some incursions in the critical moment. "Little Love" of Lil'Love, "Pump up the jam", of D.O.N.S. feat Techotronic, "Summer Moon" of Africanism Allstars, "Rain" of Dr Kucho! & Pedro of the Moral, "One Word" of Kelly Osbourne, "for Saying strings" of Tiësto, and "Nasty Girl", of Inaya Day, are some of the 20 subjects of the CD number unode "IBIZA UNITED". In the second CD 10 subjects are included, plus bonus track, of the Dj's Residents of the five clubs that carry out the album: of Amnesia, Dj Oliver, Sea-T & Them Schmitz; of Eden, Alex Ellenger and Jorge Cross; of the Divine one, Iñaki Garci'a and Franco de Mulero; of Privilege, Sebastián Gamboa and Deep Josh; and of Space, Jose de Divina and Jonathan Ulysses.
