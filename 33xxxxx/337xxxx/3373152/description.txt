Louis Lewandowski played a very important role in the history of Jewish religious music. He was one of the main and most prominent reformers of synagogal chant. He was also a significant figure in the development of organ music, composing many innovatory pieces for solo organ. OLYMPIA OCD 399 

Recorded on 24th July 1993 in Lowicz Cathedral (1-14) and 28th July 1993 at the Monastery of the Benedictine Sisters of the Blessed Sacrament, Warsaw (15-24)

Wiktor Lyjak, organ  

World Premiere Recording
