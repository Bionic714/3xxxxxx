Train Simulator - Screen Shots, set 01


46 jpeg pictures that I have taken as screen shots from the
Microsoft Train Simulator game.

Resulotion: 1600 x 1200 pixels.


These can be used as desktops or just to see what the simulator
is capable of. Most trains featured are swedish, but there are
also some american ones in there...

None of the pic's have been "improved" in any way. They're just
some simple screen shots that you might enjoy looking at.
