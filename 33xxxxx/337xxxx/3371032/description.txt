Employing gorgeous visuals tied to an imaginatively crafted world, Sudeki 
  offers gamers the classic RPG adventure with plenty of new twists and 
  features that will attract even the most jaded RPG fan. The well-developed 
  combat system keeps the pace fresh and exciting while significant character 
  customisation and NPC interaction provide depth and long-term addiction. 

  Cinematic-style, real-time combat with dozens of moves, slow-motion bullet 
  time, earthshaking spells and hundreds of destructive melee attacks.
