Starship Tycoon is the new totally-rewritten version of our popular space
  simulation game StarLines INC
  
  Starship Tycoon puts you in charge of an interplanetary shipping company,
  you compete against AI controlled firms to survive in the cutthroat world
  of space commerce. Configure and upgrade your starships, hire and fire the
  crew, smuggle illegal goods, and wage price wars against your competitors.
  
  You can play either in a mission-based or freeform 'sandbox' mode. There is
  enough gameplay in Starship Tycoon to keep you playing for months, you can
  run your company any way you like, from keeping the staff happy and well
  paid right down to running on cheap fuel, smuggling illegal cargoes, and
  bribing the customs officials
