From FOX, the creators of American Idol and the producers of American Bandstand 
and The American Music Awards comes So You Think You Can Dance. This new 
competition is looking for the next big name in dancing. 

In the premiere season of this show, dancers were auditioned in Chicago, New York 
and Los Angeles in order to find the top 50 dancers to take to Hollywood. In 
Hollywood, the first week is spent working with challenging routines to impress 
the choreographers/judges.
