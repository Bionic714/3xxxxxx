Joey DeFrancesco - Jazz organ - Hammond organ

Joey DeFrancesco is widely credited with bringing back the popularity of the organ as a jazz instrument. Since releasing his debut album back in the eighties his music has been appreciated by audiences throughout the world.

DeFrancesco is a virtuoso, a complete Hammond jazz organ player: able to play improvised solo and comp with his both hands while at the same time playing the bass lines with the organ's foot pedals.

Here You will find DeFrancesco playing the mighty Hammond B-3 organ, in different settings and with a number of artists, including Pat Martino, John McLaughlin and Danny Gatton.
