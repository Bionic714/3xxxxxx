This program is considered by many to be one of the most important documentaries ever assembled on the UFO phenomenon. Nuclear Physicist Stanton T. Friedman presents stunning evidence that we are not alone. These programs document solid physical evidence of UFO landings, photos and videotaped evidence of UFOs from around the world and reveals the startling story of the governments ?Cosmic Watergate?, a massive effort to keep UFO information from the public. Also presented are the details of a massive effort by Friedman and others, utilizing the U.S. Government Court System and the ?Freedom of Information Act? to gain access to Government and Military documents which should now be available. Presented in part two is the startling story of Betty & Barney Hill, the first abduction story to receive international attention and why. Discover where some Alien visitors to earth are likely to originate and learn about technology now available which may make interstellar travel possible.
