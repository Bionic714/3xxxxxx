My last Del Amitri contribution. Can you do me good from 2002, their last record before beeing sacked by their record company. Not my personal favourite as a whole but still good record, containing a couple of their best songs "Drunk In A Band" and "Just Getting By".

7.  Wash Her Away   
8.  Last Cheap Shot at the Dream   
9.  Out Falls the Past   
10. She's Passing This Way   
11. Jesus Saves   
12. Just Getting By
