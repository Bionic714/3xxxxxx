The Dog Whisperer - National Geographic series (2004) on training and handling your dog.  Great stuff!  This guy is brilliant.  Any dog owner should watch these -- eventually, you will see something that will make life better for you AND your dog. http://www.nationalgeographic.com/channel/dogwhisperer/

Meet Josh, a lively Maltese poodle mix who won't let anyone go near him with a brush. Then meet Boomer, a clown-like dog that must always be the center of attention.
