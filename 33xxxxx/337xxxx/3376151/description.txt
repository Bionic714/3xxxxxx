 For the past 30 years, triple j has been busy recording some of the world's
most important bands to play in this country. Nirvana, Jeff Buckley, Nick
Cave, the Chilli Peppers... just to name a few.

We now have the staggering list of legendary acts appearing on triple j's
inaugural IMPOSSIBLE MUSIC FESTIVAL line-up this coming weekend.

It all kicks off 6pm this Friday 26th and goes til midnight Sunday 28th. Here's the entire list of "on stage" times for each of these amazing acts below.

Recorded at Livid Festival, Sydney. Oct 10, 2003. The set features 'Hotel Yorba', 'The Hardest Button To Button', 'Seven Nation Army' and 'I Can Smell A Rat'.
