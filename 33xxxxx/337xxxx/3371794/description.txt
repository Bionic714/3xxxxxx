Pinnacle recommends installing the 9.4.3 patch for all previous Studio 9 and Studio 9 Plus versions. Studio 9.4.3 is the latest version of Studio 9 to be released. Click here to go to the 9.4.3 download page. Make sure you look at the fix list below before downloading and installed the patch.

The history of fixes in ALL previous Studio 9 patches is listed below. All of the fixes below are also incorporated into the latest version of Studio 9.

Features in 9.4.1 from 9.3.5

# Import, edit and export of progressive scan MPEG-2 files
