Get up close and personal with 10 of the globe’s top-ranked contenders at 6 stunning locales including bars and blockbuster arenas. For the first time ever, you’ll learn what it’s like to rearrange rivals’ faces from a boxer’s perspective. Over-the-shoulder and landscape views also available.
