Música de amor y locura. Con Maestro plays romantic rock en español, filled with latino rythm, romantic lyrics of amor. A tribute to spicy latin romantic music, still portraying the chilean everyday life. Fuelled by inspiration from Maná, Luis Miguel, Bob Dylan, while Chilean music experts have compared Con Maestros with The Beatles and Miles Davis.
