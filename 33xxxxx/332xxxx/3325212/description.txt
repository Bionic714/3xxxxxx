TRAKTOR DJ Studio combines DJ mixing, live remixing, and mix production in a single software. It provides two audio players, DJ mixer including filters and EQ, beat matcher, digital scratcher, cue and loop section, and a convenient database for managing tracks and playlists. The software records all mix movements, allowing selective editing of mix control. Ten cue points and ten loops can be saved for each audio file. Complete with harddisk recording, interactive waveform displays and full MIDI control, TRAKTOR DJ Studio is the ultimate tool for the professional DJ.
