SetMask.dll Plugin for use with Indigo Rose AMS

With this tool you can easily change the shape of your application window at runtime.  AMS already allows for window masking, but SetMask DLL takes it one step further allowing you to change the mask on the current window.  With SetMask DLL you can also mask objects within your application.
