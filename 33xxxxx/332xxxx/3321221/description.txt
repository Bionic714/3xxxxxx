Now available for the first time on DVD. Originally released in 1989 Hokus Pokus is the second release from H-Street skateboards, this video picked up right where Shackle Me Not left off. With an even stronger team consisting of Matt Hensley, Sal Barbier, Danny Way, Eddie Elguera, Donger, John The Man Reeves, Ron Allen, Ton Magnusson, Alphonzo Rawls, Colby Carter, and Brian Lotti.
Running time: 57:29
