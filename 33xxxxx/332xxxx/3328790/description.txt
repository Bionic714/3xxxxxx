Xplode is like nLite, used to make automated/unattended installs of Windows.

This program is very powerful and not for novice, some background will be necessary.

I think most support for the program has been pulled by the creator, because of annoying "noob" questions.  But you can still find some information there as well as the forums there and at 

http://www.msfn.org/board/lofiversion/index.php/f86.html

http://xplode.voidfx.com

You will need to click the last word in the Warranty Section of the EULA "here" to continue the installation.
