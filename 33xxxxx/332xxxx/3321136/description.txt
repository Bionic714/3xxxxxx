Revelations is a 40 minute movie written, shot, and produced by Star Wars fans. It is the first Star Wars fan film made with the consent of George Lucas. 

Showcasing unbelievable new CG graphics, original screenplay, actors, and locations, Revelations demonstrates the high quality filmmaking that can be accomplished by an independent, dedicated cast & crew.Revelations is as close as it comes to an entirely new Star Wars film!
