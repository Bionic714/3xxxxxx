	
Latest Version: mIRCStorm V2.0.0
mIRCStorm is an easy to run, easy to set-up, easy to use eggdrop alternative. Commands following from the highly successful stormbot.tcl make the bot fun as well as understandable. 

mIRCStorm is both suited to the beginner and the expert offering a wide range of features from basic channel command/protection commands to partyline and botnet functions. The bot also includes 8 levels of access, ranging from channel voice to full global.
