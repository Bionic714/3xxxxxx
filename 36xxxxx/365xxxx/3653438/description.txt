The Shins are a band that deserve far more recognition than they currently receive from the general public. Already an indie favourite with a large number of indie fans 'in the know', they've been going since 1997 and have already released three popular albums, the most recent of which is the intriguingly titled Wincing The Night Away, which amazingly hit number two on the US Billboard charts upon release. From this album comes latest release 'Australia'.

Although it's far from certain whether the band could manage the same feat in the UK, it's still a really fantastically bouncy and catchy indie pop tune that shows off exactly why The Shins are such an attractive prospect to the Americans and indeed to an increasing number of fans in the UK, as evidenced by their upcoming sold out shows around the country and abroad. It's a funky song, it's simple yet clever, and it's the mark of a very talented band.
