---==< Hermano >==---

Hard hitting beats from former Kyuss vocalist John Garcia, along with fellow bandmates bassist David Angstrom (Supafuzz, Black Cat Bone), drummer Steve Earle (Afghan Whigs), and guitarists Mike Callahan (Disengaged) and Dandy Brown (Orquesta del Desierto).

This is quality hard rock that you shouldn't miss out on. The torrent contains the two releases made up to date:

Hermano - 2002 - Only A Suggestion
Hermano - 2005 - Dare I Say
