Hampton Hawes Trio, Vol. 1 (1955)

HAMPTON HAWES - piano
RED MITCHELL - bass
CHUCK THOMPSON - drums

Recorded in Los Angeles; June 28, 1955

This is jazz piano legend Hampton Hawes' first recording with his trio for Contemporary records. His breathtaking solo on "I Got Rhythm" is one of the best solos ever played on a rhythm-changes-type tune. If you're a jazz musician, this is a great solo to transcribe!
