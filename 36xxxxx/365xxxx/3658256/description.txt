The long awaited sequel to the puzzle game Treasure Island. Now it\'s a bright and breathtaking pirate story with colorful graphics and beautiful music. You have a chance to solve the puzzles and find the pirate treasures hidden by old captain Kidd. The gameplay stayed the same - you need to match 3 pirate treasures in a row. We added new elements that give you some extra opportunities.

After every level funny and eccentric skeletons will make their little dance for you. And in the end of each stage there is a little surprise - mini games with bonus points.

Treasure Island 2 is a perfect game for puzzle fiends. It\'s a nice combination of a thrilling plot, catchy gameplay, rich graphics and romantic music.
