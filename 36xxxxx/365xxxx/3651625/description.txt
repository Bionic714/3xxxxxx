This is the first collection of the legendary band tarmborstarnas earliest songs that has ever been releasted in an official album.

When tarmborstarna fist hit the underground scene they immediately achieved myth-status and the only way to get to listen to them were to actually know the band or one of the band's friends.


But now, five years later, tarmborstarna is entering the world of information technology and file-sharing. Music is meant to be listened to!
