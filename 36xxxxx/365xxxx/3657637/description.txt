Liquid Tension Experiment is an instrumental progressive rock / metal project initiated by Dream Theater drummer Mike Portnoy.
This is a project featuring Tony Levin, Jordan Rudess, Mike Portnoy and John Petrucci. The music is high-energy power prog with maximum complexity and really resembles its neighbours of MAGELLAN and SHADOW GALLERY. . There is plenty of blistering tunes on their discs. It gives a technical, square but highly efficient Progressive rock.
