Plot Outline A crimefighting organization called the Angels goes up against a drug-smuggling ring headed by a female kung-fu killer.

Plot Synopsis: The Government lays waste to Thailand's Golden Triangle poppyfields' production, and the Drug Lord seeks vengeance on the police forces involved. For 10 million Swiss Francs, he even buys the services of Madame Sue, a ruthless killer, to dispose of the Iron Angels, a trio of highly trained agents in martial arts and gunfighting...
