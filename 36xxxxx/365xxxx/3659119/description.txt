Klaus Schønning :
CYCLUS

Instruments 
Organ, synthesizer, piano, string machine, clavinet, acoustic guitar, 
slide guitar, saz, accordion, autoharp, penny whistle, rhythm computer 
& percussion.  
 
Already the year after his debut, in 1980, Klaus Schønning's second 
album 'Cyclus' was released. The concept this time centered around a 
theme of 8 beautifully arranged pieces. Klaus Schønning's melodic 
richness is clearly sensed in the framework of harmonies and moving 
rhythms. A striking characteristic in Klaus Schønning's musical universe 
is his use of the accordion, an instrument first introduced on Cyclus, 
combined with numerous vintage synthesizer tones, which are today 
enjoying a revival.
