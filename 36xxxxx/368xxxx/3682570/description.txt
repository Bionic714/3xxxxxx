Anna, Miles, Milly, Egg, And Warren are bright young lawyers who share a house in South London. Kindled by ambition, sex, love, drink, and drugs, their professional and personal lives are frenetic, stormy, and interwoven. As the series gathers pace, so do their careers and relationships motorcycle courier Ferdy and the conniving Rachel enter the fray and the whole gang chaotically move towards a delicious, cataclysmic finale. 

The TV envent of the 90s, This Life brought the lives of young people to the screen as never before. With honesty, a head-on confrontation of taboos and a gripping story, it provided a completely refreshing and real take on modern 20something life.
