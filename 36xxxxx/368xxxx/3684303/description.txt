       ┬ª_                    Ableton.Live.v6.0.7                    _┬ª
        ┬ª                                                           ┬ª
        ┬»┬ª                                                         ┬ª┬»
         ┬ª  SUPPLIER ..: TEAM AiR                                  ┬ª
         ┬ª  PROTECTION : SERIAL                                    ┬ª
         ┬ª  SIZE ......: 15 * 4,77MB                               ┬ª
         ┬ª  DATE ......: 04/2007                                   ┬ª
         ┬ª                                                         ┬ª
         ┬ª                                                         ┬ª
         ┬ª  Ableton  is  proud  to  release Live 6. Like no other  ┬ª
         ┬ª  Ableton  release,  Live  6  has  opened itself to new  ┬ª
         ┬ª  creative    communities+offering    Quicktime   video  ┬ª
         ┬ª  support,  a  professional  multisample  library,  and  ┬ª
         ┬ª  customizable racks of instruments and effects. Live 6  ┬ª
         ┬ª  also  satisfies  frequent user requests by delivering  ┬ª
         ┬ª  multicore support, enhanced project management tools,  ┬ª
         ┬ª  and improved MIDI control.                             ┬ª
         ┬ª                                                         ┬ª
         ┬ª  "Live  6 upholds Ableton's tradition of responding to  ┬ª
         ┬ª  and  respecting our vocal user base while maintaining  ┬ª
         ┬ª  our vision for the program," says Ableton CEO and co-  ┬ª
         ┬ª  founder Gerhard Behles. "The results are               ┬ª
         ┬ª  exciting+Live  6  is  our most versatile and powerful  ┬ª
         ┬ª  version   to   date.   We   also   spent  significant  ┬ª
         ┬ª  development  resources  on  workflow  improvements to  ┬ª
         ┬ª  make   sure  Live  continues  to  feel  good  as  the  ┬ª
         ┬ª  functionality grows."                                  ┬ª
         ┬ª                                                         ┬ª
         ┬ª  With  Quicktime  video support, Live enters the world  ┬ª
         ┬ª  of  post-production.  Drag  and  drop movies directly  ┬ª
         ┬ª  into  Live's Arrangement View, and monitor the result  ┬ª
         ┬ª  on a video window or second screen. Then, align music  ┬ª
         ┬ª  to  visuals,  and  process  the  movie's audio signal  ┬ª
         ┬ª  within  Live.  It has never been easier to improvise,  ┬ª
         ┬ª  compose and warp music to picture.                     ┬ª
         ┬ª                                                         ┬ª
         ┬ª  The  Essential  Instrument Collection provides Live 6  ┬ª
         ┬ª  users  with  a  comprehensive library of multisampled  ┬ª
         ┬ª  instruments.   The  included  instruments  cover  the  ┬ª
         ┬ª  entire  musical spectrum, from orchestral strings and  ┬ª
         ┬ª  brasses,  to  classic guitars and electric keyboards,  ┬ª
         ┬ª  to  instruments  like  the harp and the glockenspiel.  ┬ª
         ┬ª  Load and play the collection in Live's Simpler, or in  ┬ª
         ┬ª  Sampler+Ableton's     new    optional    multi-sample  ┬ª
         ┬ª  instrument.                                            ┬ª
         ┬ª                                                         ┬ª
         ┬ª  Instrument  and  Effect  Racks  offer  a new level of  ┬ª
         ┬ª  depth  to Live without sacrificing accessibility. Use  ┬ª
         ┬ª  Racks   to   create,  save,  and  control  customized  ┬ª
         ┬ª  groupings  of multiple instruments, effects and plug-  ┬ª
         ┬ª  ins.  Then  distribute your creations online. Use the  ┬ª
         ┬ª  Rack's   included  knobs  to  adjust  any  number  of  ┬ª
         ┬ª  parameters  with a single knob or fader. Live 6 comes  ┬ª
         ┬ª  with  many  preconfigured racks with both instruments  ┬ª
         ┬ª  and  common  processing  tools  like  guitar and bass  ┬ª
         ┬ª  distortion,    drum    processing,   and   mastering.  ┬ª
         ┬ª                                                         ┬ª
         ┬ª  Live 6 also features a number of exciting changes and  ┬ª
         ┬ª  improvements:                                          ┬ª
         ┬ª                                                         ┬ª
         ┬ª  Multicore   and   Multiprocessor  support  allow  the  ┬ª
         ┬ª  computational  workload  from instruments and effects  ┬ª
         ┬ª  to  be  distributed  to  other  processors  or cores,  ┬ª
         ┬ª  speeding up system performance.                        ┬ª
         ┬ª                                                         ┬ª
         ┬ª  Deep Freeze expands editing functionality with frozen  ┬ª
         ┬ª  tracks  (temporary  rendered  files  created  to free  ┬ª
         ┬ª  system  resources).  Cut,  copy, paste, duplicate and  ┬ª
         ┬ª  consolidate   clips   without  having  to  re-freeze.  ┬ª
         ┬ª  Automate  mixer  and  clip  envelopes,  record frozen  ┬ª
         ┬ª  session  view  clips  into  the arrangement, and drag  ┬ª
         ┬ª  frozen  MIDI  clips  into  audio  tracks, all without  ┬ª
         ┬ª  missing a beat.                                        ┬ª
         ┬ª                                                         ┬ª
         ┬ª  New  and  Improved  Devices  add to Live's palette of  ┬ª
         ┬ª  creative tools.                                        ┬ª
         ┬ª                                                         ┬ª
         ┬ª  EQ  Eight  has  replaced  EQ  Four, with improvements  ┬ª
         ┬ª  turning  it  into  both a sound-shaping and mastering  ┬ª
         ┬ª  tool.                                                  ┬ª
         ┬ª                                                         ┬ª
         ┬ª  Add  analog  richness  with  the  warm  distortion of  ┬ª
         ┬ª  Dynamic  Tube. Like many of Live's effects, it can be  ┬ª
         ┬ª  used   for   subtle   coloration   or  drastic  sonic  ┬ª
         ┬ª  manipulation.                                          ┬ª
         ┬ª                                                         ┬ª
         ┬ª  Saturator  has  been  improved with the addition of a  ┬ª
         ┬ª  true   analog   saturation  curve,  a  user-definable  ┬ª
         ┬ª  waveshaper,  and  an optional second output saturator  ┬ª
         ┬ª  stage.                                                 ┬ª
         ┬ª                                                         ┬ª
         ┬ª  Automate  and  "play"  incoming  MIDI notes with Note  ┬ª
         ┬ª  Length.  This MIDI effect can generate new notes when  ┬ª
         ┬ª  incoming  notes  are  released, emulating the release  ┬ª
         ┬ª  behavior   of  instruments  like  the  piano  or  the  ┬ª
         ┬ª  harpsichord.                                           ┬ª
         ┬ª                                                         ┬ª
         ┬ª  Operator has been augmented with a set of 24dB filter  ┬ª
         ┬ª  modes,  as  well  as  new FM algorithms, giving users  ┬ª
         ┬ª  more sonic possibilities than ever.                    ┬ª
         ┬ª                                                         ┬ª
         ┬ª  Conveniently  organize collections of Live Sets, Live  ┬ª
         ┬ª  Clips,  presets,  samples,  and movie files with Live  ┬ª
         ┬ª  6's  new  Project  Management  Tools.  Self-contain a  ┬ª
         ┬ª  project  and  pack  it  with lossless compression for  ┬ª
         ┬ª  archiving  or  transfer using Project Folders. Search  ┬ª
         ┬ª  for  missing files and purge the disk of unused files  ┬ª
         ┬ª  with ease.                                             ┬ª
