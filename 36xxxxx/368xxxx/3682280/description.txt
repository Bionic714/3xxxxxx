Butterfly Eighteen aka Secret of Shaolin Kung Fu (1977)

Plot:	"Mystery Of Chess Boxing" star Lee Yi Min stars as the leader of the beggar gang. Armed with the Deadly 18 Butterfly fist, he must defeat the clan's deadliest rival: the Golden Fox. Great training and acrobatic fights make this one of Lee's best films!

Cast: 	Lee I Min (Lee Yi Min)  ||  Chan Wai Lau (Chan Hui Lau)  ||  Sun Jung Chi (Sun Wing Gi)  ||  Chui Chung Hei  ||  Goo Chang  ||  Ma Chin Ku  ||  Ching Kuo Chung  ||  Shih Ting Ken  ||  Ma Cheung
