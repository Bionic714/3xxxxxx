If you like the symphonies of Mozart and Haydn, then check out these symphonies of one of their contemporaries in the Classical period, Austrian-born Ignace Joseph Pleyel. 

This CD on the Chandos label features 3 of his symphonies, played by the London Mozart Players and Matthias Bamert conducting. 

All tracks are in MP3 format, 192kbps. Front cover included.
