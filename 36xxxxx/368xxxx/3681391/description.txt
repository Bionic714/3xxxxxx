Based on the award-winning story by Kazunori Kaneshiro, the film \"GO\" is an alternative youth drama directed by Isao Gyojo of \"Himawari.\"

It centers around a boy who finds himself through hardships of love and friendship, learning to overcome prejudices based on nationality and race. Japanese hottest artist Yosuke Kubozuka , who plays excellent role as a Korean highschool student who lives in Japan, won both the \"Best Actor\" and the \"Best New Artist.\" Also starring Kou Shibasaki, Shinobu Ootake and Tsutomu Yamazaki. This movie was gigantic in Japan, and even in Asia and to the world!
