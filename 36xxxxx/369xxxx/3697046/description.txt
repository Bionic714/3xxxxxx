Rassegna - Dominos: Chants of Méditerranée (2003)

From Cádiz to Corsica, southern France, Greece, and the Balkans, Rassegna (Marseille, France) have compiled an excellent compilation of traditional Mediterranean songs foregrounding the convincing Corsican polyphony and north African folk-song tradition. The album was nominated for the German ?Critical Record Award? in the category of Folk Songs in 2004.
