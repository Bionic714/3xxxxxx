The Jade Bow (1966) - REMASTERED

Plot:	Two rivals each have half of a precious book of martial arts secrets, and must come together to battle each other to gain control of the other half.

Overview:	In a fight for possession of a precious book containing martial arts secrets, Li Fan-Shan, the owner of the book, is killed by Meng Sheng-Tung. While Meng's wife is killed by Li's brother, Meng runs away with half the book, while Li's brother keeps the other half. Tulung Taun-Zhe tries to get the book from Meng, but fails. Twenty years later, the book appears again and the cycle begins once again.

Cast: 	Carter Wong (Ka Tat)  ||  Polly Kuan  ||  Chang Yi  ||  Tin Fung  ||  Yee Yuen  ||  Liu Ping  ||  Tin Peng
