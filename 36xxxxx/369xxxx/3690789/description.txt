18 Shaolin Golden Boys (1996)

Plot:	Part of China's national treasure is hidden inside the Shaolin Temple. Those who want to find the treasure must seek out a map of the Shaolin Temple that has been divided into thirds. The gold-diggers soon discover that this escapade has turned deadly!

Cast: 	Yu Rong Guang  ||  Willie Chi (Tin Sang)  ||  Cherie Chan Siu-Ha  ||  Shing Fui On  ||  Billy Chow Bei-Lei  ||  William Ho Ka-Kui  ||  Chung Gwok Lung  ||  Choi Yue  ||  Lee Chi Chung
