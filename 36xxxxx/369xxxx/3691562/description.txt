This is Nirvana bootleg released by Dead Men's Paradise under the "Robin" logo. Recording sessions are as follows:

Tracks 1 - 7: first demo tape, reciprocal recording, Seattle - Jan. 23, 1988

Tracks 8 - 16: Studio Outtakes, demos and rehearsals

Tracks 17 - 19: unreleased sub-pop mini album, Smart Studios, Madison WI April 1990
