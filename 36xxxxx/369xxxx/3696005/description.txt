EURO CLUB HITS VOL. 1          
 
RELEASED MARCH 16, 2007...... 12 European club hits, previously unreleased in North America. G-Spott and Roy Gates are kings of the Dutch dance/pop scene -- both "City Streets" and "Midnight Sun" were top-40 pop hits in that country. Dan Marciano, Logic/David Guetta and Menace & Adam round out the contributions from France; Groove Cutter is a rising remixer for the UK scene; The Pull hails from Naples, Italy; CIRC, Phonjaxx, Egohead Deluxe and Alexander Perls vs. Thomas Falke represent the cutting edge of house from Germany.
