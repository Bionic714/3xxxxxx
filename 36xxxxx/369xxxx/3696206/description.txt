     The Three Heroes is a massively multiplayer online role-playing game based in a mysterious Oriental-style world. In The Legend of Mir you can be a powerful warrior and develope your ability in close combat, a skilled wizard with a whole set of spells or a mystic taoist provided with inner spirituals powers. Thousands of users can play together in the challenging world of Mir, which allows you to access numerous levels as you become stronger and stronger... to explore dangerous dungeons and fantastic cities... to create powerful Guilds and conquer legions of opponents defending amazing castles but this is only the beginning...
