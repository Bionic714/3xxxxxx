This is rare document of American SF author Robert Sheckley (www.sheckley.com) taking part to a science fiction roundtable on Romanian TV in 2005 in Bucharest, Romania, together with top British writer Ian Watson (www.ianwatson.info - author of the screen story of A.I., written for Stanley Kubrik and later filmed by Steven Spielberg). Audio is on English, except a few minutes on Romanian. Good quality video. The clip is about one hour long.
