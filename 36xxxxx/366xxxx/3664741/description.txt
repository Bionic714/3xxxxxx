FYI:
This guy is one of a kind french Canadian singer and songwriter. Thank you France (a friend of mine from Quebec) for letting  me discover this original artist. Good rhythms, sometimes funky, sometimes ethereal, lovely lyrics, a whiff of pure oxygen in the universe of pop music.  Pleasant and cheerful, I would say sparkling.

Enjoy !
