Plot:	It's Superman meets Hercules wrapped in an old style martial art film with demon-paced martial arts action choreographed by Jackie Chan's kung-fu classmates Yuan Pin and Yuen Hua to compliment Toho Studio style optical effects.

Overview:	Celebrated director Chu Yuan helms "Descendant Of The Sun", a Superman meets Hercules a la old style martial art film. Derek Yee is a magic solar baby sent Earthbound by a benevolent god, raised by an old carpenter, has martial art superpowers and "green kryptonite" loses them during solar eclipses. That's when the evil baby counterpart shows up. Demon-paced martial arts action by Jackie Chan's kung-fu classmates Yuan Pin and Yuen Hua compliments Toho Studio style special optical effects

Cast: 	Derek Yee (Erh Tung-Sheng)  ||  Cherie Chung Cho-Hung  ||  Ku Kwun-Chung  ||  Lung Tien-Chiang
