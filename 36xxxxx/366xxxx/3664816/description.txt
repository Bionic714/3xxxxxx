FYI:
This album is quite hard to describe. Those songs are from various artists, but you'll never hear them play like that. It's magic, sensual, a fabulous mood. 
I'll quote Jon Hassell: "With this recording, I locate myself squarely within that aspect of music which is fundamental and irreductible: the beauty of the sound... This music created a kind of permanent technicolor oasis in my spirit _ a place where i always want to stop for a cool drink, whether from Duke Ellington and Juan Tizol's "Caravan", or Ravel or raga, or gamelan or Gil, or Jo√£o or Joujouka _ and a place which became the underlying spring from which flowed my "fourth world" musical paragdim." 
So good! It goes beyond the trumpet, it's primitive, smoothly hypnotic. Try this journey, and let Jon Hassell make you discover charming landscapes.


Enjoy !
