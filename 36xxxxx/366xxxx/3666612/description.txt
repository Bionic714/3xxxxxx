It is the world's most famous sports car. Go behind the scenes of Ferrari's top-secret factory in Marnello, Italy, to see why vehicles bearing the Prancing Stallion emblem are so highly prized. Out of molten metal is born the latest, most powerful sports car that will op 200 miles an hour. Incredible robots and spectacular processes at every point in the production line are filmed with special close-
up lenses and illustrated with advanced 3D graphics. Learn the design secrets of Ferrari's newest car and put the pedal to the metal with a test drive on Ferrari's private race track.
