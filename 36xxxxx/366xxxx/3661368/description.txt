Mexico's Government-in-Resistance, led by Andres Manuel Lopez Obrador, broadcasts a weekly program called La Verdad Sea Dicha - Truth Be Told.

This is the full broadcast of 03/APR/2007, with English subtitles (SRT format). Discussion about fiscal realities in Mexico, continued corruption to cover the electoral fraud, reflections on recent events, further chronicle of the second assembly of the National Democratic Convention and workshops, including additional footage from the Assembly on 27/MAR.


Official page and full broadcast (without subtitles): http://www.laverdadseadicha.org/
