Ebooks associated with the Penn Foster Gunsmithing program detailing everything from standard firearms maintanance, to building and customising firearms, to handloading custom cartridges.

Excellent source of material for aspiring and/or home gunsmiths.

Further Information on the actual program can be found at: 
http://www.pennfoster.edu/gunsmith/index.html

Enjoy and happy shooting!
