Dark Colony: The Council Wars is an Expansion pack to the 1997 video game, Dark Colony. It is developed by Sold-Out Software, and published by GameTek, and came out in 1998.

New things you will find are crashed flying saucers, Taar fortress markings, and even Area 51. There is a new rattlesnake species that you can capture and use and much more.

Continue your work to eradicate the humans as you play the alien Taar, or to destroy the aliens as you play as a human.


P.S. To play addon you have to run dcexp.exe, dc.exe starts the original game.
