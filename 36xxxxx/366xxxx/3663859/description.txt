Plot:	Another of Fu Sheng's Fong Sai-Yuk films. Sai-Yuk leaves Shaolin Temple to seek revenge against various people.

Overview:	It is the final days of the majestic Ming Dynasty when the Chinese mainland is overrun by the Manchu troops. The disciples of the Shaolin Monastery are soon fighting alongside the patriots in their fierce struggle against the invaders--and the fate of a nation lies in the lethal hands of the Men From The Monastery.
