Plot: Marshal Ma makes a promise to his old drunken friend to find and deliver Fratty, his son. The rascal Fatty, who is training toad Kung Fu is after the bounty of the notorious criminal Shung Woo. However, confronting Shung Woo turns out to be more than Fatty bargained for and he has to turn to Marshal Ma to gain the skills needed to defeat the villain.

Director: Wong Sing-Lui

Cast: Ng Ming-Choi, Fan Mei-Sheng, Lau Yat-Fan, Go Miu Si, Tiger Yang Cheng-Wu
