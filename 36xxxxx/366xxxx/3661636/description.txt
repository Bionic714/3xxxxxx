Plot:	While master Wong Fei Hung is away traveling, the impulsive Lau Zhai (Yuen Biao), an initiate into Wong's kung fu school, begins wandering town. He soon allies with police chief Panther in order to offer assistance in toppling an opium distribution ring.

Overview:	Being forced by his dad, the spendthrift Lau Zhai joined a kung fu teaching school called Po Chi Lam which was held by Wong Fei Hung, a Chinese legendary master in martial arts. This is a heroic story about how Zhai became the famous "Kickboxer".
