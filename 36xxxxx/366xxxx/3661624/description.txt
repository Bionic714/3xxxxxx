Plot:	A ruthless emperor thinks he has vanquished his enemies by burning a temple to the ground. The survivors have other plans.

Overview:	Yongzheng, the Qing Emperor, orders the destruction of the Shaolin Temple so as to eliminate the main power behind his enemies. The Temple is completely destroyed by a huge fire, accompanied by rampant massacres. After three years of struggle, the surviving Shaolin disciples decide to assassinate the ruthless Emperor.

Cast: 	Carter Wong (Ka Tat)  ||  Chia Ling  ||  Chang Yi  ||  Nam Siu Foo  ||  Barry Chan  ||  Yee Yuen  ||  Kam Kong
