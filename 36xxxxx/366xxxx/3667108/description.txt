Title: Dead Or Alive
Also Known As: D.O.A. (Japan) (short title) / Dead or Alive (USA)
Genre: Crime / Drama / Thriller
Runtime: 105 min 
Country: Japan 
Language: Mandarin / Japanese

Plot:
Without a home and feeling no obligation to Japanese society or Yakuza,
Ryuichi (Takeuchi Riki) and his small group decide to make their own place
by trying to take over the Shinjuku underworld and the drug trade from Taiwan.
As they plan an all-out-assault on the remaining Chinese and Japanese mafia
kings,only Detective Jojima (Aikawa Sho) stands between them and complete
domination.

WARNING: This motion picture contains explicit portrayals of violence; sex;
violent sex; sexual violence; clowns and violent scenes of violent excess,
which are definitely not suitable for all audiences.
