Overview:	John Liu stars as an orphan in search of the perfect kung fu master, thus enabling him to take revenge on the crazed martial arts master who killed both his parents. On his travels, John meets up with some amazing masters of the martial arts, the most formidable being the leg fighter Kwan Young Moon. The scenes of these two bootmen training are worth the purchase price alone. High kicking kung fu action!
