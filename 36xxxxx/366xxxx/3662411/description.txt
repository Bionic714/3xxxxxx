Overview:	Legendary director Huang Feng directs the equally legendary CARTER WONG and ANGELA MAO in this action epic! When Prince Yung hires a Japanese guard to transport a rare and precious "Dharma's Relic", all hell breaks loose as various fighters across the land compete for the Buddhist artifact. LEGENDARY STRIKE is one legendary battle of the kung fu titans you don't want to miss!
