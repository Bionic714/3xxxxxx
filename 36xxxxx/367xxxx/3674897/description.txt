Tangerine Dream celebrate their 40th Anniversary in 2007 with the release 
of their latest album - \"Madcap?s Flaming Duty\". Released 2nd April, 
the album is dedicated to former Pink Floyd guitarist Syd Barrett who 
died in July 2006.

The album recorded in Vienna and Berlin during October 2006 features 
Edgar Froese, Thorsten Quaeschning, Chris Hausl, Bernhard Beibl, 
Linda Spa, Gynt Beator, Thomas Beator and Iris Camaa.
