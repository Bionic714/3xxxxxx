Kazaki (eng - Cossacks) - Animated Series 1967-1995

* KievNauchFilm studio (Kiev Science Film) - Color, USSR (Ukraine)

The main characters in a series of three zaporozhskih Cossacks: Lanky fellow, shorty and strong man.

They are incredible adventures, meeting with people from different countries and eras, even with the gods and aliens.

The movies have no dialogues or text, the act takes place in the form intuitively understandable scenes.
