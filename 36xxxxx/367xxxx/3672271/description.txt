Do you like funk and house music, and want all the mainstream hits as well? Then this is for you.
This is a collection of Hits from a funky house perspective going all the way back to the early 70?s. All the mayor hits are included and a lot of funky house stuff. Although the collection is concentrated around funky house, you will of course find a lot of Pop and Rock tunes, but also a substantial number of R&B tunes. This collection also includes a lot of reggae, some funky Grunge, all the major Ska releases from the early 80?s, and the most important Synthpop releases from the 80?s.

The collection comes in two seperate releases, the is the second one from 1967-1989. Check the comments to find the release from 1967-1989.

The songs are grouped according to the year they were released.
All songs have been tagged with Artist, Title, Year, Genre, Band, Album and a special designed Cover representing the year.
The Album and Band tags are the same for all songs in the same release year; that makes it possible to group the songs around the year of release if they are imported into iTunes.

A playlist for every year are included, sorting the stuff with the funkiest house at the top of the list.

Enjoy ...
