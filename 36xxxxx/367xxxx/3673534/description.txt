Overview:	Ling Yun-Chang and Ko Lung-Ta were disciples in the Shaolin Temple for 10 years. Ko was born in a family of Manchurian nobility, and was appointed "South China Area Commander" by the government. Ling rejected Ko's offer to serve the Manchu government. Ko then began to persecute Ling and those who worked on anti-Qing government...
