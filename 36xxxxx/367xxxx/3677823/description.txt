This is a fan mix for 3 Nine Inch Nails tracks, into 5.1 surround sound:

-Me, I'm not
-Capital G
-My Violent Heart

Mixed in Dolby Digital 5.1 448kbps, I created a miniDVD for those that really can't handle AC3 files (so called naabs). Just burn it and listen it into your 5.1 system, be careful numberous DVD player refuse to play a miniDVD burned on a regular CDR (that means use DVDRW).

I used the free official "generic" source mix files offered on nin.com on 24apr07. I tried to stay the closest to the original stereo mix, so i didnt touch the stereo or volume informations. The whole point of this was to move the mix tracks in the sound space from front to rear, and choose specific tracks to play in Front & LFE.

So this is a stereo to surround fan mix, not a fan remix. Warning: playing this in stereo is pointless.
