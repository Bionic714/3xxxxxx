Brothers Five (1970)

Plot:	Cheng Pei-Pei stars as Yen Lai, the one woman who can reunite the Kao brothers to rid the Teng Lung Manor of killers--as well as take revenge for their father's murder.

Overview:	Years before he was to become famous for directing Bruce Lee in Fist Of Fury and Jackie Chan in New Fist Of Fury, Lo Wei teamed with superstar swordswoman Cheng Pei-pei (Crouching Tiger, Hidden Dragon) for this action thriller of brotherly love?and death. She stars as Yen Lai, the one woman who can reunite the Kao brothers to rid the Teng Lung Manor of killers - as well as take revenge for their father's murder.
