*******************FA Premier League Football Manager 2001*************************
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Become the manager of your favorite football (soccer) team and lead them to victory. Manage any team from from 7 nations and 4 leagues (including of course the FA Premier League) and choose from 3 objectives for the season like league or cup success. Pay attention to your club's administration, as well as your players' stats and of course, your financial situation.
