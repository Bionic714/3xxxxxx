Music from the future played on instruments from the past

The unlistenable psychedelic soundtrack to a positive post-apocalyptic experience..... Domer, with no memory of what he has done, emerges from the basement with cassette 4-track masters and urine-soaked pants...

This psychadelic prog rock band from Oregon are definately worth checking out. This album is a stunner.
