Grandmaster Of Shaolin Kung Fu aka Mantis Fist Boxer

Plot:	The mantis is small but deadly. And so is our hero in "Mantis Boxer". Using the rare and unique form of Mantis Boxing he takes back control of his village, which is held hostage by evil bandits. Top notch action and mayhem!!
