High resolution scans of original FLCL concept character and post-production art books. These have since been published in printed form but are hard to come by, especially outside Japan, especially the pre-production concept art.

Here's a link to the Wikipedia article for more info: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Flcl

FLCL, an abberviation of the Japanese "Furi Kuri", often titled "Fooly Cooly" in English-speaking countries. However, the second word is clearly spelled with 2 letter L's in at least one place in the anime as this: "Fooly Coolly".

BTW... grow up, Furi Kuri is NOT Japanese for "breast fondling".
