reviews

Apr 2007

'Funky House Sessions' returns with another stellar line-up of the most uplifting vocals, funky bass lines and peak time floor fillers to satisfy anyones yearning for the funkiest in sexy house.
Sitting somewhere between the upfront musical coolness of a Hed Kandi album, and the fun and familiarity of a super hit powered Ministry of Sound classic like 'The Annual', 'Funky House Sessions' spins just the right amount of chart success and underground house hits to present the perfect package for any self respecting lover of dance.
2 CDs of the most upfront chart smashes, with Alex Gaudino Feat Crystal Waters 'Destination Calabria' and Camille Jones Vs Fedde Le Grand 'The Creeps', together with recent super hits such as Mason Vs Superstar 'Perfect Exceeder', Eric Prydz 'Proper Education', Cascada 'Miracle' and Booty Luv's 'Boogie 2Nite'.
