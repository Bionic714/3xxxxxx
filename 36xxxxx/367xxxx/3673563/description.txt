Blade of Fury aka One Dagger Collapsed City (1993)

HKFlix Says:  	4 Stars

A swordplay masterpiece starring Sammo Hung and taking place in the Manchu Dynasty.

Plot:	In the twilight of Manchu rule, a band of noble swordsmen (and swordswomen) defend the dynasty. directed and featuring the portly Sammo Hung in an unusual dramatic role.

Cast: 	Ti Lung  ||  Yeung Fan  ||  Cynthia Khan  ||  Ngai Sing  ||  Sammo Hung (Kam Bo)  ||  Wong Kam Kong  ||  Rosamund Kwan Chi-Lam  ||  James Tin Jun  ||  Yip Wing Cho
