Your only chance to race with classic Squaresoft characters is in CHOCOBO RACING. Play the Story mode and help Chocobo as he tries to win the pieces of the legendary Blue Crystal from the other racers, which include Mog, White Mage, Chubby Chocobo, and others. If you want racing without a story, hit one of the eight tracks in the Grand Prix, Time Attack, or Relay modes. However, none of these races are ordinary: racers can collect magic stones that will allow them to cast spells to slow down the competition.
