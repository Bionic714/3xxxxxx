Latest release edition of SimplyMepis, a Linux Distribution based on Debian, Fedora and Gentoo. I comes with a live disk, which makes it possible to run it completely without a harddrive, booting from the DVD-Rom, programs are preinstalled and hardware is autodetected.

You can choose to install it on your harddisk with the Mepis installer.

More info on http://www.mepis.com and http://mepis.org
