Swarm Assault is a real time strategy game that requires you to control 
huge swarms of ants, beetles, spiders, scorpions and wasps 
in a desperate attempt to control the landscape. 
Begin your crusade by conquering easy levels and progress to harder levels 
where your knowledge of each creatures strengths and weaknesses will be heavily tested. 
Play Swarm Assault today and discover why the creepy crawlies dominate this planet!
