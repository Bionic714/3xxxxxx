With superb AI and a powerful 3D engine, you will play in fully destructible environments with an incredibly flexible camera through rain and snow and wind, during the day and the night. With 23 single player missions through a rich and exciting storyline and various multiplayer options, SOA is the title gamers have been waiting for.


Minimum System Requirements:
Pentium III 600 MHz, 128 MHz RAM, Windows 98/2000/ME/XP, DirectX 8.1, 16 MB DirectX compliant video card, DirectX compliant sound card, 4x CD-ROM, 600 MB hard-disk space
