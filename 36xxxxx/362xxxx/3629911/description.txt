Mexico - La Verdad Sea Dicha [Truth Be Told] - 27/FEB/07 - highlights

Mexico's Government-in-Resistance, led by Andres Manuel Lopez Obrador, broadcasts a weekly program called La Verdad Sea Dicha - Truth Be Told.

This clip includes highlights from the broadcast of 27/FEB/2007, with English subtitles (SRT format). It talks about repression and violence suffered in Oaxaca and across the country, human rights violations, the movement to free political prisoners, Calderon's militarization of the country, and peaceful civil resistance turned into an art. It also includes a recent speech made by AMLO about the National Democratic Convention tasks ahead, and the activities planned for the 21st to 25th of March.
