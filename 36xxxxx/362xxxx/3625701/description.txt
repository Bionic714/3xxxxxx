BRYAN FERRY televised BBC1 Sessions Live Concert recorded February 2007.

To promote the new album DYLANESQUE this intimate concert features a mixture of Bryan and Roxy Music classics with a selection of new Bob Dylan cover-versions/reworkings. 


The Track performed are;

ALL ALONG THE WATCHTOWER
SLAVE TO LOVE
TO MAKE YOU FEEL MY LOVE
LETS STICK TOGETHER
JUST LIKE TOM THUMB'S BLUES
DON'T STOP THE DANCE
GATES OF EDEN
SMOKE GETS IN YOUR EYES
I PUT A SPELL ON YOU
A HARD RAIN'S GONNA FALL
JEALOUS GUY
