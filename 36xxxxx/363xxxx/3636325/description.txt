Two audio plays of Neil Gaiman's work performed by Seeing Ear Theater:

Snow Glass Apples:
	
Bebe Neuwirth stars as a wise Queen who wants nothing more than to reign over her kingdom peacefully but is forced to match wits with an inhuman child.

Murder Mysteries:

Before the fall of Lucifer, heaven knew sin. An angel is mysteriously murdered by one of its brethren and the Angel of Vengeance must find the killer.  Starring Brian Dennehy.
