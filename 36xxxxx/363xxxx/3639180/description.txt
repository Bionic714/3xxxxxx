Based on true events, Ring Girls is a fast paced story about five
          gorgeous  young American  women from  Las Vegas  who train  under
          their  mentor,  Master Toddy.  These  five fighters  take  on the
          ultimate challenge of fighting the best female Muay Thai fighters
          in the world.

          The five  American beauties  give up  the glitz  and glory of Las
          Vegas and  travel deep  into the  jungles of  Thailand. Its there
          where they  battle the  elements and  train to  use all  of their
          weapons: kicks, knees, elbows and fists. The story climaxes in an
          epic battle in the ring:  a bloody, fierce fight that  brings the
          entire country to its feet!

          For  five  warriors   from  Las  Vegas,   it's  the  trip   of  a
          lifetime....and the fight of their lives!
