
 The Many Adventures of Winnie the Pooh (1977)
   
 
What bear could be more loveable than the honey-eating, huggably pudgy, sweet-hearted Winnie-the Pooh? Certainly his owner and best friend, Christopher Robin, can't find one! Enjoy the adventures of Pooh bear and his pals in this animated adaptation of A.A. Milne's classic children's books. Includes three featurettes: "Winnie the Pooh and the Honey Tree", "Winnie the Pooh and the Blustery Day", "Winnie the Pooh and Tigger Too" 
 
Genres: Kids/Family 
 
MPAA Rating: G
