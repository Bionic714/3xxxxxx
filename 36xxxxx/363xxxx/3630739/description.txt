Survivor is a novel by Chuck Palahniuk. A satire of our commercial culture, it is the story of Tender Branson, a member of the Creedish Church, a death cult. In it, every member learns how to be a servant for the human race?most of them are butlers and maids?and fear most human pleasures. They await a sign from God to tell them to deliver themselves unto Him, that is, commit suicide.

Read by Scott Brick.
