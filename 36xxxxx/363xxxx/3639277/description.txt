Bon on a Stick and Slither

Boy on a Stick and Slither is a comic strip about a boy on a stick and his friend "Slither". BOASAS began in 1998. For more information contact Steven L. Cloud < boasas@boasas.com >

BOASAS has been featured in The Atlanta Journal Constitution, Esquire, H2S04, TMCM and While You Were Sleeping.
