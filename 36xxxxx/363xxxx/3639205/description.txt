Mexico - La Verdad Sea Dicha [Truth Be Told] - 13/MAR/07 - highlights

Mexico's Government-in-Resistance, led by Andres Manuel Lopez Obrador, broadcasts a weekly program called La Verdad Sea Dicha - Truth Be Told.

This clip includes highlights from the broadcast of 13/MAR/2007, with English subtitles (SRT format). It talks about the proposed road rescues, their ties to billionaire financial "rescues", President Bush's Latin American tour, and the upcoming National Democratic Convention on March 21st to 25th in Mexico City.
