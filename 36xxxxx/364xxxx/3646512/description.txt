After the fall of the Ming Dynasty, China is thrown into turmoil. The loyalists seek the return of Ming, the government determine to stay in power. One power hungry government official grabs the chance of promotion when he learns the whereabouts of both the Dynasty's Prince and the loyalists leader. The intervention of a mysterious Kung Fu Master with superb Shaolin techniques prevents their capture. The master's symbol is the lotus blossom, identical to that worn by another new arrival. The only difference between the two is one saves life, while the other takes it away.
