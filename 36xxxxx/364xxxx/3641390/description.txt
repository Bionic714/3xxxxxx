Overview:	This is one of the few films where "Godfather of the kung-fu film" Chang Cheh headlined Lo Lieh as the leading hero, three years before Lo would become Shaw Brothers' first international star in King Boxer. Here he plays a dedicated chief constable for Tsang Chou village, who falls in love with the blind daughter of a bandit who is wreaking havoc. Joining him in all the action and emotion are award-winning actor Ku Feng and future superstar David Chiang.
