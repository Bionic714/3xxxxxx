Plot:	An ambitious prince learns martial arts in order to consolidate his power and vanquish his enemies.

Overview:	The rulership of a young prince (Carter Wong) inspires fear because of his ruthless means of securing power. So as to be prepared to fend off any rebellion, he endures lengthy lessons in shaolin kung fu from masters of the martial arts. Before he can reclaim his throne, though, tradition demands that he fight and defeat the apparently invincinble 18 Bronzemen. This sequel to the popular first film includes plenty of battle scenes and a variety of martial arts styles are on display as staged imaginatively by director Joseph Kuo (Born Invincible).

Cast: 	Carter Wong (Ka Tat)  ||  Tien Peng  ||  Polly Kuan
