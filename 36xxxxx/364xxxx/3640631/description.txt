Guido Crepax is a great erotic drawer, very famous in Europe. He is italian.
In 2003, to announce his death, a french newspaper write, as title :
\"Crepax ou le dessin qui bande\"
(Crepax or the drawing which has a hard-one)

This package contains
    3 english editions (_EN extension)
    11 french edition
and 1 dumb edition (Lanterne Magique)

Enjoy !
