                       Description
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Five of the world's top scientists propose five radical scientific inventions 
which could stop global warming dead in its tracks. The ideas include: a giant 
sunshade in space to filter out the sun's rays and help cool us down; forests 
of artificial trees that would breath in carbon dioxide and stop the green 
house effect and a fleet futuristic yachts that will shoot salt water into the 
clouds thickening them and cooling the planet.

Are these the crazy ideas of mad scientists in white coats or could they be 
our saviours and save the world from global warming?
