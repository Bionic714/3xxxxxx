Overview:	The Miracle Fighters Yuen Jun Yeung and Fong Ke An are two feuding disciples of a magical sect who have been trying to outsmart each other since childhood.

They adopt two followers (Tsiu Siu Keung and Sonny Yue) in hopes that they will carry on their childish squabble. The hard fighting fists of Tsiu Siu Keung, combined with Magic Rocking Horse kung fu, guarantee the ultimate in martial arts madness!
