Release Notes:

Great album by this lady. Excellent electronica
compemented by very nice vocals that sets an
interesting mood.

A comparison I read is that this sounds like Eva
Dahlgren (another Swedish artist) remixed by Tiga
and it's not too far off i think.

Nevermind that the lyrics are in Swedish, if you
don't understand Swedish, look past the chef in the
muppets, consider it an interesting flavour (it
is!), give it a listen and buy it if you like it!
