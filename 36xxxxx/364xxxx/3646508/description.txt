Premise: Gao Jian (Yuen Tak) is a low-ranking kung fu student frequently bullied whose fortunes change when he begins training with a wounded kung fu expert (Chen Kuan-tai) hiding from the 'Three Devils.' Forced to leave his school, Gao returns a year later to get revenge for the kung fu expert's death at the hands of the Three Devils and to free his school from their subjugation.
