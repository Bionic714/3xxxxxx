Plot:	Gordon Liu stars as a policeman on the take who ends up becoming a powerful mob boss.

Overview:	Although director Kuei Chih-Hung is noted for horror flicks that best many of Hollywood's "slashers", he began with martial arts films under legendary Chang Cheh. In "Godfather From Canton", Kuei returns to his roots starring the Yul Brynner of Shaolin movies, Gordon Liu Chia Hui, as a policeman on the take who ends up becoming a powerful mob boss.

Cast: 	Gordon Liu Chia-Hui  ||  Wong Yung  ||  Ku Feng  ||  Choh Seung-Wan  ||  Chan Si-Gaai  ||  Tang Ching  ||  Hon Gwok-Choi
