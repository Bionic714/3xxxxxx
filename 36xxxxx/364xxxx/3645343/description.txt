Navigon MobileNavigator 6 (version 6.1-build 555) for PNA (150, 240T, 3002, 400, 405T, 4000, 4000C, 5000, 6000, BM-6300, TS7000) complete with the full Europe map (Europe_MN60.map).

Quick install:

* Extract content of archive to root directory of a 2GB SD-memory card.
* Switch on your PNA and carry out a reset. Your PNA will restart.
* On prompting, insert the memory card and confirm with ?OK?.
