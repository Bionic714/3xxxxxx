Mexico's Government-in-Resistance, led by Andres Manuel Lopez Obrador, broadcasts a weekly program called La Verdad Sea Dicha - Truth Be Told.

This is the full broadcast of 27/MAR/2007, with English subtitles (SRT format). Full chronicle of the second assembly of the National Democratic Convention, the workshops of which it was composed, conclusions and proposals that resulted, and the plenary session at the Zocalo on Sunday March 25th 2007.
