Synopsis:
It's an all out power struggle when the old master of the Lung Clan dies. The manipulative and power-hungry Mar Chen Hui gains control over Ku-Feng, crippling him by using the demonic Devil's Palm technique. Ku-Feng despondently wanders, turning to the bottle until he meets up with three other crippled men, who band with him to become the Four Invincibles and fight Mar Chen Hui and his demonic Devil's Palm.
