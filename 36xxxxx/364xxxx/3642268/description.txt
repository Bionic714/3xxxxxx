Overview:	Mang Fei (Meng Fei) stars as Fong Sai Yuk, a wrongfully accused murderer in this HK actioner, The Prodigal Boxer. Two vicious masters of the local kung fu school, seeking revenge against Fong Sai Yuk, attack his home and kill his father while Fong is away. Left fatherless, Fong trains at the hands of his mother and a bloody trail of revenge is set in motion. Can a year's worth of training prepare Fong Sai Yuk for his deadly confrontation with the vicious masters?

Non-stop action and dramatic fight scenes punctuate this old school classic. This is the first film in Crash Cinema's Shaolin Boxer Collection.
