Overview:	The ultimate martial arts classic from the great director King Hu ("Swordsman") and the prime inspiration for "Crouching Tiger, Hidden Dragon"! Ming Dynasty noblewoman Yang (Hsu Feng) must escape from the evil eunuch Hsu. She seeks refuge at a decrepit town where she gets assistance from a naïve scholar and a group of mysterious yet powerful monks. Winner of a special technical award at the Cannes Film Festival for "Superior Technique", "A Touch Of Zen" is a milestone in martial arts cinema, filled with gorgeous sceneries, superb fights, and an unforgettable finale that ranks as one of the greatest in film history.
