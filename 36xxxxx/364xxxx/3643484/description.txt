Overview:	A rather bizarre film with several partially intertwining plots. Hwang Jang Lee appears occasionally as a ruthless white-haired assassin who's killing folks one by one. Bolo makes a small appearance in a bizarre and unfortunate wig, alongside two other unfortunately coiffed villains, and Jeannie Chang is the cute-as-a-button soy milk vendor who likes to kick ass.
