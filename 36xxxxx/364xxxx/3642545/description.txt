Overview:	Popular kung fu leading man CARTER WONG stars in this action thriller. When a group of Japanese hoodlums take over a small town in North Eastern China by force, it's up to a band of renegade martial artists to stop this gang of ruthless criminals. MAGNIFICENT FIST is magnificent fist fighting action from start to finish!
