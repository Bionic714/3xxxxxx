Plot:	Four (not five) crippled men make the most of their disabilities as they band together to form a kung fu team and exact revenge on the men who crippled them.

Overview:	The sequel to 5 DEADLY VENOMS sees the venom mob return as four crippled masters out for vengeance. Though they are legless, blind, deaf, and brain damaged, the heroes learn superhuman techniques to battle an evil warlord and his army.
