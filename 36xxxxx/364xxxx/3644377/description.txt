Mexico's Government-in-Resistance, led by Andres Manuel Lopez Obrador, broadcasts a weekly program called La Verdad Sea Dicha - Truth Be Told.

This is the full broadcast of 20/MAR/2007, with English subtitles (SRT format). It talks about the upcoming National Democratic Convention, and plans for the five days. It also discusses continued human right abuses, evidence of corruption, as well as environmental, cultural and electoral malpractices. Finally, a short humourous segment, with an invitation to the Convention.

See you at the National Democratic Convention on March 21st to 25th in Mexico City.
