Three young avengers (Jack Long, Mark Long, and Chang Yu Yu) discover the weak spot of this seemingly invincible fighting force: his throat. But hitting it isn't going to be easy...

Independent filmmaker Joseph Kuo combines a convincing performance by Carter Wong with an excellent story and the martial arts mastery of Joseph Long, making this one of Kuo's better efforts.
