How to Install Cedega:

1.Open the cedega-small-5.2.3 folder.
2.Inside there you will find a lot of files , each one is for different linux dists (debian,mandriva,suse...)
3.If you have Debian double click on "cedega-small_5.2.3_all.deb".
4.Then click on "Applications >> Games >> Transgaming Cedega".
5.Next , until you see "System Setup Stage 1 (Page 2 of 5)" , uncheck "Check for Updates" and click on "Install local package"
6.Click on "Browse" and go to the "Updates" folder and select "cedega-engine-5.2.10-local-update.i386.cpkg"
7.Click "Forward" to check if your drivers are working.
8.Click on "Install" to install a Win32 game .
9.Enjoy this shit.
