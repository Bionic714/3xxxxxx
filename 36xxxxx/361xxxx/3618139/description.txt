Mexico - La Verdad Sea Dicha [Truth Be Told] - 13/FEB/07 - highlights

Mexico's Government-in-Resistance, led by Andres Manuel Lopez Obrador, broadcasts a weekly program called La Verdad Sea Dicha - Truth Be Told.

This clip includes highlights from the broadcast of 13/FEB/2007, with English subtitles (SRT format). It talks about privatization of PEMEX and its history, social inequality, political satire at the tune of 1940s movies, and the upcoming National Democratic Convention on March 21-24.
