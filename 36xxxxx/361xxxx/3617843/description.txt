A loose conglomeration of porn scenes, probably from other movies, strung together in the form of a 'current affairs' program with Foxx and Drake as the hosts. There are about eight scenes in all, and the quality of both filming, acting and actors is variable. Traci Lords plays a secretary who takes dictation from her boss; a door-to-door salesman finds only a willing schoolgirl at home; an older woman gives a teenage boy a lesson or two; there are hillbillies, an underwater sex scene complete with SCUBA gear, and even the two hosts are swept up in the sexual goings-on at the end of the compilation scenes. It's not a great porn flick, but by no means the worst.
