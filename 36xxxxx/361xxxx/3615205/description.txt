How William Shatner Changed the World, aired 13 November 2005.

From flip phones to medical imaging, William Shatner lists these and many other examples of how the fictional U.S.S. Enterprise influenced the world of science in reality in this two-hour documentary.

The special is based on Shatner's book "I'm Working on That," and features interviews with some of the famous inventors who were inspired by Star Trek.
