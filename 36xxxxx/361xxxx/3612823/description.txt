
Mexico - La Verdad Sea Dicha (Truth be Told) - 06/FEB/07 - clip 

Mexico's Government-in-Resistance, led by Andres Manuel Lopez Obrador, broadcasts a weekly program called La Verdad Sea Dicha - Truth Be Told.

This clip includes highlights from the broadcast of 06/FEB/2007, with English subtitles (SRT format). It recaps the protest march last week, AMLO's proposal for immediate action, effects on the people, and international news.

It also includes previously seen footage from Oaxaca, and reminds of the upcoming National Democratic Convention on March 21-24.
