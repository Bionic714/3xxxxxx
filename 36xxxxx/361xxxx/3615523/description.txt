Now  Cannibal Films, in partnership with the Bulhorgia Films, presents the following day to the attack of the Monster vegetable where we discover that the wounds of the libertarian alien was not fatal and he come back to fight against the prejudices of the colonists of the Santa Catarina West.  

A reflexive and sensible film about the routes of the humanity ? with hilarious moments of humor nonsense ? for amuse and to cry.
