The Comedy Central Roast of William Shatner, aired 21 August 2006.

Participants: Jason Alexander, Nichelle Nichols, George Takei, Betty White, Farrah Fawcett, Andy Dick, Lisa Lampanelli, Fred Willard, Greg Giraldo, Jeff Ross, Artie Lang, Patton Oswalt and via video feed Leonard Nimoy, Ben Stiller, and Sandra Bullock.
