CorePak is a series of packs containing affordable and quality game assets made by myself, they are free to use in commercial and non-commercial games, of any development tool, not just FPSC.

Volume One contains 35 game ready models ready for instant use in FPS Creator. It is the first in its series of custom media installments to FPSC. Containing plenty of generic and everyday models, they are sure to fit perfectly in most games, and play a great role.

The Models are in Direct X format, each textured with a .DDS texture, some with multiple variations, built specifically for the FPSC engine.
