Rocker Bryan Adams takes his show to Ireland in this concert video, where he performs a powerful selection of new and old favorites in the Summer of 2000. Bryan Adams: Live at Slane Castle includes the songs "(Everything I Do) I Do It For You," "Cuts Like a Knife," "Summer of 69," "18 Till I Die," "Run to You," "Can't Stop This Thing We Started," "She's Only Happy When She's Dancing," and 11 others.
