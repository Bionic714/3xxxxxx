                       Description

It's the most popular flavour in the world and as CHOCOLATE CONFIDENTIAL 
reveals, the lengths to which some people will go to pursue their passion for 
chocolate are amazing. Some would say that chocolate rivals sex as a universal 
obsession. You can drink it, eat it, it's good for your heart and can even be 
a beauty toolùchocolate is a billion dollar industry.

According to the documentary, the average North American consumes 11.7 pounds 
of chocolate each yearùa low statistic considering the Swiss gobble up to 22.3 
pounds each.

CHOCOLATE CONFIDENTIAL includes an interview with Chloe Doutre-Roussel, the 
so-called 'chocolate angel' who eats pounds each day and travels all over the 
world to find the perfect cocoa bean.

Even New York cosmetic mogul Bobbi Brown has jumped on the chocolate bandwagon, 
launching a new line of cocoa-hued makeup. From the artistry of high-end 
chocolate-makers in Paris to the cocoa bean plantations in the Caribbean, 
CHOCOLATE CONFIDENTIAL travels the world, offering delectable insider 
information.
