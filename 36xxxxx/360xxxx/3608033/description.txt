Mexico - Mexican Politics - La Verdad Sea Dicha (Truth be Told) - 30/JAN/07 - clip

Andres Manuel Lopez Obrador, and his government-in-resistance, have started a weekly news & opinion TV program. This clip includes highlights from the most recent broadcast of 30/JAN/2007, with English subtitles. It outlines his basic goals and aims, talks about Oaxaca, the economy, and the rally planned for Jan 31st.
