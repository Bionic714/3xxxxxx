The successful climax to the great fight for votes for women came in the early years of th twentieth century, but the struggle had been going on for many years. At this distance it is difficult to appriciate the fuore the Suffragette campaign caused throughout the country - or understand the pain and indignity its leaders endured in pursuit of their dreams.

Feauturing vivid, atmospheric reconstructions - dramatised 'eye witness' accounts and extensive contemporary images, this video is a hugely entertaining and informative look at the fascinating story of Emmeline Pankhurst and the Suffragette movement.


(Castle Home Videos, 2000). The History Makers. Running time: approx 55 mins. Certificate E (exempt from classification).
