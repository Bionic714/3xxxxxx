Substantial edits in several of the songs ranging from a whopping minute extra of 'Don't Wanna Go Home', 47 seconds from 'Biggest Mistake, 37 seconds from 'Under the radar', 33 seconds from 'Driving Too Fast', 27 seconds from 'Dangerous Beauty', 18 seconds from 'It Wont take long', 10 from 'she saw me coming' down to a couple of seconds (or less) from some others 

Some songs appear to be longer, have slightly different solos or have extra bits of vocals from Mick which were later mixed out, with 'Dangerous beauty', 'Let me down slow', 'It wont take long' and 'Biggest mistake' being noticeably different.

Most of the ABB songs are on it, with the exception of Rough Justice, Look What the Cat Dragged In, Laugh I Nearly Died and Infamy. Some tracks appear a couple of times ('I Dont Wanna Go Home' appears three times)
