GENIUSER - MUD Black (2005)

Geniuser have released their debut album Mud Black, on the Danish Phisteria label.

Former singer of 4AD legends The Wolfgang Press, Michael Allen, and Giuseppe de Bellis, known for his collaborations with The Orb, teams up on this album, with a strong line of songs.

The remarkable grooves that made The Wolfgang Press pioneers are present with added cosmic futuristica. Mick Allens outstanding voice is significant and ever present, this album not only reminds us of The Wolfgang Press, but also breaks new ground, as Giuseppe de Bellis remarkable production skills catapults the understated funk.

This is an album that will unfold itself for each listen?.open up wide and let yourself be seduced!!!
