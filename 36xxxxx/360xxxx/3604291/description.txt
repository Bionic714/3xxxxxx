Recovery Is Posible Linux Live CD/USB/Flash

 Really useful distro for recover damaged disks that can be stored into a bootable miniCD or an USB pen.

Bottable distro instalable in several media tipes USB, flash cards or mini CDs with several an powerful utilities for recovering data of many kind of disks. It can repartitiong, recover and do images of almost any kind of filesistems.
 The interface could be either text console or xwindowed, this one includes several graphic appz as Firefox or Qparted.
 Includes a version of QEmu that supports x86 Machines.
