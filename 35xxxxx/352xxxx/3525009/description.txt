B E N E F A C T O R
PinkRoof Pictures

Release date: Q3 2005
Running time: 19min


This short film tells a story about a guy who runs
into a kid from another planet whose means to travel
across the universe - the artifact - decayed before
their eyes. In addition they realize that they are
being chased by something known only as the entity.

Will they survive long enough to repair the artifact?
