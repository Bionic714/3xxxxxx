---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                                             Review
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


In '89 'Odyssey', Ian's first CD, sees the light of day.
The musical style has progressed since Jade, and is more varied.
Some tracks, such as the opening piece 'Hyperion' sound very majestic and grand, while others
are very intimate.
There are various influences to be found, which give certain tracks a very peculiar quality,
almost like adding a pinch of spices to the main course.
Listen to 'Lammergeyer' for example, which sounds a bit Peruvian (conjuring up images of those
Peruvian groups playing panflutes and drums).
Also very special, is the last track 'Chameleon' where Ian uses more sequences.
