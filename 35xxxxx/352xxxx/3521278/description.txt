Considered one of the best haunted house movies made, this movie was directed by Peter Medak and stars the late George C. Scott and his wife Trish Van Devere.

George C. Scott plays a man who had just lost his family and must get away to put the tragedy behind him. He moves into an old, abandoned mansion for solitude and starts to experiance strange happenings in the house. He then becomes an unwilling instrument of a ghost's revenge.

With seances, nocturnal grave-diggings, and supernatural hauntings, this movie provides a great ghost story with a suspenseful plot, plenty of spooky atmosphere and beautiful, haunting music! BE SURE TO WATCH THIS WITH THE SOUND VOLUME UP AND ALONE IN THE DARK!
