These scans come from the polish book by Ryszard Wojcik:

"Kusiciel Demonow, opowiesc o Stefanie Zechowskim"
("Temptor of Demons, a story about Stefan Zechowski")
edited by "Towarzystwo Reklamowe Tilosz i Ska"
ISBN 83-916510-0-2

It is a very interesting book, containing more than these illustrations, and a lot of text along with drawings and stories behind them.

Stefan Zechowski was born in 1912 nearby Kielce, in Poland, and died in 1984.
He lived of illustration, but was not too lucky with his own art, so he didn't reach much acclaim.

There is not much on the web about him, but on  
http://www.muzeumkielce.net/prezentacje/zechowski/zechowski.html
I managed to find a short bio:
