       Hybrid vintage virtual analogue soft synthesizer. (is not  
       just another VA synth, is much more!) 
                 
       Features: 
                 
       o  Two oscillators, 4 octaves selector, output level, saw  
          and pulse waveforms,pulse width control on osc 1, fine  
          tuning up to 1 octave on osc 2.    
       o  Noise generator.     
          One LFO with rate from 0.1 to 100 Kz!,    
          MIDI sync from 32/4 to 1/36,       
          Filter cutoff and osc pitch destinations, 
          6 shape modes, sample&hold. 
       o  Multimode "vintage" resonant filter with keyfollowing   
          function,LP,BP,HP.   
       o  Two envelope generators, VCA and Filter control, with   
          REPEAT function (loop the envelope).      
       o  Polyphonic mode (2 to 16 voices) and mono legato mode   
          with tunable glide effect.  
       o  MIDI learn on every knob to freely assign any MIDI CC.  
       o  MIDI velocity sensitivity adjustable indipendently for  
          VCA and Filter envelope.    
       o  Master volume.       
       o  Master tuning.       
       o  Virtual keyboard with hide/show button.   
       o  128 presets factory bank.   
       o  Random presets generator.   
       o  Saving default setting on registry option.       
       o  Compatible with any SampleRate. Eg: 44.1, 48, 96...     
       o  Support for SSE, MMX and P4 instructions. 
       o  VolumeType: High, Normal, Soft. (For use with those     
          VST-Hosts with too high volume)
