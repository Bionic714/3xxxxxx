AimOne Video Converter v2.03
AimOne Video Converter is a powerful batch Video converter supporting nearly all video format, including AVI, MPEG, ASF/WMV, RM, DivX. With it, you can easily convert AVI to MPEG/VCD/SVCD/DVD/RM/WMV, ASF/WMV to AVI/MPEG/VCD/SVCD/DVD, AVI/MPEG/ASF/WMV to AVI, AVI/MPEG/ASF/WMV to ASF/WMV, all to RealMedia. 
 

Features:
Convert AVI, ASF, WMV to Standard MPEG-1 
Convert AVI, ASF, WMV to MPEG-1 with VCD extensions(VCD). 
Convert AVI, ASF, WMV to Standard MPEG-2 
Convert AVI, ASF, WMV to MPEG-2 Super Video CD extensions(SVCD). 
Convert AVI, ASF, WMV to MPEG-2 with DVD extensions(DVD). 
Convert MPEG to AVI, ASF, to AVI, WMV to AVI 
Convert AVI to WMV, MPEG to WMV 
Convert AVI to RealMedia, ASF to RealMedia, WMV to RealMedia, MPEG to RealMedia 
Convert Real Media(.rm, .rmvb) to AVI, MPEG, WMV 
Convert Quick Time(.mov, .qt) to AVI, MPEG, WMV, RealMedia 
Support batch Convert. 
System Requirements:
Microsoft Windows 98/ME/2000/XP/2003

AVI (DivX, XviD, MS MPEG4, Uncompressed, Cinepak and other); 
MPEG (MPEG-1 and MPEG-2 Video);
