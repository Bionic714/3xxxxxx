Here's a nice package for you to remember the beloved past. Games ranging from early 80's to around 2000.




Some notes:

- IMPORTANT: I noticed a small amount of these games contain viruses, however the viruses are as old as the games themselves. They will probably do no harm on a WinXP-system. Within a properly configured DOSbox, you're safe no matter what. If you're unsure, don't run games that your antivirus software complains about. 

- I can highly recommend DOSbox (emulation) for running most of these games. DOSbox can be found at http://dosbox.sourceforge.net

- The rar-files are not compressed because inside are over 2800 zipfiles, one for each game. I put them in a rar container because some torrentclients don't appreciate >1000 files... blablabla, you know where I'm going with this.

- If anyone wonders where the files come from - they come from Eggmans Emulation Archive, I bought the dvd's myself, well worth it ;)

- I will seed this 24/7 on a 30mbit upstream for a couple of weeks, depending on popularity. Please leave a comment, I believe appreciation is the reason people upload stuff. 

- Dear god, forgive me for uploading stuff that is too old to be found in retail stores.
