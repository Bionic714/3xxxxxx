THE GREAT COLLECTION OF SWEDISH ROCK
This is a collection of Swedish rock sung in english. You'll find no ABBa, Roxette or Ace of Base here. Insted you find the best of Swedish rock from the past 20 years. Among the bands you find Monster and Union Carbide Production who was the bands before Moneybrother repectivley The Soundtrack of Our Lives. There are no full albums just songs from diffent albums. 

If you like the music please buy it.
