Ringtones Collection

The MIDI folder has ringtones ready to put right onto pretty much
any phone on the market.

The MMF folder has ringtones that are in the MMF format that play
on some phones, to convert to MIDI to use on any phone:

TO CONVERT MMF TO MIDI:
=======================
Open psmplay.exe
Open the MMF ringer you want
Choose the File(F) menu
Choose Create MIDI
