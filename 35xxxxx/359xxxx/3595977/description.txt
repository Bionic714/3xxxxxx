                       Description
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Over the course of this year, the planet will undergo remarkable changes: 
tumultuous weather, new species discovered, others lost, resources consumed 
and millions of new humans added to the population.

This fast-paced documentary will explore the effect humans, devastating floods, 
raging tsunamis, deadly droughts and remarkable new technologies, such as new 
fuel sources, have on the way people relate to the world. With unique access, 
Earth Report takes the pulse of the planet as never before, and presents an 
assessment of the current health of our world. The program tracks everything 
from the impact of natural disasters and shifting temperatures of the sea, to 
changes in the animal kingdom and the number of humans added to the population 
last year.
