Clockwork Productions Presents: Best of 2006			
150 songs from 150 artists, all released in 2006. Genres ranging from piano-based pop music to guitar-based pop-rock, garage rock, surf, power-pop, punk, stoner and metal, from country-rock/alt. country/americana, singer/songwriters and bluegrass to blues, jazz, gypsy, klezmer and tango.
