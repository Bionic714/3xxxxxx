DesktopX is a desktop enhancement utility designed specifically for Microsoft Windows. It integrates seamlessly into the operating system giving Windows a host of new features.
Widgets and gadgets are mini-applications that run on the desktop. It might be something as simple as an analog clock or a weather reader, or might be more complex. One of Stardock?s most powerful programs, DesktopX even allows for the creation of entire desktops.
