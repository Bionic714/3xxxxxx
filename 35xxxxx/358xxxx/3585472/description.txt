Beet, Kiss, Poala, and Milfa continue their journey to Grancista. However, along the way their ship is attacked by a new Vandel, the Dark Dungeon Warden Vaus, who takes particular interest in Beet.

The Beet Warriors escape with most of the crew to a nearby island where they meet Leon, the only son of the Lord of Shantigo, a town of Tengeki. What mysteries and new adventures lie ahead for the Beet Warriors?
