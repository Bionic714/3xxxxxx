                                 What is it?                           

            Awave Studio is a multi-purpose audio tool that reads a    

              veritable host of audio carrying file formats from       

              different platforms, synthesizers, trackers, mobile      

          phones... you name it! It can be used in a variety of ways:  

           as an audio file format converter, as an audio editor, an   

             audio and MIDI player, and, last but not least, as a      

          wavetable synthesizer instrument editor & format converter.  

           Think of it as the swiss army knife for anyone working in   

                      digital audio or with synthesizers!              

                                                                       

                 Here are just a few examples of common uses:          

                                                                       

           Do you have a strange audio file that you want to use but   

          can't figure out how to convert it into any sensible format  

          that can be used by the other programs that you use? Maybe   

            you even have a large number of files that you need to     

          convert or process in some way? Chances are good that Awave  

                            Studio can help you!                       

                                                                       

           Are you a composer? Ever wanted to take advantage of all    

           those free instruments available on the web but found it    

          impossible to find any in the format used by your favourite  

          synthesizer? Or perhaps you have a set of sample libraries   

          that you purchased for your old synth and want to use them   

            on your new one too? Awave Studio reads the instrument     

           formats of most commercial synthesizers and soundcards on   

            the market, and it can also write to a many of them! It    

             also supports the standard SDS and SMDI protocols for     

                   transferring samples over MIDI or SCSI.             

                                                                       

              Ever wanted to play a MIDI song that comes with an       

           'instrument bank' that were created for another wavetable   

           sound card than yours? Awave Studio cannot only make high   

            quality conversions between the most of the major sound    

          cards; it can also load both the actual MIDI song plus the   

           instruments and play it directly for you! And that's not    

           the end of it - it can also can do very high quality MIDI   

           song to waveform (audio clip) conversions! (e.g. convert    

                           your song to an .MP3).                      

                                                                       

          Need an audio editor? You can do such tasks as resampling,   

              fading in and out, normalizing amplitudes, applying      

                        effects, and much, much more.                  

                                                                       

               Want an instrument editor for your synthesizer or       

            wavetable sound card? You get editors for a vast set of    

           instrument synthesis parameters. You can also 'customize'   

               it for use as an editor for a particular synth.
