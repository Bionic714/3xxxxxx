         ?  SynthMaker is a graphical programming environment for  ?
         ?  creating and  manipulating digital  audio. Using  the  ?
         ?  software  you  have  complete  flexibility  to create  ?
         ?  exactly  the  kind of  sound  you want.  You  can add  ?
         ?  customised controls to modify parameters in real time  ?
         ?  and group  these controls  together to  make powerful  ?
         ?  user interfaces.                                       ?
         ?                                                         ?
         ?  Your  completed  creations can  then  be exported  as  ?
         ?  completely  independent  VST  instrument  or   effect  ?
         ?  plugins. These  plugins can  then be  used in  a wide  ?
         ?  variety of recording applications for composing        ?
         ?  your own songs or musical arrangements.                ?
         ?                                                         ?
         ?                                                         ?
         ?  Changes in v1.0.1                                      ?
         ?                                                         ?
         ?  This contains some important fixes and  optimisations  ?
         ?  including changes to the way that triggered float and  ?
         ?  int types are incorporated into the stream code. This  ?
         ?  was  causing some  bad glitches  with multiple  notes  ?
         ?  playing close together during playback in a host.      ?
