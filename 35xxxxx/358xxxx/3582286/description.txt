DESCRIPTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Warplanes of the Luftwaffe is the most complete volume available on the military 
aircraft of the Third Reich. Hundreds of photographs are complemented by color 
illustrations to provide detailed visual profiles of the aircraft. The 
combination of detailed text and specifications and superb illustrations 
provides a unique reference work to the aircraft, their histories, their 
achivements and their colors. (back-cover)
