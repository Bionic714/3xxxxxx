BBER!  This is a classic movie that I ripped from VHS that I bought from a video store that was going out of business.  This is a hard, EXTREMELY funny, comedy to find.  Here is a brief recap of what it is about:

An advertising firm, desperate to keep an account from a financially-ailing brewery, concocts a macho ad campaign centering on three losers who inadvertently prevent a robbery at a bar.
