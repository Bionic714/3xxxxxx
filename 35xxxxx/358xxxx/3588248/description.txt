Director: Katsuhito Ishii
Writer: Katsuhito Ishii
Cast: Masatoshi Nagase, Tadanobu Asano, Yoshio Harada, Tatsuya Gashuin
Japanese audio
ENGLISH subtitles


Seven characters, introduced at the start of the film, get thrown together into the same hotel room: a yakuza who's stolen a suitcase of money from his boss, his ex-girlfriend, her boyfriend, the yakuza sent to retrieve the briefcase, another assasin sent to kill them, master voyeur Captain Banana and his new apprentice.
