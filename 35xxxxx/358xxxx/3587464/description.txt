All of Shankly's 2006 compliations in all their glory - I challenge to to find a better mix of the best music of the past year! 

Over 600 (619 to be exact) of the years best tracks - mostly indie, but a fair enough spread of style to please almost anyone. If you can't find something here that you love - give up listening to music!

Look out for more in 2007. 

If you want just the December mixtapes, go to: http://thepiratebay.org/tor/3587451/Shankly__s_Best_of_2006_December

And Shankly's end of year best of's are at: http://thepiratebay.org/tor/3587455/Shankly__s_Best_of_2006_-_End_of_Year_Charts_(not_to_be_mi
