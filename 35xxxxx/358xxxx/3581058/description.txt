Tug is a one man band adn has many years of activity in the Greek diy scene. He is playing a mix of punk rock and more alternative styles, he ussually plays with Greek lyrics but sometimes he also sings english. Here you can find an unbelievable cover of Tom Wait's Lullaby song but in a punk way!
He has released many demos through the years and made concerts all over Greece. This release is a cd he made back in 2003.
