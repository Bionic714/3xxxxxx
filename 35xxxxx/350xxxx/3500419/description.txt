Johnny Cash - A Concert Behind Prison Walls (DVDRip, DivX)


A Concert Behind the Prison Walls features Cash performing for 
inmates inside the Tennessee State Penitentiary in Nashville. 
Shot in 1976, this is one of only two prison concerts Cash ever 
shot for broadcast and features legendary performers Johnny 
Cash, Linda Rondstadt and Roy Clark performing their greatest 
hits. The concert aired on national television, and this 
Collector?s edition video features rare, live performances of 
mega hits Folsom Prison Blues, Sunday Morning Coming Down, Boy 
Named Sue, Love Has No Pride, and more.
