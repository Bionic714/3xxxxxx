In this album, Rudiger Gleisberg provides us with a series of tracks performed live, besides three unreleased pieces so far. Known thanks to his mastery in destroying the frontier between Classical Music and such trends as Space Music or New Instrumental Music, the selection of tracks that constitutes this album will not deceive his usual followers, and it means a magnificient opportunity for those who do not know him to get a precise idea about his style. By using the synthesizers with the grandeur of a classical orchestra, something that only a few artists know how to do (for instance, Vangelis), Gleisberg takes us through journeys to wondrous worlds, and re-creates situations where feelings are manifested in all their depth.
