Startup guide:

1. Fuel pumps upper position

2. Turn battery and generator on (47 then 46) (upper pos) 

3. Turn starter on(upper pos) 

4. keep starter spinning until electrics is 40-60 ams (marked - left pos) 

5. Starter swtich to the left until started 

6. Starter off (down)
