                                [PLAYING INSTRUCTIONS]

Click mouse1 to the place you want to fish and then [siirry]. When you have arrived then on the right side of the screen click [kairaa]. When you have drilled the hole into ice click [laske]. When the fish is trying to eat the bait click mouse 1. button. If you want change the bait click mouse 2 onto bait picture. The green bait is for pikes(hauki) and perch´s(ahven). They are rare but usally big. Rest of baits are almost for all fishes. with starting bait you could catch fast small fishes and sometimes mediums size of fishes (it depends on your place of course). The another yellow one is for medium size of fish and for whitefish(siika). Click both mouse buttons to see water level -map. the more deeper water the bigger fish you could catch but more rarely than small ones.
