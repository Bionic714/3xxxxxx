  Here's another quick blast of funk, thump and bang, mixing up the old
  with the new.  From the Wipeout memories of FSOL's We Have Explosive
  through the harmonised prog of Dirty Vegas and onto the dirty funk of 
  Remy vs Klinkenberg and Hystereo, we take you from the brash to the
  lush and back again.  We change tack with the breaks of Forme and into
  the home straights with the thumping threesome of Carola, Vitalic and
  the unpronounceable Valentino Kanzyani.  
  
  Normal service resumed, more to follow shortly, stay tuned.......
