Dirty Mary Crazy Larry (1974)



# Actors: Peter Fonda, Susan George, Adam Roarke, Kenneth Tobey, Eugene Daniels, 

# Directors: John Hough

Plot Synopsis: Mary and Larry are two lovers who, with Larry\'s ace mechanic, kidnap the daughter of a grocery story owner, and make off with the ransom. They are chased over hill, over vale by the cops, who deploy everything from 426 Hemis to helicopters to stop Larry\'s Dodge Charger. Except Mary was not with them when the kidnap took place.
