Eddie Angel is lead guitarist for Los Straitjackets, Americas premier instrumental combo. He has been a devoted Link Wray fan for over 20 years. He learned to play Rawhide, Run Chicken Run and Jack the Ripper in the nightclubs of Washington D.C., Liink's old stomping grounds. This is his tribute to the worlds coolest rock-n-roll guitarist.
