Supernatural is an American paranormal/dark fantasy/horror/thriller/drama-themed television series that debuted on September 13, 2005 on the WB Television Network, and is now part of the new CW's lineup (the show is aired on ITV in the UK, TV3 in Ireland & Network Ten in Australia). The show follows brothers Sam and Dean Winchester, who travel across the country in a black 1967 Chevy Impala investigating paranormal events and other unexplained occurrences. The show has been described by many as a cross between The X-Files and the film Frailty.
