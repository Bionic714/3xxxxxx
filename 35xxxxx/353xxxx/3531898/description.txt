  ==================
 
  Get ready for the ultimate in hardcore, first person bloodshed!

  Quake 3: Arena sets new standards in PC gaming with some of the most amazing 
  graphics and hardcore multi-player action ever seen.  With new powerful 
  weapons along with some of the old classics, you can lay waste to the 
  battlefield as you strive to be #1

 
  Game Features:
  ==============  

  * Completely new graphics engine developed by ID Software using the latest
    technology to achieve both smooth and curved surfaces, realistic textures
    and enviormental effects such as fog and flowing lava just to name a few.

  * 3 Different player classes to choose from. Light, Medium, and Heavy, all
    with their own strengths and weakness including armor protection and 
    player speed.

  * A deadly arsenal of weapons ranging from shotguns, to rocket launchers,
    lightning guns to flame throwers, and even the dreaded BFG. Choose the 
    right weapon for the right situation, and you just might survive.

  * A vast array of items to help you stay alive. With health kits and body 
    armor to better shield you, along with the feared "Quad Damage", with
    which you can cause havok upon your foes.

  * State of the Art Enemy Intelligence. Play against computer bots and see 
    if you have what it takes to defeat these deadly drones.

  * Full support for 3d Accelerators including 3dfx, 3dNow!, OpenGL and
    Direct 3d, however Quake 3 is optimized for the new Voodoo 3 line of 
    accelerator cards
