     The Liquid Bundle gives you a variety of incredible effects. 
     This bundle include Liquid Chorus, Liquid Flanger, Liquid 
     Compressor, Liquid Delay and Liquid Reverb. These tools are 
     ideal for the sound designer, mixer and musician. Although 
     these plug-ins provide intuitive and simple controls, the 
     sound quality they deliver is full and warm. The Liquid 
     plug-ins operate efficiently to avoid over-taxing your CPU 
     resources. Designed by musicians for musicians, the Liquid
     Bundle is an essential tool for your virtual studio. 

     Contains the following VST Plugins:

       + Liquid Chorus 
       + Liquid Flanger 
       + Liquid Compressor 
       + Liquid Delay 
       + LiquidVerb 
    
     Note:  Due to a bug in the developers code, the delay plugin
            will not operate properly under Fruity Loops.
