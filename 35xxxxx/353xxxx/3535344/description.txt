Kingdom... was the first banned release in the history of nsbm.Released for the first time on cd
back in 1995 by a Greek label called Molon Lave in cooperation with the German
Sounphage International.In the artwork of this release was the symbol of the
swastika combined with the baphomet and some demeaning but true comments about
Christians and Jews.All these, together with some statements and an interview for
the first time in the Greek porn magazine Metal Hammer was the beginning of a hard war
against the band...And up to now, 11 years after this release that war has not yet ended...
