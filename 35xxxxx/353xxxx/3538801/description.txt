Hellgate London Fansite Kit.

Flagship studios released this on Oct 9 2006, available for download at fileplanet. Torrents are better. 

The original download was a single 1,66 GB rar file, and the total size after unpacking was 2,56 GB. I repacked all the images into separate rar files, shrinking the total size to 1,68 GB. This way, you can choose the files you want and which you don\'t.

Content:

/Art - 18 individually rar:ed art images, in psd and tga formats.
/Documentation - 4 .doc files with info about the project and the companies
/Logos - Namco, Flagship, Hanbitsoft and Hellgate logos, in ai and psd formats. Individually rar:ed.
/Podcasts - Seven podcasts. Mp3 format.
/Screenshots - 174 screenshots. Jpg and bmp formats. Individually rar:ed.
/Video/Cinematics - Two cinematics from E3 2005 and 2006. HD, wmv format.
/Video/Gameplay - Six gameplay montages. Wmv format.

Sorry about archiving all the images, but it really shrunk the file sizes.
