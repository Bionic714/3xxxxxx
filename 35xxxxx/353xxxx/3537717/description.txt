Microsoft AutoRoute 2006

The customisable trip planning software that helps you get accurate directions, easily explore new areas and find the services you want and need along the way. With more personalized trip planning features than online mapping sites, and map coverage of 27 European countries, AutoRoute 2006 makes it easy to plan your trip, your way.


# NEW! Voice Prompted Directions.
Verbal turn-by-turn directions provide an easy-to-use, hands-free guidance solution.

# NEW! Dynamic Turn View with Rotate-able Maps.
Easily see upcoming turns with an automatically magnified view.

# Personal Driving Profile.
Plan your way with flexible trip planning options like custom start times, driving speeds, rest stops ? even preferred types of roads!

# ENHANCED! Updated maps.
Trust the most accurate maps covering 3.4 million miles in 27 European countries.
