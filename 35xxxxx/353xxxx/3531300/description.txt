TrueCombat: Elite is a free modification for the free game Wolfenstein: Enemy Territory. It is a CQB-oriented FPS with modern weapons. Windows, Linux and Mac are all supported. 
 
http://www.truecombatelite.net

This is the full release as a zip file, unzip to your ET directory. Make sure to rename/delete your earlier version (0.48) tcetest folder first!

See you on the servers!
