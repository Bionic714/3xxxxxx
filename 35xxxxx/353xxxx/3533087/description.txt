Small program I made for Calculation of your Crimeskill in the web-based game TheCrims. (http://www.thecrims.com)

Just enter your atributes, weapon and armour and press run and you will get the calculation.

As long as your skill is above the difficulty of the robbery you will have a 99 % success chanse!

The program is in swedish for the time being, I will make an English version soon.

Just unrar,
run setup
and Enjoy Robbing that poor defenseless lady.
