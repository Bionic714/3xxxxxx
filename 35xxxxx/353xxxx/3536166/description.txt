Immediately after 9/11, the verdict was in: A combination of fires and structural damage caused the progressive collapse of the Twin Towers and WTC 7.  For many that watched the events in horror, however, the striking similarity to controlled demolitions was immediately apparent...and persistently haunting.
  Now, after five years of research, the hypothesis that controlled demolitions brought these buildings down has gained significant attention.
  Improbable Collapse invites viewers to critically examine the demolition hypothesis, and the growing phenomenon of doubt surrounding these events and their aftermath
