This bootleg is a concoction of the original dialogue and bits of score, along with assorted remixes, mash-ups and white labels - many from the Ulladubulla albums.  It's all been mixed together to produce an hour-long continuous story (like the original) but with remixed versions of all the different pieces of music and songs so it sounds much more modern and darker than the original.
