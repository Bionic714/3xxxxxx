Elegant and deep sequences overlay picturesque melodic structures that weave in and out of consciousness. Merging New Age Berlin School stylings with New Age Space music sensibility, the Northern California duo of Craig Padilla and Skip Murphy explore new sonic territory, while keeping firmly rooted to the influential New Age music from the golden age of sequencing. Using the latest virtual instruments alongside rare analog synthesizers, this music explodes old genre barriers while simultaneously forging new sonic possibilities.
