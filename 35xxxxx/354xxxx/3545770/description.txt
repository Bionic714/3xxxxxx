ROM files for various Texas Instruments calculators. These can be used with an emulator such as:

  Virtual TI (All models) - http://www.ticalc.org/archives/files/fileinfo/84/8442.html
  Timu (89,92,92+) - http://lpg.ticalc.org/prj_tiemu/
  TiLem (82,83,83+,85,86) - http://lpg.ticalc.org/prj_tilem/
  PindurTI (83,83,83+) - http://users.hszk.bme.hu/~pg429/pindurti/
