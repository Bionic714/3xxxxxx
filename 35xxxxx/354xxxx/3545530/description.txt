size=4] Michael Flatley ?Celtic Tiger? [/size]

Michael Flatley, de internationaal erkende 'meester van de dans' is terug op het
podium met een splinternieuwe dansshow 'Celtic Tiger'. Met ontzettend sterke muziek,
ongelofelijke kostuums en adembenemende special effects is celtic Tiger een episch meesterwerk,
die de Ierse historie combineert met dans en muziek.Deze film werd gemaakt in Budapest en geeft je
een inspirerende kijk in de creatie van de Celtic Tiger show en laat je een verbluffende opening zien met
meer dan 100.000 mensen, waardoor Celtic Tiger de best bezochte dansshow ooit werd.

[size=4] Michael Flatley ?Gold? [/size]


Gold: a celebration of Michael Flatley" contains all of Michael Flatley's greatest hits. And since I'm a huge
fan of Michael Flatley this was a videotape I just had to buy. This video was released in November 2000
and it contains some new as well as some old stuff. It contains pieces of Riverdance, Lord of the Dance and
Feet of Flames. But it also shows us some footage of Michael Flatley's last and final show: The Feet of Flames
World Tour. That footage was shot in Budapest, Hungary in front of 100,000 people. This video contains also
a preview of Michael Flatley's new film and some special footage of an old performance from Michael back in
1987 and a reunion with the Chieftains in 1998. I hope that in the future they will release a video of the entire
Feet of Flames World Tour show, but for now we'll just have to do with the 10 pieces of footage that are on
this tape. For every fan of Michael Flatley this is definitely a must-see!
