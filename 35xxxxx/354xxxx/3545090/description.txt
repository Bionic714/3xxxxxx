A collection of John Shuttleworth material. John Shuttleworth is the (geniusly gentle) comic creation of Graeme Fellows, and features most on UK's BBC Radio 4, the birthplace of much comic talent. The folders are:

: Christmas Special

: John Shuttleworth and Family

: Radio Shuttleworth, Series One and Two

: Seven Songs By Sunset

: The Shuttleworths, Series One, Two, Three, Four
