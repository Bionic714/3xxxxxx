Hack360 4.1

The process is made from extracted firmware and copies are created that already include dvd original key, thanks to ppf made the modifications necessary in firmware to load backup.

- Added to the Firmware Xtreme 4.1A (Full Speed by default) and 4.1B (Quiet by default)
