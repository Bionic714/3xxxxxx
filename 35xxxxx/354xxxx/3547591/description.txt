Count Dracula has been kidnapped by the dreadful vampire-hunter Doctor Lifelust! He plans to use vampire blood to create an elixir of life so he can live forever! Now it\'s up to Dracula\'s twins, Drac and Dracana, each with their own special moves and abilities, to bite back and save their father in this ghastly platform adventure! Collect blood rubies to gain magic abilities and cast fireball spells as you experience the gorgeous 3D world of Transylvania!
