Lord Peter Wimsey Investigates - The Unpleasantness at the Bellona Club
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
When aristocratic amateur-sleuth Lord Peter Wimsey finds General Fentiman
dead in his favourite chair, the dignified calm of the Bellona Club is shattered.
A straightforward death by natural causes? Perhaps. but why can no-one
remember seeing the General the day he died? And who is the mysterious
Mr Oliver?. Moving between London and Paris, salon and suburbs, Lord
Peter Wimsey unfolds an intriguing case and reveals all in one of
Dorothy L. Sayers\' most popular mysteries.

A full-cast dramatisation of the classic mystery by Dorothy L. Sayers
published in 1928.

First broadcast from 23 - 28 June 1975. Six episodes of approx 30 minutes
each in high quality MP3.
