Adrian Chiles presents highlights of the day\'s games, 
plus a review of Saturday\'s action.

In the tie of the weekend, two former European 
champions go head-to-head, with Manchester United 
welcoming Liverpool to their Old Trafford home.

Both sides are used to success, with 33 league titles 
between them, and three invaluable Premiership points 
is the prize on offer in Manchester.

In the day\'s other match, promoted Reading take on 
Arsenal in the clubs\' first-ever league meeting.
