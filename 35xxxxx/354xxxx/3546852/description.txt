B/W maps. 1:50000. From mid 80's. Index map included
Usefull for Twilight 2000 rpg first adventures and the campaign in Poland
I couldn't find a legend. 
When in Photoshop chose: Print size (or similiar) and enlarge to 200 % and put in papersheet "A2" size if optional. Then you'll have the right size of it. If you encounter problem open the files try different types of imageprogrames. 
Some files are somewhat corrupted and the doesn't work in Photoshop 5.5 but in later versions.
About one third of the files are disorderly
I packed them in a .rar beacause of the corrupted files. 
Some files may be missing.
