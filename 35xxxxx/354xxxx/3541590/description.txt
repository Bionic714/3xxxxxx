For those who have downloaded the German version of Gothic 3, this is the Speech_English.pak, i.e. the speech file of English version of Gothic 3. So you don\'t need to download the whole image of English version again.

Just put it in \\Gothic III\\Data, then open \\My Documents\\gothic3\\UserOptions.ini, change

VoiceLanguage=German
SubtitleLanguage=German

to

VoiceLanguage=English
SubtitleLanguage=English

save and play.
