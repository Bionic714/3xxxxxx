Xmal Deutschland was a musical group from Hamburg, Germany. Founded in 

1980 as an all-girl band, they became successful outside their native 

country. Vocalist Anja Huwe was often compared to contemporaries like 

Siouxsie Sioux (Siouxsie & the Banshees). Xmal Deutschland's last album 

was released in 1989.

Albums:

1. Fetish, 1983
2. Tocsin, 1984
3. Peel Seesion, 1985
4. Devils, 1989
