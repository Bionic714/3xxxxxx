The Birthday Party was an Australian post punk rock group, active from 

1977 to 1983.

Despite their limited success, the creative core of the Birthday Party 

have gone on to acclaimed careers: singer and songwriter Nick Cave, 

multi-instrumentalist Mick Harvey and singer, songwriter and guitarist 

Rowland S. Howard.
