CAN I HEAR YOU SCREAM NORWAY VOL 1

This is a collection of both signed and unsigned norwegian bands within the genres of metal, punk, alternative and scandinavian hard rock. They all have in common that they may not be that well known, but are still potentially great bands. Some of these songs are leagl free copies downloaded from official sites and music sites, some are ripped from albums. The oldest songs are from 2003, but most are from 2005/2006.

Hope you enjoy the music and hopefully there will come a volume 2...!
