         ÛÛ  The Maggots have been playing their brand of
         ÛÛ  rockin' garage punk since 1999. The main influence
         ÛÛ  behind The Maggots lay in the original garage punk
         ÛÛ  from the 60's but that's not the whole picture.
         ÛÛ  Years - hell, decades! - of dedicated love for
         ÛÛ  everything that is real rock'n'roll is rolled into
         ÛÛ  the Maggot sound!
         ÛÛ  The Maggots has been touring Germany, Spain,
         ÛÛ  Italy, England, Scandinavia, Belgium, France,
         ÛÛ  Holland. They've been touring with the Fleshtones
         ÛÛ  and successfully backed up Jeff Conolly as "Lyres"
         ÛÛ  in Sweden.
         ÛÛ  'Monkey Time!' is the fourth Maggots album and
         ÛÛ  we're happy to release it on Screaming Apple
         ÛÛ  records - a label that goes way back and who's
         ÛÛ  love of the garage/punk sounds is well documented.
         ÛÛ  On this album The Maggots have added a fourth
         ÛÛ  member on Farfisa organ to spice up the sounds
         ÛÛ  some more.
         ÛÛ  'Monkey Time!' is a vital record, trying to
         ÛÛ  capture the timeless qualities of all good
         ÛÛ  rock'n'roll while paying the dues both to the
         ÛÛ  original 50's/60's garage/r&r/beat and to the
         ÛÛ  member's own roots in the first 80's garage wave.
         ÛÛ  The record consists of 9 original songs and 3
         ÛÛ  covers where we do our takes on some old fave
         ÛÛ  songs. We had a helluva fun recording this one -
         ÛÛ  hope that's evident upon listening!
         ÛÛ  The line-up will surely set more than one stage on
         ÛÛ  fire, and the new album is sure to appeal
         ÛÛ  togarage-a-holics and rock'n'roll fans everywhere.
         ÛÛ  The Maggots play the music they love, and have a
         ÛÛ  great time doing it! They are out to make you
         ÛÛ  move!
