Capcom's legendary fighting series heralds a new generation of games in Street Fighter EX3. Awe-inspiring realism and unbelievable graphics bring the world of Street Fighter to life in this amazing tag-team free-for-all. Choose from 16 characters to play 1-on-1, 2-on-1 and even 1-on-3 battles! Loaded with four modes of play and new enhancements like the Critical Parade and Emotional Flow super moves, Street Fighter EX3 is one of the first of its kind on PlayStation 2.
