Prime Suspect 8 - Sista Vittnet   

TV-serie med Helen Mirren.


Filmens handling:

Ett helt nytt fall med Helen Mirren som Superintendent Tennison.

Detective Superintendent Tennison takes over a murder investigation by pulling rank on a junior officer. Her team identify a chief suspect but are unable to find enough evidence for an arrest. When another murder occurs the arrest is secured, but Tennison is convinced that there's more here than meets the eye. When she tracks the murders to a protected war criminal she must gamble everything to bring the real culprit in.
