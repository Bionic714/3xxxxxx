Step out of the rush & let Freddy's mystic soul connection wash over you?

Pacific purveyors of hi-tek soul Fat Freddy's Drop are a seven-piece family of musicians who have grown up together in Wellington, New Zealand. Emerging through a multitude of incarnations from funk jam bands to reggae soundsystems, jazz improvisation to live techno, their eagerly awaited debut studio album is
'Based On A True Story', a fresh slice of future funk complete with surprises, fakes & freaky sidesteps...

100% New Zealand Music
