For english-only speaking:
This book is in german. English title would be: Time machines (Reichgerman flying saucers). This book by Norbert Jurgen-Ratthofer is one of rare attempts to decypher the story and especially the physics of german WW2 flying saucers. There were many designs of them during the war, some were using conventional while others used much more interesting electrogravitic propulsion. Book deals with the latter ones and author also makes claims that they were also capable doing some time jumps. 108 pages, many pictures of saucers from secret SS archive which landed into allied hands after the war. Worth a look even if you don't speak a word german. A short story about the german saucer happenings can be read from: http://www.galactic-server.net/rune/vril5.html

Fur Deutschsprachige:
Autor Norbert Jurgen-Ratthofer versuch im Zeitmachinen die Physik von Reichsdeutschen Flugscheiben zu entschlüsseln, namlich diese die mit elektrogravitische Antriebe ausgerüstet waren. Aus den Inhalt: Prinzip und Konstruktions-und Funktionsbeschreibung der Stationaren Zweigleisigen Zeitmaschine, Das Raumzeit-Schiff, Die reichsdeutschen Tachyonen-Elektrogravitations-Raumschiff-Geheimprojekte und die Zeitfallen. 108 Seiten, viele Abbildungen auch einige seltene von geheimen SS Archiv die nach dem Krieg ins Allierte Hande gefallen ist.
