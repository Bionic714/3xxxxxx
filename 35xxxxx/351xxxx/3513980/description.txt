For english-only speaking:
This book is a german translation of Aric Z. Leavitt's explosive book The Illuminati 4th Edition (1997) which exposes the history of this secret organization from its inception to current days. The Illuminati were designed with one purpose only - to take over the world primarily through the manipulation of politics and finance. This rare book goes deep into the philosophy of the power elite and how it has managed to manipulate the world for so long. 250 pages, some pictures. Worth a look even if you don't speak a word german.
