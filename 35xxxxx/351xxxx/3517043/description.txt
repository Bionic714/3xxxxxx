Jesters are entertainers of the court and are often summoned to try to lift the
king out of a bad mood. Their tricks and pranks can sometimes be cheap and
trivial. But nevertheless, they can even make the highest royalties bursts out
in a well needed laughter. Jesters are by no means stupid, on the contrary they
are cunning characters equipped with the fool's license.

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

JESTERS was founded in 2005 with the aim to release quality live performances.
The motivation for creating this group was based on the observation that people
value music video singles and live performances differently. Since we only do
live performances, everybody knows what to expect from us.
