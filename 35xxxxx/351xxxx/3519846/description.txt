Doc & Lena Selyanina
Distant Transmissions - Ambient Reflections on Bach
Musictrade release MT002
Originally released July 26, 2005
Lame HVBR, including hi-res cover artwork.

Three ambient reflections on J.S. Bach's music. 

This is the first album of Doc & Lena Selyanina from summer 2005. The music is deep, beatless ambient ranging from dark to bright in tones -  suitable for relaxation, meditation, deep listening, audio voyaging and creative visualization.

The original works that inspired and provided the tonal material for these tracks are: Invention No. 2 in C Minor & BWV 773 & Sinfonia No. 2 in C Minor, BWV 788 (Andromeda Calling), Prelude No.1 In C Major, BWV 870 (Carrier Waves) and "Ich ruf zu dir, Herr Jesu Christ", BWV 639 (Ich Rufe).
