Motorcycle Mania 1

Documentary from Discovery Channel about West Coast Choppers.


Step inside the garage of Jesse James as he puts the finishing touches on several custom-made choppers set for debut at Daytona Beach's Bike Week. A descendant of the famous outlaw of the same name, Jesse has garnered worldwide respect with his lean, mean choppers. Watch as he prepares several custom beauties for the over 600,000 bikers who will size up his creations. With so many bikes to finish, including his own, it's going to take a monumental effort to get the job done in time for the show.
