One Actor, One Room, Seven Characters: 9/11.

Traumatized by the September 11th attacks, one man struggles to dismantle 
official history, at the expense of his sanity and even his life. Grappling 
with multiple realities - and multiple personalities - he must retreat 
into his mind in pursuit of the truth. In a fictional film about 
non-fictional events, there is a place where belief and faith will blind 
you, where nothing is sacred, and to get there all you have to do is ask:

"Who Killed John O'Neill?"
