- ALT+TAB
  Using the ALT+TAB key combination to switch between applications may
  cause problems. We do not support the use of the ALT+TAB function.

- US Keyboard binding
  In the demo of Dark Messiah, your keyboard will be detected as a US keyboard. If you have
  an international keyboard, like the French keyboard, reassigning your keys will give you
  the US equivalent. For exemple binding "W" to crouch will read "Z" in the 
  the keyboard options' menu.
  
  French keyboard binding: 
  Z 		- Move Forward
  S 		- Move Backward
  Q 		- Strafe Left
  D 		- Strafe Right
  E 		- Interact with the environment
  F		- Kick
  C 		- Crouch
