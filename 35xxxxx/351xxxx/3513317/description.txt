Product Description
Until There\'s Nothing Left Of Us, featuring the single \"Lips Like Morphine\", is the latest offering from the Chicago based quintet. Kill Hannah\'s sexy songs and style to match have helped establish them in the arty alternative scene. Tirelessly touring, the band has a cult-like status to their die-hard army of fans--many inked with the band\'s distinctive heart in crosshairs logo.
