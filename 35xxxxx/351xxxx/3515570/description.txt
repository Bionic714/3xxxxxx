University professor initiates slaughter in a tourist hotel, killing 11 guests and employees. While she films its acts with a camera 8mm, esfaqueia it a victim after to another one, while they try to run away. Thirty and five years later, Matsumura (Kippei Shiina) is a director who wants to transform this crime into film. Entitulado Memory, it invites the actress Nagisa Sugiura (Yuuka) to interpret the heroine of its project. But, when the beginning of the filmings if approaches, Nagisa starts to have frightful hallucinations and dreams.
