    			      MOVIE Plot

	After a waterfront explosion, Verbal (Kevin Spacey), an eye-witness
	and participant tells the story of events leading up to the
	conflagration.
	The story begins when five men are rounded up for a line-up, and
	grilled about a truck hijacking (the usual suspects).
	Least pleased is Keaton (Gabriel Byrne) a crooked cop - exposed,
	indicted, but now desperately trying to go straight.
	The cops won't leave him alone, however, and as they wait for their
	lawyers to post bail, he is talked into doing one more job with the
	other four. All goes tolerably well until the influence of the
	legendary, seemingly omnipotent "Keyser Soze" is felt. Although set
	in the modern day, it has much of the texture of the forties, plus
	suspense, intrigue (a fairly high body count), and lots
	of twists in the plot.
