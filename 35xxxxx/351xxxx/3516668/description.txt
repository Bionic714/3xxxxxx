The Definative Science Fiction Television Encyclopedia (3rd Edition)

This is the 3rd Edition of this massive guide on science fiction television, the previous edition we released in November 2005. 

For this Edition we add a further 59 shows, details of which are in the introduction of the e-book.

Some info for you on this volume.

We now have 1067 TV Shows, 20,137 episode titles, and some 700 pictures, the book is now over 1.6 million words long, one of the largest single records on science fiction television ever published.

At 400 pages longer than the 2nd Edition, this work stands at 2907 pages long.
