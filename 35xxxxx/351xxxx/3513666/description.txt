Morning of the Earth is a 1972 classic surf film by Alby Falzon. Its success was much greater than Falzon or anyone expected and it boosted the profile of the surfers in the film.


Locations featured
Australia:
	Kirra, Queensland 
	Lennox Head, New South Wales 
	Broken Head, New South Wales 
	Angourie, New South Wales 
	Whale Beach, New South Wales (Sydney) 
Bali:
	Uluwatu 
Hawaii:
	Rocky Point 
	North Shore, Oahu 

Surfers featured
	Baddy Treloar 
	Barry Kanaiaupuni 
	Gerry Lopez 
	Michael Peterson 
	Mark Warren 
	Nat Young
