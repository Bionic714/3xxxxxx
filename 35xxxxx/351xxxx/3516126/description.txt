this is a package of all the software you will need to make professional music at home. you will of course need to buy a better sound card for better sound, but for now this will do. the stuff here is top notch. included is:

-cubase sx3-this is a sequencer and recording program-the core of your system. there are alot of fakes out there but this of works

-guitar rig 2-just plug your guitar into your cpu-no amp needed-and this thing gives you all the great amp sounds. a great program

-arturia vintage synths- these are all great and sound just like the originals

-native instruments package- synths and a sampler and a drum machine- all great

-novation bass station- great bass sounds

-korg legacy collection- more great synths

-dr-008-best drum machine you can get. i gave you plenty of drums too.

-vst/dx plugins- great quality plugins to sharpen your sound


enjoy !
