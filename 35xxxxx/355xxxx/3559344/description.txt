 Well well well, I know you didn't expect this release from us. But also SQ
 loves legends, and this is what I call a fucking legend: THE BEATLES!
 We present to you, this brand new album from The Beatles, called "LOVE".

 After being asked by the remaning Beatles, Lennon and Harrison, to make
 experimental mixes from the oringial master tapes for a collaboration with
 Cirque du Soleil, Sir George Martin (the legendary producer from The Beatles)
 and his son have been working with the entire archive of Beatles recordings
 to create LOVE.

 The result is an unprecendeted approach to the music. Using the master tapes
 at EMI's Abbey Road Studios, Sir Geroge and Giles have created a unique
 soundscape. The relase of this album, wich is also featured in the Cirque De
 Soleil/Beatles collaborative production of the same name at the Mirage Hotel
 in Las Vegas, has been much anticipated.

 "This music was designed for the LOVE show in LV but in doing so we've
 created a new Beatles album" he said. "The Beatles always looked for other
 ways of expressing themselves and this is another step forward for them."

 "We took all the Beatles catalogue from tape, the original four tracks, eight
 tracks and two tracks and used this pallete of sounds and music to create a
 sound bed. What people will be hearing on the album is a new experience, a
 way of re-living the whole Beatles musical lifespan in a very condensed
 period."

 This album will be released at 20-Nov-2006 worldwide, but we could't wait
 with this diamond! So, here it is, and enjoy the good old tunes :-)
