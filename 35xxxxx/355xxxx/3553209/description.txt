Hang onto your ski hats! Extreme snowmobilers show off their wildest moves in this action-packed video. In a stunt that\'s sure to go down in snowmobile history, Jay Quinlan succeeds at a back-flip. Featuring footage of powder and big-mountain riding in Canada, Utah, Alaska, Colorado and California, Slednecks 6 also includes stunts by riders Ryan Britt, Rob Alford, Heath Frisby, BJ Murray, Chris Burandt, Sam Carver and many others.
