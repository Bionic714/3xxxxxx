Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose: The Forgotten Hero is a 2004 in film directed by Shyam Benegal and starring Sachin Khedekar, Kulbhulshan Kharbanda, Rajit Kapoor and Divya Dutta.

The movie is about the last 5 years of the life of the Indian independentist Subhash Chandra Bose Netaji. It starts out at the point where Bose resigns from his position as the president of INC and ends with a radio announcement of his death. The music for the movie is by A. R. Rahman.
