The Taxman Cometh
Season 1, Episode 6
When Robin and his gang encounter Flaxton, a farmer, and his son while on their way to Nottingham it doesnt take them long to discover all is not as it seems. When they discover that Flaxton is actually a tax inspector, on his way to count the mountain of tax silver in Nottingham Castle a plan is hatched to allow Robin access to the castle vault.
