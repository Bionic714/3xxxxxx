Anywhere but Home is a live album and DVD by Rock band Evanescence, released in 2004 (see 2004 in music). It includes all four of their Fallen music videos, an hour of behind the scenes features, and a recording of a concert at the Zenith in Paris. It also includes a previously unreleased B-side on the CD, titled \"Missing\" (which also plays during the DVD\'s credits).

Personnel

    * Amy Lee - vocals, piano
    * Terry Balsamo - guitar
    * John LeCompt - guitar
    * William Boyd - bass
    * Rocky Gray - drums

Extras

You can locate a secret performance of Bring Me To Life in Las Vegas performed by the band in the DVD\'s main menu by moving the cursor to the lowest choice which is \"Behind the Scenes\" and then moving it two times to the left. The symbol of the previous album \"Fallen\" will be shown, taking you to the hidden footage.
