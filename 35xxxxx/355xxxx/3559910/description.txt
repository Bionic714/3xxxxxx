Walk & Follow is an addon designed for MSFS 2004. It allows to move in MSFS space fast and easy. Utilizing new technologies it brings no artifacts to scenery and doesn't interfere any other addons. It is small, fast and easy. 
BDS Walk and Follow addon includes two independent modules: Walk&Follow (exterior module) and CockpitWalk (VC module).
