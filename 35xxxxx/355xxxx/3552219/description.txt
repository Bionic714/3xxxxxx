Enhancing Your Experience is a short documentary made by Cheyenne "Lightswitch" Workman. It is a look into the behind-the-scenes of a Techno Hippy Crew party.

The Techno Hippy Crew is a group of open-minded individuals who are working to bring a conciousness back to the party scene in Edmonton.
