TITLE: DJ Nino Brown & DJ Samrai - Blazin' 5 The Mixtape
GENRE: Hip Hop/RnB
BITRATE: 320Kbps
RELEASE DATE: 23/01/06
RIP DATE: 07/12/06



Blazin' 5: The Mixtape is the much anticipated fifth volume in this groundbreaking hip hop series. Australia's biggest R&B and hip hop Club DJ Nino Brown mixes up an upbeat party selection of the hottest hip hop in the clubs right now with tracks from Rihanna, Ludacris, Kanye West, Akon, The Game and Snoop Dogg. 

The recent winner of the ITF Turntable National Championships DJ Samrai brings us a mix of current hip hop bangers and some old favourites with tracks by Naughty By Nature, Terror Squad, LL Cool J, Memphis Bleek, Phrase and heaps more.
