A film about sking and the people doing it, we get to	
know the places were we ski and the people infront    	
aswell as behind the camera. We reflect on the        	
process of making a film together, the good times and 	
the bad times. The relationsships between the whole   	
team behind a ski film. And that it all comes down to 	
having a good time while doing it!
