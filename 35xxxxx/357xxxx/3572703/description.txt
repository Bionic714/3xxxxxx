American Soccer - A show that reviews what's happening in soccer in the United States from the World Cup to youth soccer.
American Soccer covers all levels of soccer with weekly updates from Major League Soccer, USL First Division and the growing number of US players abroad.
The show is hosted by veteran soccer announcer Phil Schoen with Ray Hudson and Lindsey Dean who provide analysis, debate, discussion and interviews...
