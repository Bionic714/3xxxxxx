Armenian Patriarchate Choir - Nativity Of Christ

A live performance of hymns from the Feast of the Nativity, sung by the choir of the Armenian Patriarchate in Jerusalem, and recorded in the Keshet Studio. His Beatitude Archbishop Torkom Mannogian, Armenian Patriarch of Jerusalem celebrating.

1. Christ is Born, Magnificat (Komitas)
2. O Wonderful Mystery, This Day the Paternal Voice (Komitas)
3. Praise the Lord, O Jerusalem (Ekmalian)
4. Holy God, Again in Peace Let Us Beseech the Lord (Komitas)
5. Greet One Another, Christ Among Us (Komitas)
6. Sanctus (Komitas)
7. In All Things Blessed Art Thou (Komitas)
8. In All Things Blessed Art Thou (Komitas)
9. Amen, Blessed Be The Lord
10. Christ is Born, Mystery Great (Komitas)
11. Glory to God in the Highest, The Sun of Righteousness (W. Sarxian)
12. Christ is Born, This Day is the Feast of the Nativity (Shahan, Berberian)
13. Who is like Unto the Lord Our God (Komitas)
14. We Give Thanks to Thee, O Lord (Komitas)
