Red Steel is a game published by Ubisoft for Nintendo's Wii console. 
It was developed by the Ubisoft Paris studio and was unveiled in the May 2006 issue of Game Informer. Red Steel was the first game for the Wii platform to release in-game screenshots, thus giving the public an idea of what the console was capable of. It was released on November 19, 2006, in synchronisation with Nintendo's seventh generation
console, Wii.
