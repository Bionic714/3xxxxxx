Eye Candy is a set of filters that functions as a Photoshop-compatible plug-in. The list of filters includes Antimatter, Carve, Chrome, Cutout, Drop Shadow, Fire, Fur, Glass, Glow, HSB Noise, Inner Bevel, Jiggle, Motion Trail, Outer Bevel, Squint, Star, Swirl, Water Drops, and Weave. All the filters have zoomable and resizable previews with thumbnail navigation. Eye Candy users can apply effects based on transparency information from other layers. Slider controls give control freaks command over every aspect of their effects. New features include a Bevel Profile editor, color gradient editor, and unlimited undo/redo. In addition, you can now specify absolute units to create effects that are resolution-independent
