     This CD returns to the first's formula with progressive
     introduced to the mix.

     Some great nighttime tracks and outstanding morning tracks, some
     from familiar names like Sirius Isness some less so like Atma,
     an artist from Romania who's going to be really big soon with
     his distinctive sound. With names like Ultravoice, DTek and
     Sangeet on there it's a great chance for people to pick up new
     music that's just coming out and to encourage them to buy the
     artist's album.

     Each artist on the CD is also inteviewed in the magazine and
     there's interviews with Hom Mega's Intelabeam and Ace Ventura,
     Nano Records' Tristan, label features on AP Records, Com.Pact
     Records, Tribal Vision Records and Maia Records. Interviews with
     established names like Sangeet from Germany, Ultravoice from
     Israel and underground artists like Error Corrective from the
     UK.

     There's photospreads looking back to last Summer's parties at
     Glade, Sonica, Boom and photos from the recent Twisted Records
     10th anniversary party.

     With features on decor specialists DreamTeam, an interview with
     the visual artist Jarah Tree who did this issue's cover art. It
     is of the Earth mother Gaia and the environment, GM crops,
     bio-engineering and our future energy needs are the main feature
     of this issue.
