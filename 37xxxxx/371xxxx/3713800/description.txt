Take a trip across the USA without ever leaving your home! American Road Songs 
10 CD set collects 200 songs that pay tribute to some of America's beloved cities, 
states and locations. Most of the 200 original tracks are vintage country and western songs by legends including Gene Autry, Sons Of The Pioneers, Bob Wills, Hank Penny, Red Foley, and others. But you'll also find some cross-over hits by the likes of Nat King Cole, Dinah Shore and Glenn Miller. This boxed set features songs from the obvious to the obscure, with classic tracks throughout, and is an excellent overview of the melting pot of songs inspired by this great land.
