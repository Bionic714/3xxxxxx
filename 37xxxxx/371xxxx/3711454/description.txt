Homespun Fiddle Course DVD

Learn to Play Irish Fiddle

Taught By Kevin Burke
Level 2
Two-DVD series
Catalog Code: DVDBURFI23

Master fiddler Kevin Burke covers the basics of true Irish fiddle style. He teaches the melody of each tune, then gives you the nuances that make this music come alive. You'll learn to add rhythmic devices and melodic variations such as grace notes, ornaments, rolls and double-stops. By the time you've finished this course you'll have acquired a repertoire of great tunes and the technique to hold your own in an Irish music session.
