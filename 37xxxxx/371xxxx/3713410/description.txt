Six Thanos clones, hybridised with other prominent Marvel Universe figures including Doctor Strange, Professor X and Galactus acting on their template's nihilistic desire to end the universe, while they were opposed by a group including Adam Warlock, Spider-Man, Doctor Strange and Thanos himself.

A sequel was produced that continued the focus on Thanos, in Marvel Universe: The End.
