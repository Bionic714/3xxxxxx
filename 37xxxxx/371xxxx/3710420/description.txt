
Showit? Effects is a program that will greatly increase the impact your slideshows have and greatly decrease the amount of time you spend making them!

Showit? Effects handles the opening of the image, applying the effect(s), flattening and saving the image, and then opening of the next image - all within the context of Adobe Photoshop CS2.

Effects include: IR Portrait, Dress Magic, Punched, Vintage, LB, X-Process, Black & White, White Vignette, Black Vignette, LOMOit, and Glow. Additional effects can be added including some free effects at getit.davidjay.com
