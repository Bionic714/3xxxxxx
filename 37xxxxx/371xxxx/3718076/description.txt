VSX Ultra is a next-gen 3d OpenGL-based music visualizer for Windows, displaying a wide range of cool effects to music.
It's a standalone application listening on the soundcard's recording channel meaning it works with any
media player. It differentiates from the usually boring visuals shipping with today's media players.

Available at
  http://vsxu.com
	
Screenshots:
  http://vsxu.com/screenshots.e

This release includes the following visuals (and more):
  - a school of herring swimming to the music
	- a cool plasma world
	- soap bubbles
	- particle explosions to the music
