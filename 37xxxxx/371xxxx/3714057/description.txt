Soundtrack to the 1981 MGM movie musical "Pennies from Heaven," starring Steve Martin and Bernadette Peters.  Originally based on a BBC serial, featuring many songs from the 1930's - the majority of music on this soundtrack is from recordings made at that time.

This is a transfer from an original LP and is not currently availabe on CD, though some of the songs can be found for download on the web.  Additional music to augment some of these tracks was written by Marvin Hamlisch.
