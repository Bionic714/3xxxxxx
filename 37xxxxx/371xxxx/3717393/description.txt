Yu-Gi-Oh: The Abridged Series is a well-known YouTube (and DailyMotion) series by LittleKuriboh parodizing the infamous Yu-Gi-Oh anime series, exposing its plotholes and character flaws, and even inventing its own. LittleKuriboh is now known for his voice talents, and the ability to portray, and make caricatures of, a broad range of characters. This video encompasses the first 16 5-minute episodes of Yu-Gi-Oh: Abridged. More can be found at the following websites.
