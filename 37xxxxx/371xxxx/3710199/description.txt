SoKo - Not Sokute and Live Compilation

This is an mp3 + ogg compilation of SoKo's Not Sokute EP and her live performance on De Sorte Spejdere of DR P3 (Danish national radio).

Tracks:
01      SoKo    The Dandy Cowboys
02      SoKo    Shitty Day
03      SoKo    I'll Kill Her
04      SoKo    Take My Heart
05      SoKo    It's Raining Outside
06      SoKo    People Always Look Better In The Sun
07      SoKo    My Ex-Boyfriends
08      SoKo    Enemy
09      SoKo    Everybody's Seeking For Happiness
10      SoKo    Wet Dreams
11      SoKo    Souvenirs
12      SoKo    I'll Kill Her (DR P3 Live Version)

The first 5 tracks are taken from the "Soko - Not Sokute EP (2007)" torrent on TPB ( http://thepiratebay.org/tor/3683849/Soko_-_Not_Sokute_EP_(2007) ) and are kept in their original mp3 format.
The 7 remaining live tracks are cut out from from P3's De Sorte Spejdere's podcast ( http://podcast.dr.dk/p3/dss/2007/DSS080607.mp3 ), and has been amplified. These are compressed using ogg/vorbis quality 9,99 which is probably a waste due to the quality of the source, so it is not my fault the sound quality isn't any better, blame the recording and compression of the podcast.
