BBC4 - North Korea - Crossing The Line

The story of US soldier James Joseph Dresnok, who deserted his unit in 1962
while guarding the peace in South Korea. After walking the most heavily
fortified area on earth, he defected to the Cold War enemy, finding fame as
a film actor and being hailed as a coveted star of the North Korean
propaganda machine. Forty-five years later, this film reveals the lives of
Comrade Joe and other American defectors.
