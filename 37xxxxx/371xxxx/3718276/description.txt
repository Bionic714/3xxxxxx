The Fall Out Boy / Project Rocket Split EP is an EP released by Uprising Records in 2002 (see 2002 in music). It was recorded jointly by Fall Out Boy and Project Rocket. It was the first Fall Out Boy and Project Rocket first release.

Tracks 1 through 3 are recorded by Project Rocket, while tracks 4 through 6 are recorded by Fall Out Boy.
"Formula For Love" ? 2:31
"You Charlatan" ? 3:14
"Someday" ? 2:31
"Growing Up" ? 2:53
"Switchblades and Infidelity" ? 3:13
"Moving Pictures" ? 3:33
The tracks were reversed in the re-release of the album. Fall Out Boy's tracks came first, followed by those of Project Rocket.
