:. About .:

Activision?s CATERPILLAR CONSTRUCTION TYCOON for the PC puts players in charge 
of a construction site and a handful of different Caterpillar construction vehicles, including
the track-type tractor, excavator, and the Lexion combine. 

The game has over 10 single-player missions which require players to 
command their Caterpillar
machines to dig trenches, destroy buildings, build wind blocks or dams to stop natural disasters, 
or perform rescue missions.
Once players have mastered these missions, they can up the difficulty level and play them again or
plan their own constructions in free mode.
Players can also customize the inside of their Caterpillar machines or create personalized certificates.
Gamers of all ages who are looking for a challenge will enjoy CATERPILLAR CONSTRUCTION TYCOON. 

The different construction vehicles, missions, and free mode keep the game interesting and fresh.
