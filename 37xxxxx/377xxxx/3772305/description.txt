BSW Presents - The Disco Collection Parts 1-9 (MP3 Edition)






The Disco Collection is a collection of classic and influential disco tracks that were recorded from the mid 1970's to the mid 1980's
featuring tracks from artists Chic, Sister Sledge, Earth Wind and Fire, Imagination, The Bee Gees, Boney M, Kool and the Gang,
KC and the Sunshine Band, Donna Summer, Heatwave, The Gap Band, and many, many more ..........................






Compilation Track Information



After finishing my "Disco Collection" compilation i wanted to do a "I-Pod / MP3" friendly version of this collection of great disco music for
those who are not "lossless" aware yet !!!! (And also because i need some great tunes to listen to on my holiday / vacation i'm taking very soon !!!!)


Here follows some information about how i coverted this upload to MP3.





Compilation Audio Conversion Information



All audio files converted from FLAC codec to MP3 codec using Easy CD-DA Extractor 10.
