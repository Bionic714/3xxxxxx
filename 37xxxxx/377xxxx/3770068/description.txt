Originally on: ABC

Mini-Series Summary:

Dr. Joyce Reardon assembles a team of paranormal talents to investigate an allegedly haunted mansion known as Rose Red in this mini-series written by Stephen King. Can the team survive the horror that they uncover within the walls of this secretive structure?

Part 2:
While investigating the mansion, the team starts seeing visions and at night they have disturbing dreams.
