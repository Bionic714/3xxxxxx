Hot shot skydiving instructor "Ditch Brodie" gets hurled into the world of international espionage and intrigue by a sexy, mysterious student - who supposedly plunges to her death during a parachute jump. Suddenly Ditch finds himself racing along a deadly collision course with the Russian mafia in a chilling game of cat and mouse. With pulse-pounding suspense and sensational skydiving, highspeed adventure doesn't move any faster than TERMINAL VELOCITY.

Starring:

Charlie Sheen, Nastassja Kinski and James Gandolfini.
