MusicID app, put your phone upto a loudspeaker, wait 30 seconds and find out which song it is your listening to for Free! (Assuming you do not pay for data - Uses around 10-30kb)

More info will eventually be available at http://www.cingularmusicid.com/

Installation Instructions:
Copy MusicID.cab over to your PDA and install. Then run the MusicID Icon in your Programs menu.
