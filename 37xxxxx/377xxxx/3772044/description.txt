- Release Notes ------------------------------------------------------------- -
   
  An Ocean Between Us is the title of the forthcoming studio album by As I
  Lay Dying, and is their follow-up to Shadows Are Security. Initially, it 
  was thought that the name of the album was to be titled Evolution. The band
  finished recording the album on May 5, and is currently set for an August 
  21, 2007 release. The band worked on the album with producer and current 
  guitarist for Killswitch Engage, Adam Dutkiewicz. The album has since then
  been mixed by Colin Richardson (Napalm Death, Bullet for My Valentine).
