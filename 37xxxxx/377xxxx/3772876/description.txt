Bex Bissell, a respectable estate agent who loves his wife, loves his son and loves his violence. Bex is the leader of a gang of football supporters known as the Inner-City Crew - his firm. He plans to invite rival firms and lead them all to the European Championships in Germany, a chance to take on the locals. But thats a position he's going to have to fight for.

From the director of Scum.

Starring:

Gary Oldman, Lesley Manville, Philip Davis and Andrew Wilde.

Enjoy!
