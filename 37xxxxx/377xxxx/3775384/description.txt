Aero Files Texture Booster Pro is a high quality texture enhancement for Microsoft Flight Simulator X (only) which will improve your Flight-Sim experience.

Texture Booster Pro features over 250+ new textures for your flight simulator.

This texture product is recommended to use with other texture products made by Aero Files to give your flight simulator a boost of a lifetime.

Why no runway and taxi way textures?

There would be no reason to include runway and taxiway textures in this product, as it is allready available for purchase by Aero Files for FSX and FS9 users.

All textures feature photoreal quality settings. All Seasons available.

All textures are reduced to improve performance without reducing the quality.
