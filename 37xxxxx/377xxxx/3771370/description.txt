A new version of JobTabs 2006. This release comes pre-cracked, so no keygenning, or cracking required (which is always nice!). For anyone looking for a new job, this tool could be just what you are looking for.

Our purpose is to empower the individual job seeker in the face of the sea change taking place in the employment space. Simply put, the individual job seeker is facing a hail storm of trials in the pursuit of new opportunities. These include the traditional challenges of finding a job, new challenges as a result of globalization, and a considerable paradigm shift brought about by the Information Age.

Program Features:

* Qualify yourself as an ideal candidate for every job you apply to.  
   
* JobTabs makes tailoring your experience to the job requistion a breeze!

* Save time and energy by applying to only those contracts/jobs where you will be in the uppermost percentile.

* Convert your unsuccessful employment applications from liabilities to assets. Pursue new opportunities with even less effort.

* Build on your resumes which are the closest match to where you want to be. Change only what you have to.
