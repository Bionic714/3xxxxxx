Willy Fog: Journey to the Center of the Earth, Episodes 1-9 

In the same vein as Animalympics, Willy Fog is a lion that stars in animated adaptations of Jules Verne's more famous works. Willy Fog is accompanied by a host of other animated animals in a series based on "Journey to the Center of the Earth." 

The first 9 of 26 episodes are released here. 

PAL video, AC3 audio, XVID, English spoken, no subtitles.
