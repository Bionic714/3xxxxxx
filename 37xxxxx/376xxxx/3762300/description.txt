It's the 1950's and ambitious Hollywood screenwriter, Peter Appleton, has eveything going for him. That is until the FBI discover he attended a communist meeting during college and blacklist him.
Drunk and desperate, Peter drives aimlessly through the streets of California, finally crashing into a stormy river and waking to find himself in a small coastal town, minus his memory.

Starring:
Jim Carey, Bob Balaban, Brent Briscoe.
