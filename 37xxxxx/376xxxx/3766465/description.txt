A gentle lamb seeks his true identity in this enthralling and beautifully animated fantasy for all ages. The innocent world of Chirin, a fluffy lamb who wears a golden bell to signal his presence, is shattered when his flock is attacked by the Wolf King. When his mother is killed defending him, he is left alone. Determined to avenge her death, Chirin sets out in pursuit of the Great Wolf. In his quest, the lamb decides that Kings strength and ferocity may be preferable to his own gentleness. Chirin convinces the Wolf King to teach him the ways of a hunter and develops a close attachment to him. After growing into a fierce fighting ram, Chirin faces a terrible choice - attack his former herd or defend it against his mentor.
