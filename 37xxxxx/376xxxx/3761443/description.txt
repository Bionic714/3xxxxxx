Jonny dreams of leaving his dead end job as a courier.Through his best friend, nephew of the notorious crime lord Ray Kreed, he wins his way into the toughest gang in North London.
Hungry for some real gangster action, Jonny sparks a deadly feud between Ray's gang and another South London firm.

Starring:
Sadie Frost, Ray Winstone, Jonny Lee Miller, Jude Law, Sean Pertwee, Kathy Burke, Denise Van Outen, and Rhys Ifans.

A really funny black comedy.

Enjoy!
