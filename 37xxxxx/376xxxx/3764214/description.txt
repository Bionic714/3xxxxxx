           Series examining the way Earth may look in the distant future,
           and the weird and wonderful creatures that may populate 
           various areas of the planet. 200 million years in the future
           and there is one huge super-continent, Panagea II. Eminent
           scientists with the help of state-of-the-art animation bring 
           to life creatures such as Gloomworms, Slickribbons and Garden
           Worms.
