Originally on: ABC
First Aired: Jan 27, 2002
Show Category: Movie/Mini-Series

Mini-Series Summary:

Dr. Joyce Reardon assembles a team of paranormal talents to investigate an allegedly haunted mansion known as Rose Red in this mini-series written by Stephen King. Can the team survive the horror that they uncover within the walls of this secretive structure?

Part 1:
Professor Reardon assembles a team of students to investigate the paranormal activities at a large mansion in Seattle. When they arrive at the house, strange things start to happen.
