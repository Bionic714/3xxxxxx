Enhance you digital photos and give them a whole new look with this innovative photo enhancement software! 
Among Photo Frame Show's major features are:

 You can save your framed photos in popular formats for personal use or for your projects. 

 You can easily print your framed photos through a "virtual" printer that appears on screen. 

 You can e-mail your framed photos to your friends directly from within the program.

 You can blend your favorite framed photos with your desktop just by dragging and dropping them at your desired location on screen.
