This genre classic predicted today's epidemic of violence in schools......
Class of 1984 follows mild-mannered music teacher Andy Morris who makes the mistake of standing up to thr thugs at notorious 'Lincoln High', a school overrun with delinquents.
Tough kid, Peter will stop at nothing to protect his turf, run drugs, pimp girls and keep the school living in fear. As the battle between teachers and students intensifies someone is going to be taught a lession they will never forget.

Starring:
Perry King, Merrie Lynn Ross, Timothy Van Patten. Also including a very young Michael J Fox.

Enjoy!
