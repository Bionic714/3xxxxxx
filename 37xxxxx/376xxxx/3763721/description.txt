Get out your chalk, grab your favorite pool stick, and aim carefully. 3D Live Pool is an excellent pool simulator that puts you right in the game.

If you want a fun, easy to use, and varied computer pool game, you must give this one a shot. 3D Live Pool has great sound, the balls roll just like real ones, and you can position your cue shot to hit at various places on the cue ball for different spins! Play against the computer or another player. If you aren't familiar with computer pool games, be sure to read the help file to learn how to work the controls.
