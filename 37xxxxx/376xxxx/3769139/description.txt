The Collective is a group of mountain bikers, filmmakers and photographers that shoot films, ride bikes and take photographs. In 2004 The Collective released it own self tittled film, 'The Collective". The film won many high-status awards and soon become known to mountain bikers world over. Since then they have released a second filmed tittled Roam.

The Collectives goal is to create outstanding, world class films in exotic locations world wide. It has many high profile riders participating in its films adding to its prestige. The Collective is based in Whistler and Vancouver, British Columbia, and has now set the standard for mountain bike movies, and is supported by many highly esteemed companies. The Collective was filmed entirely on high quality 16mm film ensuring its extreme high quality.
