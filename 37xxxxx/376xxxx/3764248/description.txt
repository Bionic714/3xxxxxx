Dr. Goldfoot (Vincent Price) plans on taking over the world with his beautiful female robots, who seduce rich and powerful men. Robot #11/Diane (Susan Hart) is sent after millionaire Todd Armstrong (Dwayne Hickman). Secret agent Craig Gamble (Frankie Avalon) tries to stop the plot but ends up in the torture chamber with Armstrong. The parody of "The Pit and the Pendulium" is the highlight of the film which also includes bits by Annette Funicello, Harvey Lembeck and Deborah Walley. Title song performed by The Supremes.
