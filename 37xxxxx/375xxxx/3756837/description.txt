Star Wars - The Emperors New Clones...

... is an action parody, full length film, produced by Backyard Productions (www.backyardproductions.co.uk) in the UK. This is the full film, and extras (trailers, mockumentary etc). Originally premiered in September 2006, and since receiving critial acclaim at TheForce.Net Star Wars Fan site (http://www.theforce.net/fanfilms/).
