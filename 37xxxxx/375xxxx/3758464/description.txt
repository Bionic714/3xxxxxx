The film music of Ingmar Bergman performed by Kabi Laretei.

Fantastic recording of music performed by Bergman's 
ex-wife Kabi Laretei, recorded in 1978.

Cries and Whispers, Autumn Sonata & Face to Face

02 - Chopin - Fantasia Impromptu

04 - Chopin - Mazurka A minor
05 - Handel - Chaconne G major
06 - Scarlatti - Sonata D major
