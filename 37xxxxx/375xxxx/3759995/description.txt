What does the MikeAndPetra toolbar do? Gives you instant access to MikeAndPetra.com from any site on the web. Let's you search from anywhere on the web. Plays online radio from the toolbar. Notifies you when you receive new emails in your POP3, Yahoo!, Gmail and/or Hotmail accounts. Provides a weather forecast directly in the toolbar and more. Customize the toolbar by selecting which personal, predefined and extra components to activate and display. Add additional gadgets such as translator, calculators, to do list, music and video feeds, TV, games, and more! You can shrink the toolbar.
