Sweethurts is a duo doing Alternative/Singer-songwriter music with a touch of country and rock.
The founders of the band is the lovecouple Jimmy Kristo and Hanna Jacobsson from a smalltown called Fagersta in Sweden.
Thay started in 2006 making coversongs but went over to do their own music, they unofficially released the 9 track album "Talking To God" in 2006 and are for now trying to get noticed on a higher level.
For now they are recording their songs at home in their little studio and writing songs all the time.
They can be found here on last.fm and all their songs are for now free for download and so on..

The Album contains three covers.
