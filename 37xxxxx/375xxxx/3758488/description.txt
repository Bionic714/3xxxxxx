Power Modell, based on insanity, fuelled with a sick sense of humour and armed with a shotgun of keyboards.

With a arsenal of unique sounds, song structures and harsh blend of English & Russian vocals, Power Modell is one of the rare few unique sounds moving about in the world of music these days.

Primarily Power Modell is electronic, but continues to move through the styles that have influenced it. The next record, claiming to be an eccentric mix of 50?s rock and roll, punk and the insanity of industrial-pop that has established the Power Modell sound.
