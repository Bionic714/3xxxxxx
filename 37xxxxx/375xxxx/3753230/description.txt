Tilix e bulgarska Linux bazirana operacionna sistema, ednakvo lesna za upotreba ot nachinaeshti i ot naprednali. Moge da se izpolzva bez instalacia direktno ot diska, razpoznava hardware-a vi avtomatichno i raboti pochti izcialo na bulgarsk ezik.

Molq, ne zabravqite, che RC1 e testova verisa i moje da ima problemi ot vsqkakvo estestvo.

Tilix is a Bulgarian Linux based operating system, easy to use for beginners and for advanced user. The distribution can work directly from CD or can be installed to you hard drive. The hardware recognition is automatic.
