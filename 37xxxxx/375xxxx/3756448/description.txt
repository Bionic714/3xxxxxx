Dr. Dre and Ed Lover star in this self-billed "hip-hop whodunit" as two inept newbie cops who somehow must solve one of the biggest cases they've ever come across in Harlem: the killing of the owner of the barbershop where they used to work.  

I personally didn't care for the movie that much.  But the parts Denis Leary was in were pee-in-your-pants hillarious.  For me, it was worth that.
