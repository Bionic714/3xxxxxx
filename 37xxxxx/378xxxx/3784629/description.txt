Sixty videos, most in black and white, of Jacques Brel singing his own songs.  If you haven't seen Brel present it his work French, you're missing the real thing. This is it.

Some videos have subtitles (English, Dutch, Spanish); this is usually noted in the filename.  All are in mp4 format (iPod/iTunes).  If anyone is interested, I also have them in mpg format (Windoze Media Player, VCD, etc.), but they're a lot bigger.

Enjoy!
