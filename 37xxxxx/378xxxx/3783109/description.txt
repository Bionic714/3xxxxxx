KVCD allows you to fit over 2hrs of video on one single cd-r. Just use \"Nero\" to burn this video.

Beginners 101 for NERO (BurningSoftware)
1. Choose \"create video-cd\" in the StartMenu. 
2. Choose the mpeg-file (a KVCD mpeg-file).
3. Wait a minute or so for Nero to finish mounting the VideoFile.
4. Insert an empty cd-r.
5. Choose a good BurningSpeed(4x or slower.  NOT faster than 4x).
6.  ..and burn it!!
