Kraftwerk - Aerodynamik & La Forme (Remixed By Hot Chip)
--------------------------------------------------------

Hot Chip, UK dancepunk/electropop group, and Kraftwerk, have joined forces
for a special collaboration: a remix single.

The single will consist of remixes of two tracks from Kraftwerk\'s 2003 album,
Tour de France Soundtracks: \"Aerodynamik\" and \"La Forme\".


01 - Kraftwerk - Aerodynamic (Intelligent Design Mix) (8:31)
02 - Kraftwerk - La Forme (King of the Mountains Mix) (11:32)

more information : http://www.kraftwerk.com/info/de/Remix/body_remix.html

This one is taken from the Promo cdsingle. The official release will be end september in Europe.
