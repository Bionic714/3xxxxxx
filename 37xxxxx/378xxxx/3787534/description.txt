The Buck Dharma Archive Series as a 4-disc set. The CDs contain original song demos for legendary songs like "(Don't Fear) The Reaper" and "Burnin' for You" as well as unreleased recordings like "Devil's Nail" and "Pack of Lies". The discs also show you the lighter side of Buck, with various audio oddities, jams and humorous songs, all featuring the songwriting finesse and guitar virtuosity of Buck Dharma. All tracks were digitally remastered by Buck himself in his home studio.
