26 December 1987 pm in Gautam the Buddha Auditorium

Question 1
BELOVED MASTER,
IS ENLIGHTENMENT BEYOND THE NATURE OF THINGS?

Milarepa, enlightenment is the very nature of things. But it has never been said that way; on the contrary, people\'s minds have been corrupted by creating a goal against nature, giving it beautiful names, \"supernature.\" And man was caught in this because of a very simple reason:
The nature of things is already where you are.....
