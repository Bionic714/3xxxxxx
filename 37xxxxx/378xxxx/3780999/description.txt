Sierras 3D Ultra Cool Pool is still the best online pool game around. In August -07 Sierra decided to take down its servers for this great game. But new rooms are up now to ensure this crazy fun game will live on forever.

Check www.pauth.com to find out more about the new rooms. Fixes for online play are available there for both coolpool and maximum pool (maxpool, max pool, maximumpool). 


How to Install: Just download, unzip, run the self extractor - Ultra Cool Pool.exe - For your game to find the new rooms, open the - Online fix folder- and click the registry fix - aug.16.fix.for.cool.pool.reg - and select merge.


Good Luck all and please seed to keep this great pool game alive
