Why ogg ?
Ogg Vorbis has been designed to completely replace all proprietary, patented
audio formats. That means that you can encode all your music or audio content in Vorbis and never look back.

It can also be burned to a CD just like mp3 files.
Ogg files uses approximately 38% less space compared to mp3 files while keeping
the same sound quality level as good as a CD
