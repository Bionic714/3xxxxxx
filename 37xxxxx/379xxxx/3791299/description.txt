Rumba De Mc\'s is an original Latin Hip Hop mixtape made by a French deejay called Dj Cheedo. It contains 30 badass tracks, switching from old school to new school Latin Hip Hop. 
The last track has got the same name than the mixtape : Rumba de Mc\'s. This song is the result of a connexion between Koolkal (a French Producer & Hip Hop Beatmaker from Rennes - France), Simba (French rapper and Hip Hop activist since 1989), trompetist Barbaro Teuntor Garcia (former Buena Vista Social Club\'s trompetist, Sierra Maestra, Afro-Cuban All Stars, Rumbayazz...), Ivan Tamayo (Sergent Garcia\'s percussionist), Pierre Dandin (Sergent Garcia\'s trombonist) and two members of the Cuban band Melao. 

Recorded & mixed at KoolkwalitySoundz Studio ! France
