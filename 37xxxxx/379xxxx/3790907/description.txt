Overview:	Produced and directed by Walt Missingham, who became the first non-Chinese to practice Kung Fu at the Shaolin Temple, this authoritative and informative programme uses rarely seen archive footage to trace both the history of martial arts and the phenomenal impact Bruce Lee had on this culture.

This program includes:

    * Narration by Bruce Lee's daughter, Shannon Lee Keasler
    * Footage of Jackie Chan and Sammo Hung ("Martial Law")
    * Martial artists Han Jin Song, Ronald Takaki, and Jim Holliday
    * Outtakes from Bruce Lee's film "Game Of Death"
    * Bruce Lee training James Coburn, watched by son Brandon Lee
    * Footage of Bruce Lee's legendary "one inch punch"
    * Interviews with Andre Morgan, Gil Hubbs, John Saxon, Peter Archer, Taky Kimura, Dan Inosanto, and ex-James Bond, George Lazenby
