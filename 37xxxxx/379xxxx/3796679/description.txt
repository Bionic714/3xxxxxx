Släng Dig i Brunnen

Swedish Stand-Up Comedy from late 80's to early 90's.
Originally recorded from Swedish TV Broadcast (SVT) on a stereo VHS, then transfered to audio cassette and then finally recorded to a computer.
I know, it sounds scary, but the quality is nonetheless really good.
