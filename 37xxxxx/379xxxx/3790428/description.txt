Spanish Airports 2: The next sequel of the succesful Spanish Airports series. Sim-wings presents 4 highly detailes replicas of Girona, Granada, Reus and Seville. 

Take a trip to Girona, destination of many Costa Brava holiday makers. Fly to Seville to discover the unique moorish terminal building. Take off from Granada and overfly the famous Alhambra, or land in Reus, the popular destination of the "low cost airlines" near Barcelona.

All airports have been rebuilt up to the smallest of details layed out on photorealistic textures including their surroundings. Animated jetways and many dynamic objects create this special airport feeling.
