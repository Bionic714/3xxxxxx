Join the Ghost Trackers as they investigate the supernatural in Tombstone,
Arizona.  Using a scientific approach to investigating, the Ghost Trackers
use infrared video cameras, digital and 35 mm cameras, electro-magnetic
field monitors, sound equipment, heat sensors and Geiger counters to
track the supernatural.  
Visit some of Tombstones most haunted building like the Bird Cage Theatre, the Buford Bed and Breakfast, the Boothill Cemetery and other locations. See for yourself the strange orbs in motion and glowing orbs are caught on film and video. Decide for yourself after watching our investigation if Tombstone really is haunted. We believe, do you?
