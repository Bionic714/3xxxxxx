Jackie Chan and Jennifer Love Hewitt are tailor-made high-tech action partners for the Tuxedo, a roller coaster ride full of laughs, kinetic kicks and crazy stunts.
Jimmy Tong is an ordinary cabbie-turned-chauffeur who slips into a 2 billion dollar, super-spy suit and inadvertently becomes a dashing secret agent. Fit for trouble, this deluxe tux unwittingly thrusts Tong and his partner into a dangerous world of international espionage.

Starring:

Jackie Chan, Jennifer Love Hewitt, Jason Issacs and Debi Mazar.
