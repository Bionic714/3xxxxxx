.:: Wildfire (c) Take2 Interactive ::.


:. About .:

Wildfire is a forest fire fighting strategy game that relies on the player
to figure out the best plan of action when money and resources are limited
and there's a fire to be stopped. 

There are two types of gameplay which includes a campaign base mode and 
a random fire mode which adds hours of forest fire fighting. 
Buildable units include:

hotshots, smokejumpers, ditch-digging bulldozers, water-dropping chinooks, 
transport helicopters and many more.
