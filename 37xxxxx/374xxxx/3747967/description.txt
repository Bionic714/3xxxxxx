:. About .:

Construction Destruction lets you live the realistic fun of building and tearing down! 
Take on projects, completing them on-time and within budget to become a construction
tycoon. However, you must keep your machines in control and your temper in check or 
you'll pay the price for a botched job. Part of the fun is the control the game gives you.
 
Bid on jobs, pushing your rigs to the limit to complete projects on time and on budget 
to make a profit.

The construction machines you operate have a very realistic feel!

Maneuver the wrecking ball to demolish a building or use the hook to transport heavy 
objects. Control this long-necked beast or mistakes will cost you the job.

Raise and lower your bucket to move debris to new sites and dump trucks. 
Perfect your aim? spilled or wasted material costs you time and money.

Try to move the impossible with your monster machine.

Bank a river, prepare a landscaping site or lose the job by flattening a house.

Operate 7 huge vehicles in 9 different construction environments!
3 different game modes will keep the fun rolling along.

Build your business in Career Mode, completing your jobs to grow your company.

Show ?em you?ve got what it takes in Free Play Mode. Follow job orders or wreak 
havoc on the work site.

Learn to control your vehicle in the Training Courses, complete with voice-over 
commands and on-screen tips.
