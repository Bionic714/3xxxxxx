Features:

A summer-season, autogenned photoreal scenery covering much of the Marlborough area; 

Photoreal, accurate models of Omaka, Blenheim Airport and Picton Airfield; 

A reasonably accurate model of the Picton waterfront area, including the ferry terminal; 

The Picton-based sight-seeing seaplane operation.

------------------

FSX version notes:

Service Pack 1, the recent update to FSX, is required.

The photo aerial textures are twice the resolution of FS2004, at 2.5 metres per pixel
