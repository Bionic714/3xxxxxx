Title: JNCIE: Juniper Networks Certified Internet Expert Study Guide

Date Published: 2003

Synopsys:

Juniper Networks develops high-speed, scalable routers for the telecom, service provider, and networking industry. They are challenging Cisco in the internetworking market with state of the art technology and innovation. Juniper Networks offers a four-tiered certification program. The JNCIE (Juniper Networks Certified Internet Expert) is an advanced, lab-based certification in the M-series & T-series certification track that tests network administrators in their knowledge of working with Juniper products and the JUNOS software in a multiprotocol network environment. It is the fourth and highest level in Juniper Networks' certification.
