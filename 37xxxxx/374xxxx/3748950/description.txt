Title: Juniper and Cisco Routing: Policy and Protocols for Multivendor IP Networks

Date Published: 2002

Synopsys:

This work discusses how network traffic flow is complicated by the fact that each routing vendor has its own proprietary implementation or extension to the routing protocols. It covers both Juniper and Cisco routing, and touches on other vendor implementations. It focuses on routing policy, covering Border Gateway Protocol in depth and includes real-world multi vendor configuration examples.
