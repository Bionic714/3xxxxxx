          Director Simon Chambers follows the lives of young Bengali
          sisters - and close personal friends of his - as they travel 
          from London to Bangladesh to undertake the arranged marriages 
          that have long been planned for them. Although apparently
          reluctant to submit to the agreed arrangements, the sisters 
          nonetheless seem unable or unwilling to ultimately escape their 
          traditional destiny.
