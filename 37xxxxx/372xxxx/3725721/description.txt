:: Introduction ::

In Secret of Time you play as Emily, a young girl who\'s grand father recently died, leaving you as the new Time Warden. You are responsible for protecting the time against bad guys - which can be hard as troublemakers Keegan and his fat assistant Hodges are up to some cunning plans.


:: Features ::

The game features a huge level and several interesting game elements such as

    * Cinematics
    * High-end graphics with dynamic lighting
    * Great soundtracks
    * Melee attack combos
    * Time freeze attack
    * A bunch of enemies
    * Platform puzzles
    * Boss with 2 phases
