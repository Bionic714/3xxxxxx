Rollcage Stage II (PC game) complete soundtrack

The game was released for PC and Sony PlayStation in 2000. The two releases have completely different soundtracks. The PC release features music by Gary McKill while PlayStation version has completely different soundtrack composed by E-Z Rollers, Technical Itch, Omni Trio, Aquasky, Flytronix and Dom & Roland.

This is a PC version soundtrack ripped from the original game CD. Additionally, there is a track from the intro movie - "E-Z Rollers - Breakbeat Generation".
