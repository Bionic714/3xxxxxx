AnimXoniX is a erotic new remake of the all-time classic Xonix/QIX game. In this
one you get to drive a little train around the game field. Your goal is to save
adult anime heroes who were trapped behind a magic wall by a mean Warlock.
His sly servants will try to get in your way and stop you. Be careful and avoid
hitting them! We prepared a lot of different bonuses for you that will help you
succeed.
