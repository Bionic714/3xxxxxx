When a Korean freedom fighter and his pupil, Carter Wong (Big Trouble In Little China; The Magnificent), go on the run from the Japanese, they get help from the legendary martial arts superstar Angela Mao (Enter the Dragon; Dance Of Death). Mao has a score to settle with the Japanese aggressors. Together, they use styles such as Hapkido and Tae Kwan Do against their foes. Featuring Sammo Hung as one of the meanest Japanese villains in the history of cinema, Sting Of The Dragon Master has an all-star cast and more fight scenes than you can shake a fist at!
