:. About .:

Based on the Antikiller motion picture, the Antikiller game continues a 
brutal war between obnoxious crime gangs and a one man vigilante, a 
former police officer known as the Fox. Your choice is to join the 
brutal gangs as one of their leaders and fight your way to domination 
through rivers of blood, have your revenge as the Fox, or enter madman 
as Barkas, the notoriously dangerous no one's man.
Enjoy this russian game. Russian speech and eng subs.
