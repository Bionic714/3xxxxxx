One of the kings of the Blue Collar Comedy Tour, Bill Engvall, is back with a set of killer material, including "Argue Naked," "Slim Jims," "Wives and NASCAR," "Free Range Chicken," "Getting Older," "Bad Weiner Day" and more in this live show at the Paramount Theatre in Austin, Texas. Engvall is a top-selling stand-up comic, and his albums have outsold those of Chris Rock, Adam Sandler and Jerry Seinfeld.
