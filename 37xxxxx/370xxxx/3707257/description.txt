ORIGINAL TITLE: The Odd Couple 
YEAR: 1968   
RUNNING TIME: 105 min.   
DIRECTOR: Gene Saks 
SCREENWRITER: Neil Simon 
COMPOSER: Neal Hefti 
CINEMATOGRAPHER: Robert B. Hauser 
CAST: Jack Lemmon, Walter Matthau, John Fiedler, Herbert 
Edelman, Monica Evans, Carole Shelley 
STUDIO/PRODUCER: Paramount Pictures 
GENRE: Comedy 
SYNOPSIS/PLOT: Two friends try sharing an apartment, 
but their ideas of housekeeping and lifestyles are as 
different as night and day. Felix Ungar has just broken 
up with his wife. Despondant, he goes to kill himself 
but is saved by his friend Oscar Madison. With nowhere 
ele to go, Felix is urged by Oscar to move in with him, 
at least for a while. The only problem is that Felix is 
neat, tidy, and neurotic, whereas Oscar is slovenly 
and casual.
