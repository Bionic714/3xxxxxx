Release Notes:

Masif embark on a new CD series showcasing the
biggest and best anthems in the Hard Trance scene,
mixed By International DJ team Steve Hill and Alex
Kidd!
Alex Kidd is one of the biggest rising stars in the
Hard scene. Ranked #168 in the world (only 10 Hard
DJ's are in the Top 200!!!) Nominated for Best
Track, Best Resident and Best DJ at the recent UK
Hard Dance Awards. Six successful Australian tours
under his belt in less than two years. Played the
biggest events around Europe including Dance
Valley,Gatecrasher,Tidy Weekender, Goodgreef and
Frantic in the UK. He is a formidable talent and has
a strong fanbase!
Steve Hill is the local hero of Hard Trance in
Australia & he produces his best mix since the ever
popular Black Magic + Transmission CD series. Steve
has also mixed six compilations in Australia in the
past four years for Central Station and Masif.
Featues a who's who of the Hard Trance scene & spans
more than 40 tracks over 2CD's. Includes tracks from
DJ Scot Project, Dark By Design, Yakooza, Vandall,
Walt, Luca Antolini, Cally + Juice, Hennes + Cold,
Technikal, Andy Whitby, Martin Roth, Kamui, ASYS.

Enjoy!
