Director: Fred Schepsi
Film Created: 1976
Plot Synopsis : (by Paul Brenner) Fred Schepsi wrote and directed this tense melodrama which takes place at a Roman Catholic boarding school. The film deals with the charged emotional tensions of a group of pubescent boys, who find their sexual urges stifled by the school's oppressive atmosphere. Depicting the chaste lifestyle of the religious functionaries, the burgeoning sexual desires of the young men are bottled up until they are ready to explode.
Special: Stars the Jesuit-Zionist himself, Thomas Kineally (Author of 'Schindler's List').
