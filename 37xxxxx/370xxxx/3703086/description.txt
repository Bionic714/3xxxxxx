R e l e a s  e  n  o t e s
In the year in which Rotterdam Records celebrates her 15th birthday the 100th
vinyl release and the 50th CD release, this mother of all the hardcore labels
reaches another milestone.. The 10th edition of the great compilation series!
" Hardcore To The Bone! " A serie which can count on a big and loyal group of
followers, increasing with every edition of Hardcore To The Bone. Needless to
say this edition has again been compiled and mixed by the almighty Neophyte
and DJ Panic, always a guarantee for the most upfront track list with many
exclusive hardcore productions, all in two seamless mixes divided on 2 CD's.
