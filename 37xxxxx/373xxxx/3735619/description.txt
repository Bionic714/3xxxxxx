The Fabulous World of Jules Verne, 1958, 83 minutes, B&W,
directed by Karel Zeman, Czechoslovakia, dubbed in English only, no subtitles.

Based on A Deadly Invention, by Jules Verne.

Count Artigas, an evil millionaire, wants to conquer the world from inside a giant volcano by using the research of a brilliant but socially naive scientist, Professor Roche.

Filmed long before the onset of computer generated imagery, this film is a product of very creative imagination! I wish all modern filmmakers would watch it and see how much can be accomplished without computers!
