Big Trouble (2002)

Tim Allen, Rene Russo, Dave Barry, Dennis Farnia, Omar Epps, Jason Lee, 
Johnny Knoxville


Adapted from the Pulitzer Prize-winning humourist Dave Barry\'s best selling novel, \"Big Trouble\" tells the story of a mysterious suitcase, and how it brings together a divorced dad, an unhappy housewife, two hitmen, a pair of street thugs, two lovestuck teens, two FBI men, and a psychedelic toad.
