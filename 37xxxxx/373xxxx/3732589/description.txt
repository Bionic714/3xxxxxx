Ue wo muite arukou~Kyu Sakamoto Monogatari | Japan 2005 | Drama/Biography 
| 1 episode | No English subs yet |

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The drama follows Kyu Sakamoto's life from being born to growing up into a teenager and finally a being family father. 

The plot focuses on many well known things about his life such as the background of the popular song Sukiyaki (Ue wo muite arukou) and his tragic death in the JAL flight 123 plane crash in 1985. It also brings up some things that aren't as well known such as surviving a car accident, how he met his wife Yukiko Kasiwagi as well as his time in the Japanese band Paradise King. Please Enjoy!
