Stop Starting takes a simple, yet efficient and professional approach to controlling unwanted programs from starting up when you turn on your computer. Stop Starting compares each item to an internal database of items we have collected and update regularly. You can easily create startup programs by dragging and dropping, or using a wizard and classic modes. Full support for Windows Vista and UAC (User-Account Control).
