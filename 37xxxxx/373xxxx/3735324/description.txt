ABOUT THE READER
A sample of Clare Higgins' theatrical work must include the Royal Court Theatre production of Jenkins' Ear, Antony and Cleopatra with the RSC, and Sweet Bird of Youth at the National Theatre directed by Richard Eyre. On television she has had numerous appearances, her roles including Inspector Alleyn, Pride and Prejudice and Silent Witness.

AN INFAMOUS ARMY
