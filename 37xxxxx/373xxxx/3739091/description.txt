       Well heres Axwells latest release.. just as his I Found U
       (Remode Remix) is picking up pace axwells throw another
       song into the public sector which I estimate will be huge
       in about 2 months time.

       His latest release, its true, is no where near as fast-
       paced as I found U but instead much more aimed at a more
       chilled out feel. Showing the talent behind his name and
       proving he can go from one dance genre to the next
       without any problem.

       The beat is smooth and light in comparison with the quite
       heavy and defined beat in I Found U. The light more
       quieter beat give a much more relaxed feeling to the song
       and with some well timed vocals makes this one a
       brilliant one to play either as a warm-up for a night out
       or a wind-down.
