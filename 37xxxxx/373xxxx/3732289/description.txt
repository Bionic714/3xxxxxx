This is a DVD with Manlio Giordano, the Swedish-Italian pianist, performing Beethovens 5th piano concerto (often called the "Emperor" concerto).

The DVD features a live performance, including all encores (Rachmaninoff etudes, a Rachmaninoff prelude and an improvisation).
